Terra Luna Resort

A calm, modern Value hotel with a clean design and an easy walk to I‑Drive.

Terra Luna Resort

Terra Luna is the quieter, more grounded counterpart to Stella Nova. Where Stella feels soft and cosmic, Terra Luna has a calmer, earthier vibe. It is still modern and stylish, but with a slightly more grownup feel. If you like clean lines, simple design and a peaceful atmosphere, Terra Luna will probably be more your cup of tea.

The hotel has a really fresh, uncluttered look. Think smooth shapes, muted colours and a design that feels modern without trying too hard. It’s the kind of place where you walk into the lobby and instantly feel like everything is tidy, organised and easy to navigate. Which is exactly what you want after a long travel day.

One of the biggest reasons people choose Terra Luna is the location. It’s very close to Epic Universe, making it a brilliant base if the new park is high on your list. Being able to pop back for a break or a swim is such a luxury, especially in the Florida heat.

The pool area matches the hotel’s overall feel, it's calm, clean and modern. It’s not a big themed pool like Cabana Bay or Sapphire Falls, but it’s a lovely space to unwind. There’s a pool bar too, which is always a nice touch.

Rooms are simple, modern and well laid out. They’re not huge, but they feel fresh and uncluttered, which makes them really comfortable for anyone who want a clean, modern base without paying a premium. If you prefer a room that feels calm rather than themed, Terra Luna is a great fit.

Dining is straightforward. A good food court with plenty of quick options, plus those infamous grab and go bits for early mornings. And again, yes, there’s a Starbucks in the lobby.

Transport is easy. You’ll have regular shuttle buses to all the parks, and CityWalk, and getting to Epic Universe is especially quick. You’re not walking distance to Universal Studios or Islands of Adventure, but the buses make everything simple.

And just like the other Value hotels, Terra Luna is only a short stop from International Drive. That means again loads of extra dining options, shops etc. It’s a lovely little bonus.

Terra Luna is a great pick if you want something modern, calm and close to Epic Universe without costing the earth. It’s clean, stylish and very easy to stay in. A brilliant option for anyone who prefer a peaceful base with great value.

Pros & Considerations

Pros

  • Calm, modern, uncluttered design
  • Very close to Epic Universe
  • Lovely, peaceful pool area
  • Starbucks in the lobby
  • Quick shuttle buses
  • Early park entry included
  • Walking distance to International Drive for extra dining options
  • Great value for a new, modern hotel

Considerations

  • Not walking distance to Universal Studios or Islands of Adventure
  • Theming is subtle rather than immersive
  • Rooms are modern but on the smaller side
  • Fewer on‑site dining options than the bigger resorts
  • No water taxi or walking path
