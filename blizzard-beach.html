Disney’s Blizzard Beach

A snowy ski resort… melted into a water park.

Disney’s coolest water park

Blizzard Beach is Disney’s winter‑themed water park — a quirky, colourful mix of snowy slopes, icy details and tropical sunshine. The story goes that a freak Florida snowstorm created a ski resort… which melted into a water park. The result is fun, imaginative and full of brilliant attractions for all ages.

With family slides, thrill rides and plenty of places to relax, it’s a fantastic day away from the theme parks.

Who is Blizzard Beach best for?

  • Families with children of all ages.
  • Thrill‑seekers who love big slides.
  • Guests who enjoy fun, quirky theming.
  • Anyone looking for a refreshing break from the parks.

Top Attractions

  • Summit Plummet — one of the tallest, fastest free‑fall slides in the world.
  • Slush Gusher — high‑speed thrills with big drops.
  • Teamboat Springs — a huge family raft ride.
  • Cross Country Creek — a relaxing lazy river.
  • Toboggan Racers — competitive mat‑racing fun.
  • Runoff Rapids — twisting tube slides.
  • Tike’s Peak — a snowy‑themed kids’ play area.

Dining at Blizzard Beach

Fun, casual dining options include:

  • Lottawatta Lodge — hearty meals and family favourites.
  • Warming Hut — snacks, wraps and light bites.
  • Frostbite Freddy’s — refreshing drinks.
  • I.C. Expeditions — ice cream treats.

Top Tips for Visiting Blizzard Beach

  • Arrive early for Summit Plummet — queues build quickly.
  • Bring water shoes — surfaces can get hot.
  • Use lockers for valuables.
  • Take breaks — the slides are more tiring than they look.
  • Consider a Polar Patio rental for shade and comfort.

Why I Recommend Blizzard Beach

Blizzard Beach is perfect for families and thrill‑seekers who want a fun, imaginative water park day. The snowy theming is brilliant, the slides are fantastic and there’s plenty for little ones too. It’s a great way to cool off and enjoy a different side of Disney.
