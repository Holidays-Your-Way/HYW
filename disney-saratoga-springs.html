Disney’s Saratoga Springs Resort & Spa

A peaceful, spacious Deluxe Villa Resort beside Disney Springs.

A relaxing, beautifully landscaped Disney Deluxe Villa Resort

Disney’s Saratoga Springs Resort & Spa is inspired by the historic New York retreat famous for horse racing, lakeside leisure and Victorian charm. Spread across beautiful grounds with lakes, golf course views and peaceful walking paths, it’s one of the most relaxing resorts at Walt Disney World.

With spacious villas, multiple pools and a walkway to Disney Springs, it’s perfect for families, groups and guests who want comfort, convenience and a slower pace.

Who is Saratoga Springs best for?

  • Families who want spacious villas with kitchens.
  • Guests who love being close to Disney Springs.
  • Groups who want multi‑bedroom accommodation.
  • Anyone who prefers a peaceful, resort‑style atmosphere.

Resort Areas

Saratoga Springs is a large resort with several distinct areas, each offering its own atmosphere and convenience:

  • The Springs — closest to the main lobby, dining and the feature pool.
  • Congress Park — closest to Disney Springs via the walking path.
  • The Paddock — home to its own pool and dining options.
  • The Carousel — quieter, more residential feel.
  • The Grandstand — first on the internal bus loop, great for transport.

No matter where you stay, the resort feels calm, scenic and beautifully spread out.

Studios & Villas

Saratoga Springs offers a full range of Deluxe Villa accommodation, ideal for longer stays or families who want extra space:

  • Deluxe Studios — sleep up to 4–5 with a kitchenette.
  • 1‑Bedroom Villas — full kitchen, living area and washer/dryer.
  • 2‑Bedroom Villas — perfect for larger families or groups.
  • 3‑Bedroom Grand Villas — spacious, luxurious and ideal for big groups.

Rooms are modern, comfortable and designed with soft colours and subtle equestrian touches.

Dining

Saratoga Springs offers relaxed, convenient dining options:

  • The Artist’s Palette — quick‑service meals and snacks.
  • The Paddock Grill — poolside dining.
  • On the Rocks Pool Bar — drinks and light bites.

And of course, you’re just a short walk from the incredible dining at Disney Springs.

Facilities

  • Multiple pools, including the High Rock Spring feature pool.
  • Senses Spa — full‑service spa and wellness centre.
  • Walking path to Disney Springs.
  • Golf course views and access.
  • Fitness centre, playgrounds and recreation.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

Saratoga Springs is located in the Disney Springs Resort Area. You can walk to Disney Springs, while buses run to all four theme parks and the water parks.

Why choose Disney’s Saratoga Springs Resort & Spa?

This resort is perfect if you want space, comfort and a peaceful atmosphere. The villas are fantastic for families, the grounds are beautiful and the ability to walk to Disney Springs is a huge bonus. A brilliant choice for guests who want a relaxed, home‑from‑home Disney stay.
