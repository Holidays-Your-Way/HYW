Candlewood Suites – Lake Buena Vista

Spacious suites, full kitchens and brilliant value near Disney.

A quiet, homely all‑suite hotel perfect for longer stays

Candlewood Suites LBV is a fantastic choice for guests who want space, comfort and flexibility. Every room is a suite with a full kitchen, making it ideal for families, couples and anyone staying 10–14 nights who wants a proper home‑from‑home. The atmosphere is calm and relaxed, the rooms are spacious, and the value is excellent.

With free laundry, a lovely pool and a peaceful location close to Disney, it’s a brilliant option for guests who want comfort without the premium price tag.

Who is Candlewood Suites LBV best for?

  • Families who want full kitchens and proper living space.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who need a home‑from‑home.
  • Couples who prefer a quiet, relaxed hotel.
  • Anyone who wants great value close to Disney.

Suites

Suites at Candlewood are spacious, practical and designed for longer stays. Expect:

  • Studio and one‑bedroom suites.
  • Full kitchens with fridge, stove, microwave and dishwasher.
  • Separate living and sleeping areas (in one‑bedroom suites).
  • Large workspaces and dining areas.
  • Plenty of storage for families.

It’s one of the best value‑for‑space options in Lake Buena Vista.

Dining

Candlewood is designed for self‑catering, with:

  • Full kitchens in every suite.
  • Outdoor BBQ and dining areas.
  • The Candlewood Cupboard — a 24/7 market for snacks and essentials.

And with Lake Buena Vista’s restaurants minutes away, you’ve got endless dining options nearby.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with plenty of seating.
  • Free guest laundry — ideal for longer stays.
  • Fitness centre.
  • BBQ and picnic areas.
  • 24/7 market for essentials.
  • Disney park shuttles.
  • Quiet, peaceful grounds.

Location

Candlewood Suites is perfectly located in Lake Buena Vista, close to restaurants, shops and Walt Disney World. It’s quiet, convenient and ideal for guests who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why I choose Candlewood Suites LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want space, comfort and flexibility. The full kitchens make life easier, the suites are ideal for longer stays, and the whole place feels calm, friendly and homely. A brilliant choice for families and couples who want a relaxed base near Disney without the premium price tag.
