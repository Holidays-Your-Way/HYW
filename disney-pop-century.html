Disney’s Pop Century Resort

Bright, fun and full of Disney magic — at great value.

A colourful, nostalgic Disney Value Resort

Disney’s Pop Century Resort celebrates the fads, fashions and fun of the 1950s through the 1990s. With larger‑than‑life icons, bright theming and a lively atmosphere, it’s a brilliant choice for families and couples who want to stay in the Disney bubble without stretching the budget.

Pop Century is also one of the best‑connected Value Resorts thanks to the Disney Skyliner — giving you quick, easy access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios.

Who is Pop Century best for?

  • Families who want a fun, colourful Disney hotel at great value.
  • Couples looking for an affordable way to stay on‑site.
  • Guests who want Skyliner access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios.
  • Anyone who loves nostalgic, playful theming.

Rooms

Rooms at Pop Century are bright, modern and cleverly designed, with a queen bed and a pull‑down Murphy bed that doubles as a table during the day. They’re compact but comfortable, with everything you need for a great stay.

Expect a mini‑fridge, coffee maker, large TV and plenty of storage — perfect for families and couples.

Dining

Pop Century offers:

  • Everything POP Food Court — a large, varied quick‑service restaurant.
  • Poolside bar for drinks and snacks.
  • Grab‑and‑go options for busy park days.

You can also walk to Art of Animation for even more dining choices.

Facilities

  • Three themed pools, including the Hippy Dippy Pool.
  • Disney Skyliner station.
  • Arcade and playground.
  • Jogging trail around Hourglass Lake.
  • Free Disney transportation.
  • On‑site laundry.

Location

Pop Century is located in the Wide World of Sports area of Walt Disney World, with easy access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios via the Skyliner. Buses run to Magic Kingdom, Animal Kingdom and Disney Springs.

Why choose Disney’s Pop Century Resort?

Pop Century is one of the best value‑for‑money Disney hotels. The Skyliner access is a huge bonus, the theming is fun and nostalgic, and the rooms are modern and comfortable. It’s a brilliant choice for families and couples who want to stay in the Disney magic without overspending.
