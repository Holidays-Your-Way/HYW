What to Pack for Walt Disney World

Everything you need for a magical, stress‑free holiday.

Pack Smart, Travel Happy

Packing for Walt Disney World is a little different from packing for a typical holiday. You’ll walk miles, spend long days in the sun, and experience Florida’s famously unpredictable weather. The right essentials can make your days smoother, more comfortable and far more magical.

This guide covers everything you need — from must‑haves to helpful extras.

Holiday Essentials

  • Passports & travel documents
  • Travel insurance details
  • MagicBands (if you have them)
  • Park tickets & reservation info
  • Credit/debit cards & spending money

Clothing

  • Lightweight, breathable clothing
  • Comfortable walking shoes (bring two pairs)
  • Sandals or flip‑flops for pool days
  • Light jacket or hoodie for cooler evenings
  • Swimwear for pools and water parks
  • Ponchos or light rain jackets (Florida showers are quick but heavy)

What to Pack in Your Park Bag

  • Refillable water bottle
  • Snacks (especially for kids)
  • Portable phone charger
  • Sun cream & lip balm
  • Hand sanitiser & wipes
  • Autograph book & pen
  • Mini first‑aid kit (plasters, pain relief, blister plasters)
  • Cooling towel or portable fan

Tech & Accessories

  • Portable chargers (you’ll use your phone a lot)
  • Charging cables & plug adapters
  • Camera or GoPro (optional)
  • Waterproof phone case for water rides

If You’re Travelling With Toddlers

  • Lightweight stroller
  • Favourite comfort toy
  • Snacks & drinks
  • Change of clothes
  • Sun hat & sunscreen
  • Portable fan
Read the Toddlers Guide

Weather‑Ready Items

  • Ponchos for sudden rain
  • Cooling towels for hot days
  • Light layers for cooler evenings
  • Sun protection (hats, sunglasses, SPF)

Helpful Extras

  • Ziploc bags (for wet clothes or snacks)
  • Reusable shopping bags
  • Travel laundry detergent
  • Cooling mist spray
  • Glow sticks for evening entertainment

Why Packing Smart Matters

The right essentials can make your Disney holiday smoother, more comfortable and far more magical. From beating the heat to staying organised, a well‑packed bag means fewer surprises and more time enjoying the magic. I’m here to help you prepare every step of the way.
