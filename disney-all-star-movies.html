Disney’s All‑Star Movies Resort

Classic Disney theming and great value for families.

A fun, colourful Disney Value Resort

Disney’s All‑Star Movies Resort celebrates some of Disney’s most beloved films with larger‑than‑life icons, bright theming and a lively, family‑friendly atmosphere. It’s a brilliant choice for families who want to stay in the Disney bubble without stretching the budget.

With Toy Story, 101 Dalmatians, Fantasia and more brought to life around the resort, it’s a hit with kids and nostalgic adults alike.

Who is All‑Star Movies best for?

  • Families who want fun, colourful Disney theming.
  • Guests looking for great value while staying on‑site.
  • Kids who love Toy Story, Dalmatians or classic Disney films.
  • Anyone who wants simple, comfortable accommodation with Disney perks.

Rooms

Rooms at All‑Star Movies are bright, modern and cleverly designed, with a queen bed and a pull‑down Murphy bed that doubles as a table during the day. They’re compact but comfortable, with everything you need for a great stay.

Expect a mini‑fridge, coffee maker, large TV and plenty of storage — ideal for families and couples.

Dining

The resort offers:

  • World Premiere Food Court — a large, varied quick‑service restaurant.
  • Poolside bar for drinks and snacks.
  • Grab‑and‑go options for busy park mornings.

You can also walk to All‑Star Music and All‑Star Sports for even more dining choices.

Facilities

  • Fantasia‑themed main pool.
  • Duck Pond Pool (Mighty Ducks‑themed).
  • Playground and arcade.
  • Jogging trail.
  • Free Disney transportation.
  • On‑site laundry.

Location

All‑Star Movies is located in the Animal Kingdom Resort Area of Walt Disney World. Buses run to all four theme parks, Disney Springs and the water parks.

Why choose Disney’s All‑Star Movies Resort?

This resort is perfect for families who want classic Disney magic at great value. The theming is fun and nostalgic, the rooms are modern and comfortable, and the atmosphere is lively and welcoming. A brilliant choice for families who want to stay on‑site without overspending.
