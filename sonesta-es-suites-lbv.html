Sonesta ES Suites – Lake Buena Vista

Spacious suites, full kitchens and a peaceful, village‑style setting.

A homely, all‑suite resort perfect for longer stays

Sonesta ES Suites is one of the most popular family hotels in Lake Buena Vista — spacious suites, full kitchens and a peaceful, village‑style layout that feels calm and welcoming. It’s ideal for guests who want more space than a standard hotel room and the flexibility to cook, relax and settle in properly over a 10–14 night stay.

With a lovely pool, friendly atmosphere and a brilliant location close to Disney, it’s a fantastic value‑for‑space option for families and couples alike.

Who is Sonesta ES Suites best for?

  • Families who want separate bedrooms and living areas.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who need a proper home‑from‑home.
  • Travellers who prefer full kitchens for easy meals.
  • Anyone who wants a peaceful, village‑style resort near Disney.

Suites

Suites at Sonesta ES are spacious, comfortable and designed for real family living. Expect:

  • One‑ and two‑bedroom suites.
  • Full kitchens with fridge, oven, microwave and dishwasher.
  • Separate living and dining areas.
  • Plenty of storage for longer stays.
  • Bright, homely décor.

It’s one of the best space‑per‑pound options in the Lake Buena Vista area.

Dining

While Sonesta doesn’t offer full on‑site restaurants, the kitchens make self‑catering easy, and you’re surrounded by restaurants in the Lake Buena Vista area. Expect:

  • Full kitchens in every suite.
  • BBQ and outdoor dining areas.
  • Grab‑and‑go market for essentials.

And with Disney Springs and LBV dining minutes away, you’re never short of options.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with plenty of seating.
  • Whirlpool spa.
  • Fitness centre.
  • BBQ and picnic areas.
  • On‑site market for snacks and essentials.
  • Disney park shuttles.
  • Peaceful, village‑style grounds.

Location

Sonesta ES Suites is perfectly located in Lake Buena Vista, close to restaurants, shops and Walt Disney World. It’s quiet, convenient and ideal for families who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose Sonesta ES Suites?

This hotel is perfect if you want space, comfort and flexibility. The suites are fantastic for longer stays, the kitchens make life easier, and the whole resort feels calm, friendly and homely. A brilliant choice for families and couples who want a relaxed base near Disney.
