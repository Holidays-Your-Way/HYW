Staybridge Suites – Lake Buena Vista

All‑suite comfort, full kitchens and a peaceful, homely atmosphere.

A homely, all‑suite resort perfect for longer stays

Staybridge Suites LBV is one of the most popular family hotels in the area — spacious suites, full kitchens and a calm, village‑style layout that feels welcoming from the moment you arrive. It’s ideal for guests who want more space than a standard hotel room and the flexibility to cook, relax and settle in properly over a 10–14 night stay.

With free breakfast, a lovely pool and a brilliant location close to Disney, it’s a fantastic value‑for‑space option for families and couples alike.

Who is Staybridge Suites LBV best for?

  • Families who want separate bedrooms and living areas.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who need a proper home‑from‑home.
  • Travellers who prefer full kitchens for easy meals.
  • Anyone who wants a peaceful, village‑style resort near Disney.

Suites

Suites at Staybridge are spacious, comfortable and designed for real family living. Expect:

  • One‑ and two‑bedroom suites.
  • Full kitchens with fridge, oven, microwave and dishwasher.
  • Separate living and dining areas.
  • Plenty of storage for longer stays.
  • Bright, homely décor.

It’s one of the best space‑per‑pound options in Lake Buena Vista.

Dining

Staybridge offers simple but genuinely useful dining options:

  • Free hot breakfast every morning.
  • Full kitchens in every suite for easy meals.
  • Outdoor BBQ and dining areas.
  • Grab‑and‑go market for essentials.

And with Lake Buena Vista’s restaurants minutes away, you’re never short of choice.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with plenty of seating.
  • Whirlpool spa.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Free breakfast.
  • BBQ and picnic areas.
  • Disney park shuttles.
  • Quiet, village‑style grounds.

Location

Staybridge Suites is perfectly located in Lake Buena Vista, close to restaurants, shops and Walt Disney World. It’s quiet, convenient and ideal for families who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose Staybridge Suites LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want space, comfort and flexibility. The suites are fantastic for longer stays, the kitchens make life easier, and the whole resort feels calm, friendly and homely. A brilliant choice for families and couples who want a relaxed base near Disney without the premium price tag.
