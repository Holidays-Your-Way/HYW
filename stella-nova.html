Stella Nova Resort

A fresh, modern Value hotel with a cosmic theme and the closest spot to Epic Universe.

Stella Nova Resort

Stella Nova is one of Universal’s newest hotels, and it has a really fresh, modern feel to it. The whole place has this soft, cosmic theme, nothing too in‑your‑face, just gentle colours, curved shapes and a slightly futuristic vibe. It feels clean, bright and very new, which is lovely if you like that crisp, modern hotel feel.

Even though it’s a Value hotel, it doesn’t feel “budget”. The lobby is airy, the rooms are nicely designed, and everything has that brand new shine. It’s simple, but in a stylish way. It is the kind of place where you walk in and think, “Oh, this is nice.”

One of the biggest reasons people are excited about Stella Nova is the location. It’s the closest value hotel to Epic Universe, which makes it a brilliant choice if you’re planning to spend a lot of time in the new park. Being able to pop back for a break, a swim or a rest is such a bonus, especially in the Florida heat.

The pool area is lovely, it is bright, modern and perfect for cooling off. It’s not themed like the bigger resorts, but it’s clean, spacious and has that relaxed “holiday afternoon” feel. There’s a pool bar too, which is always good.

Rooms are simple but modern, with everything you need for a comfortable stay. They’re not huge, but they’re well laid out and feel fresh and uncluttered. If you like clean lines and a modern look, you’ll feel at home here.

Dining is straightforward, a good food court with plenty of quick options, plus grab and go bits for early mornings. And like the other Value hotels, you’ve got a Starbucks in the lobby, which is practically essential for rope drop mornings.

Transport is easy. You’ll have regular shuttle buses to the parks, and because Stella Nova is so close to Epic Universe, getting there is quick and simple. It’s not walking distance to Universal Studios or Islands of Adventure, but the buses make it all very manageable.

And just like Surfside and Dockside, you’re only a short stop from International Drive. That means even more dining options, shops and other things to do in the eveninga. It’s a lovely bonus if you like having food choices, not just the hotel food court.

Stella Nova is a great pick if you want something modern, clean and close to Epic Universe without spending a fortune. It’s simple, stylish and very easy to stay in. It is a brilliant option for families who want a fresh, new hotel at a great price.

Pros & Considerations

Pros

  • Brand‑new, modern Value hotel
  • Clean, bright, stylish design
  • Closest value hotel to Epic Universe
  • Lovely pool area with a relaxed feel
  • Starbucks in the lobby
  • Quick shuttle buses
  • Early park entry included
  • Walking distance to International Drive for extra dining options
  • Great value for a brand new hotel

Considerations

  • Not walking distance to Universal Studios or Islands of Adventure
  • Theming is gentle rather than immersive
  • Rooms are modern but on the smaller side
  • Fewer on‑site dining options than the bigger resorts
  • No water taxi or walking path
