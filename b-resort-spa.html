B Resort & Spa – Disney Springs Area

Stylish, modern and perfectly placed for Disney Springs.

A chic, modern resort with a boutique feel

B Resort & Spa is one of the most stylish hotels in the Disney Springs area — modern, bright and full of personality. It has a boutique‑leaning feel without the boutique price tag, making it a great choice for couples and families who want something a little more polished than the standard Lake Buena Vista hotels.

With a lovely pool, a full‑service spa, on‑site dining and a fantastic location just minutes from Disney Springs, it’s a brilliant base for guests who want comfort, style and convenience.

Who is B Resort & Spa best for?

  • Couples who want a modern, stylish hotel near Disney Springs.
  • Families who prefer contemporary rooms and a relaxed resort feel.
  • Guests who enjoy a boutique‑style atmosphere without premium pricing.
  • Anyone who wants a sleek, comfortable base close to Disney.

Rooms

Rooms at B Resort are bright, modern and full of character, with clean lines, bold colours and a fresh, contemporary feel. Expect plush beds, good storage, large windows and a stylish design that feels a step above typical mid‑range hotels.

They’re ideal for guests who want something modern and comfortable with a bit of flair.

Dining

The hotel offers several convenient dining options:

  • A casual on‑site restaurant for breakfast and dinner.
  • A lobby bar for cocktails and small plates.
  • A grab‑and‑go market for snacks, coffee and essentials.

And with Disney Springs just minutes away, you’ve got endless restaurants and entertainment options nearby.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with cabanas and plenty of seating.
  • Full‑service spa.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site dining and grab‑and‑go options.
  • Shuttle transport to Walt Disney World.
  • Modern lobby and lounge spaces.

Location

B Resort & Spa is perfectly located in the Disney Springs area, just minutes from world‑class dining, entertainment and shopping. It’s ideal for guests who want easy access to Disney without staying on‑site.

Why choose B Resort & Spa?

This hotel is perfect if you want a modern, stylish base close to Disney Springs. The rooms feel fresh and contemporary, the facilities are excellent for the price, and the whole place has a boutique‑leaning vibe that sets it apart from other Lake Buena Vista hotels. A brilliant choice for couples and families who want comfort with a bit of flair.
