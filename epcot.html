EPCOT

A celebration of culture, innovation and imagination.

A park unlike any other

EPCOT is a unique blend of futuristic attractions, global culture and incredible dining. It’s a park where you can explore space, travel through time, dive into the oceans and then stroll around 11 different countries — all in one day.

With thrilling new attractions, seasonal festivals and world‑class food and drink, EPCOT is perfect for families, couples and anyone who loves exploring and discovering new experiences.

Who is EPCOT best for?

  • Foodies who want amazing dining and festival treats.
  • Families who enjoy a mix of thrills and gentle attractions.
  • Couples looking for a relaxed, scenic Disney day.
  • Guests who love culture, technology and exploration.

The Four Neighbourhoods of EPCOT

✨ World Celebration

The heart of EPCOT, home to Spaceship Earth and beautiful gardens.

🌐 World Showcase

11 countries around a stunning lagoon — full of food, culture and entertainment.

🔬 World Discovery

Thrills, space travel and cutting‑edge attractions.

🌱 World Nature

Experiences celebrating the natural world, oceans and imagination.

Dining at EPCOT

EPCOT is known for some of the best dining at Walt Disney World — from quick bites to signature restaurants.

  • Le Cellier Steakhouse (Canada)
  • Via Napoli (Italy)
  • Teppan Edo (Japan)
  • La Hacienda de San Angel (Mexico)
  • Les Halles Boulangerie-Patisserie (France)
  • Space 220 — a dining experience in orbit
  • Connections Eatery — modern quick service

EPCOT also hosts seasonal festivals with incredible food booths throughout the year.

Seasonal Festivals

EPCOT is famous for its rotating festivals, each offering unique food, entertainment and experiences:

  • International Festival of the Arts (Jan–Feb)
  • International Flower & Garden Festival (Spring)
  • International Food & Wine Festival (Late Summer–Autumn)
  • International Festival of the Holidays (Nov–Dec)

Entertainment & Nighttime Spectacular

EPCOT offers beautiful live entertainment throughout the park, from cultural performances to musicians and seasonal shows.

The nighttime spectacular over World Showcase Lagoon is a highlight — combining fireworks, music and stunning visuals.

Top Tips for Visiting EPCOT

  • Use Genie+ for Frozen Ever After and Remy’s Ratatouille Adventure.
  • Arrive early for Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind virtual queue.
  • Plan your dining — EPCOT restaurants book up quickly.
  • Take your time exploring World Showcase.
  • Stay for the nighttime spectacular — it’s worth it.

Why I Recommend EPCOT

EPCOT is perfect for guests who want a mix of thrills, culture and incredible food. It’s relaxing, scenic and full of unique experiences you won’t find anywhere else. Whether you’re exploring the countries, enjoying a festival or riding one of Disney’s newest attractions, EPCOT offers something special for everyone.
