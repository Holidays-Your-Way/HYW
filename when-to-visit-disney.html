When to Visit Walt Disney World

Crowds, weather, events and the best times to go.

Choosing the Perfect Time for Your Disney Holiday

Walt Disney World is magical all year round — but the experience can feel very different depending on when you visit. Crowd levels, weather, school holidays and special events all play a big part in planning the perfect trip.

This guide breaks down the best times to visit based on what matters most to your family.

Best Times to Visit (Overall)

For the best mix of lower crowds, comfortable weather and great prices, I recommend:

  • Mid‑January to early March
  • Late April to mid‑May
  • Mid‑September to mid‑November

These periods offer shorter queues, pleasant temperatures and plenty of seasonal events.

Month‑by‑Month Overview

🌤 January – February

Cooler weather, lower crowds and great for first‑timers. Some rides may be closed for refurbishment.

🌸 March – April

Spring break brings higher crowds, but EPCOT’s Flower & Garden Festival is beautiful.

☀️ May – June

Warm weather, moderate crowds and longer park hours.

🔥 July – August

Hot, humid and busy — but great for families tied to school holidays.

🍂 September – October

Lower crowds, Halloween events and warm weather. A fantastic time to visit.

🎄 November – December

Christmas decorations, festive events and magical atmosphere. Crowds increase closer to Christmas.

Seasonal Events to Consider

  • EPCOT Festival of the Arts (January – February)
  • EPCOT Flower & Garden Festival (Spring)
  • Mickey’s Not‑So‑Scary Halloween Party (Autumn)
  • EPCOT Food & Wine Festival (Summer – Autumn)
  • Mickey’s Very Merry Christmas Party (Winter)

These events can add extra magic — but also extra crowds.

Weather at Walt Disney World

  • Winter: Mild and comfortable
  • Spring: Warm and pleasant
  • Summer: Hot, humid and rainy
  • Autumn: Warm with occasional storms

Florida weather can change quickly — pack for sunshine and showers.

Top Tips for Choosing Your Dates

  • Travel outside school holidays for lower crowds.
  • Visit during festivals if you love food and entertainment.
  • Choose cooler months if heat is a concern.
  • Book early for Christmas and Halloween — they sell out fast.
