Homewood Suites by Hilton – International Drive

Spacious suites, homely comfort and brilliant value for families.

A homely, spacious all‑suite hotel perfect for longer stays

Homewood Suites is one of the best family hotels on International Drive if you want space, comfort and great value. Every room here is a full suite with a separate living area and a proper kitchen — ideal for families who want flexibility, extra room and the option to eat in when it suits.

The atmosphere is warm and relaxed, the staff are friendly, and the location is fantastic for Universal, ICON Park and the rest of I‑Drive. It’s a brilliant choice for longer stays or anyone who wants a more “home‑from‑home” feel.

Who is Homewood Suites best for?

  • Families who want extra space and a separate living area.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who want a homely base.
  • Travellers who like having a kitchen for easy meals.
  • Anyone who wants comfort, convenience and great value.

Suites

Suites are the star of the show here — spacious, comfortable and designed for real living. Expect:

  • A separate bedroom and living room.
  • A full kitchen with fridge, microwave, hob and dishwasher.
  • A dining area.
  • A sofa bed for extra sleeping space.

Everything feels clean, practical and well‑maintained — perfect for families who want room to spread out.

Dining

Homewood Suites offers great value with:

  • Free hot breakfast every morning.
  • A small convenience shop for snacks and essentials.
  • Full kitchens in every suite for easy meals.

And with ICON Park and International Drive just minutes away, you’ve got endless restaurants within walking distance.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool and sun deck.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Free hot breakfast.
  • Full kitchens in every suite.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Spacious lobby and lounge areas.

Location

Homewood Suites is perfectly located near ICON Park, close to restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal is a short drive or shuttle ride away, and you’ve got easy access to SeaWorld and the rest of International Drive.

Why choose Homewood Suites?

This hotel is perfect if you want space, comfort and flexibility in a brilliant location. The suites are fantastic for families, the kitchens make life easier, and the whole place feels warm, relaxed and great value. A superb choice for longer stays or anyone who wants a homely base near Universal.
