Disney’s Port Orleans Resort – Riverside

A charming, peaceful Disney Moderate inspired by the American South.

A beautifully themed resort with a relaxed, romantic feel

Disney’s Port Orleans Resort – Riverside captures the charm of the American South with its elegant mansions, rustic bayou lodges and scenic riverside pathways. It’s peaceful, picturesque and full of character — perfect for families and couples who want a more relaxed Disney stay.

With beautiful grounds, a fantastic pool and easy access to Disney Springs by boat, it’s one of the most loved Moderate Resorts.

Who is Port Orleans Riverside best for?

  • Couples who want a peaceful, romantic Disney resort.
  • Families who love beautiful theming and relaxed surroundings.
  • Guests who want easy boat access to Disney Springs.
  • Anyone who wants a charming Moderate Resort with great value.

Rooms & Royal Guest Rooms

Rooms at Riverside are warm, comfortable and beautifully themed. You can choose between the rustic Alligator Bayou lodges or the elegant Magnolia Bend mansions.

The resort is also home to the popular Royal Guest Rooms — beautifully decorated rooms inspired by Disney princesses, complete with magical touches and special theming.

Dining

Riverside offers several great dining options:

  • Riverside Mill Food Court — a large, varied quick‑service restaurant.
  • Boatwright’s Dining Hall — Southern‑inspired table‑service dining.
  • River Roost Lounge — home of the famous Yeeha Bob Jackson.

You can also walk to Port Orleans French Quarter for even more dining choices.

Facilities

  • Ol’ Man Island — a fantastic themed pool with a waterslide.
  • Leisure pools across the resort.
  • Boat transportation to Disney Springs.
  • Fishing, carriage rides and walking trails.
  • Playgrounds and activities for kids.
  • Free Disney transportation.
  • On‑site laundry.

Location

Port Orleans Riverside is located in the Disney Springs Resort Area. You can travel to Disney Springs by boat, while buses run to all four theme parks and the water parks.

Why choose Disney’s Port Orleans Resort – Riverside?

This resort is perfect if you want a peaceful, charming Disney stay with beautiful theming and great facilities. The Royal Rooms are a lovely option for families, the boat to Disney Springs is a huge bonus, and the atmosphere is relaxed and welcoming. A brilliant choice for couples and families who want comfort, scenery and value.
