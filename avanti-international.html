Avanti International Resort

Bright, modern and perfectly placed on International Drive.

A lively, modern resort with great value and a fantastic location

Avanti International is a bright, contemporary resort right in the heart of International Drive. It’s stylish without being expensive, lively without being chaotic, and perfectly placed for families and couples who want easy access to Orlando’s attractions.

The pool area is the star of the show — modern, spacious and great for relaxing on non‑park days. Add in a café, a lobby bar, a games room and a free I‑Ride Trolley stop right outside, and you’ve got a brilliant all‑rounder for an Orlando holiday.

Who is Avanti International best for?

  • Families who want a modern, great‑value hotel on I‑Drive.
  • Couples who like a bright, contemporary atmosphere.
  • Guests who want a lively resort feel without premium prices.
  • Anyone who wants restaurants, shops and attractions within walking distance.

Rooms

Rooms at Avanti International are modern, clean and comfortable, with bright décor and everything you need for a relaxing stay. Expect comfy beds, a fridge, coffee maker and plenty of space for families. They’re simple but stylish — ideal for park‑focused holidays.

Dining

The Avanti Café offers quick, easy meals throughout the day, and the lobby bar is great for evening drinks. There’s also a Starbucks‑style coffee bar for grab‑and‑go breakfasts. And with International Drive outside your door, you’ve got endless dining options within minutes.

Facilities

  • Large, modern pool with plenty of seating.
  • Hot tub and spacious sun deck.
  • Avanti Café and lobby bar.
  • Games room and fitness centre.
  • I‑Ride Trolley stop right outside.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.

Location

The location is one of the biggest reasons people choose Avanti. You’re right on International Drive, close to ICON Park, Pointe Orlando, restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal is just a short drive or shuttle ride away, making it a brilliant base for exploring Orlando.

Why choose Avanti International?

Avanti International is perfect if you want a modern, lively, great‑value hotel in a fantastic location. The pool area is lovely, the atmosphere is upbeat and the whole resort feels fresh and welcoming. It’s a brilliant choice for families and couples who want style without the price tag.
