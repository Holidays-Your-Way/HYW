Hyatt House Across from Universal

Spacious suites and home‑from‑home comfort near Universal Orlando.

A modern, suite‑style hotel perfect for families

Hyatt House Across from Universal is one of the best mid‑range hotels for families and longer stays. With spacious suites, fully equipped kitchens and a fantastic complimentary breakfast, it offers the perfect blend of comfort, convenience and value — all just minutes from Universal Orlando.

It’s stylish, relaxed and brilliantly designed for guests who want more space than a standard hotel room provides.

Suites & Rooms

Rooms at Hyatt House are bright, modern and spacious, with separate living and sleeping areas. Many suites include a full kitchen with a fridge, microwave, stovetop and dishwasher — ideal for families who want the flexibility to cook or prepare snacks.

Expect plush Hyatt beds, a sofa‑sleeper, large TV, dining space and contemporary décor throughout.

Dining

And with Universal’s dining district minutes away, you’ll never be short of options.
