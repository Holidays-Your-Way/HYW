Disney’s All‑Star Music Resort

A lively, musical Disney Value Resort with great family options.

A fun, upbeat Disney Value Resort

Disney’s All‑Star Music Resort celebrates the world of music with bright, colourful theming inspired by genres like jazz, rock, country, Broadway and calypso. It’s lively, family‑friendly and full of Disney charm — perfect for guests who want great value while staying in the heart of the magic.

It’s also the only All‑Star Resort that offers **Family Suites**, giving larger families extra space and comfort.

Who is All‑Star Music best for?

  • Families who want fun, colourful Disney theming.
  • Guests looking for great value while staying on‑site.
  • Larger families who need the extra space of a Family Suite.
  • Anyone who loves music‑inspired décor and a lively atmosphere.

Rooms & Family Suites

All‑Star Music offers two types of accommodation:

  • Standard Rooms — bright, modern and cleverly designed with a queen bed and a pull‑down Murphy bed.
  • Family Suites — sleep up to 6, with a bedroom, two bathrooms, kitchenette and living area.

The Family Suites are a brilliant option for larger families who want extra space without moving up to a Moderate or Deluxe resort.

Dining

The resort offers:

  • Intermission Food Court — a large, varied quick‑service restaurant.
  • Poolside bar for drinks and snacks.
  • Grab‑and‑go options for busy park mornings.

You can also walk to All‑Star Movies and All‑Star Sports for even more dining choices.

Facilities

  • Calypso Pool (main pool).
  • Piano Pool (quiet pool).
  • Playground and arcade.
  • Jogging trail.
  • Free Disney transportation.
  • On‑site laundry.

Location

All‑Star Music is located in the Animal Kingdom Resort Area of Walt Disney World. Buses run to all four theme parks, Disney Springs and the water parks.

Why choose Disney’s All‑Star Music Resort?

This resort is perfect for families who want great value and fun theming. The Family Suites are a huge bonus for larger groups, the atmosphere is lively and welcoming, and the rooms are modern and comfortable. A brilliant choice for families who want to stay in the Disney magic without overspending.
