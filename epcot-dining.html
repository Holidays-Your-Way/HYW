EPCOT Dining

World flavours, festival favourites and unforgettable meals.

A Food Lover’s Paradise

EPCOT is famous for its incredible dining — from global cuisine in World Showcase to modern, innovative dishes in World Celebration, World Nature and World Discovery. Whether you’re looking for a romantic dinner, a family feast or a quick bite between attractions, EPCOT has some of the best food at Walt Disney World.

With festivals running most of the year, there’s always something new to try.

Character Dining at EPCOT

👑 Akershus Royal Banquet Hall (Norway Pavilion)

Dine with Disney Princesses in a beautiful medieval castle setting.

🐭 Garden Grill Restaurant (The Land Pavilion)

Family‑style meals with Mickey, Pluto and Chip ‘n’ Dale — the restaurant slowly rotates!

Both restaurants are included in the Disney Dining Plan (Akershus may require two credits for certain meals).

Top Table‑Service Restaurants

🍣 Takumi‑Tei (Japan Pavilion)

Signature dining with exquisite Japanese cuisine and stunning presentation.

🥩 Le Cellier Steakhouse (Canada Pavilion)

Famous for steaks, cheddar cheese soup and a cosy, candlelit atmosphere.

🍝 Via Napoli Ristorante e Pizzeria (Italy Pavilion)

Authentic Neapolitan pizza baked in wood‑fired ovens.

🍛 Spice Road Table (Morocco Pavilion)

Small plates with beautiful waterfront views.

🍽️ Coral Reef Restaurant (The Seas Pavilion)

Dine beside a giant aquarium filled with sea life.

🍷 La Hacienda de San Angel (Mexico Pavilion)

Mexican cuisine with stunning views of the lagoon — perfect for fireworks.

Best Quick‑Service Restaurants

🍔 Connections Eatery (World Celebration)

Modern, international flavours with plenty of seating.

🥙 Tangierine Café (Morocco Pavilion)

Delicious Mediterranean dishes — a hidden gem.

🍱 Katsura Grill (Japan Pavilion)

Japanese quick‑service with peaceful outdoor seating.

🌮 La Cantina de San Angel (Mexico Pavilion)

Tacos, nachos and lakeside views.

🥨 Sommerfest (Germany Pavilion)

Bratwurst, pretzels and classic German snacks.

Must‑Try Snacks

  • School Bread (Norway Pavilion)
  • Caramel treats from Karamell‑Küche (Germany Pavilion)
  • Gelato (Italy Pavilion)
  • Crêpes (France Pavilion)
  • Festival booth favourites (seasonal)

EPCOT is snack heaven — especially during festivals like Food & Wine, Flower & Garden and Festival of the Arts.

EPCOT Festivals

EPCOT hosts festivals for most of the year, each with unique food booths and seasonal menus:

  • International Festival of the Arts
  • International Flower & Garden Festival
  • International Food & Wine Festival
  • International Festival of the Holidays

These festivals are perfect for snack credits on the Disney Dining Plan.

Top Dining Tips for EPCOT

  • Book Table‑Service restaurants 60 days before arrival.
  • Visit festival booths early to avoid long queues.
  • Share dishes so you can try more flavours.
  • Plan a sit‑down meal during the afternoon heat.
  • Ask me for personalised recommendations based on your tastes.

Why I Recommend EPCOT Dining

EPCOT offers some of the best dining at Walt Disney World — from global cuisine to festival favourites. Whether you’re a foodie, a family or a first‑timer, there’s something here for every taste and every budget. It’s the perfect park for exploring, tasting and discovering new favourites.
