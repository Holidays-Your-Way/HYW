Choosing the Right Disney Hotel

With more than 25 Disney hotels to choose from, picking the right one can feel overwhelming — especially for UK families planning a once‑in‑a‑lifetime holiday. Each resort has its own theme, location, transport options and atmosphere, and the “best” one depends entirely on your family’s style.

This guide highlights the top Disney hotels for different types of travellers, so you can find the perfect fit.

Best Value Resorts

🏰 Disney’s Art of Animation Resort

Perfect for families who love Disney theming. The Cars, Lion King and Finding Nemo suites are ideal for larger families and offer extra space.

🎨 Disney’s Pop Century Resort

Bright, fun and brilliantly located on the Skyliner route — great value for money.

Best Moderate Resorts

🌺 Disney’s Caribbean Beach Resort

A favourite for UK families thanks to its tropical theme and Skyliner access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios.

🎭 Disney’s Port Orleans Riverside

Peaceful, charming and perfect for families who want a quieter atmosphere.

🎷 Disney’s Port Orleans French Quarter

Small, walkable and romantic — ideal for couples or adults.

Best Deluxe Resorts

🏰 Disney’s Grand Floridian Resort

Classic luxury, monorail access and stunning views — perfect for special occasions.

🦒 Disney’s Animal Kingdom Lodge

Unique, peaceful and unforgettable — with savannah views and incredible dining.

🎡 Disney’s BoardWalk Inn

Walk to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios, with nightlife right outside your door.

🎨 Disney’s Riviera Resort

Elegant, modern and romantic — a favourite for adults and couples.

Best for Families

  • Art of Animation – themed suites & great value
  • Caribbean Beach – Skyliner access & family‑friendly vibe
  • Animal Kingdom Lodge – wildlife & immersive theming

These resorts offer brilliant transport, great pools and plenty of space for UK families.

Best for Adults

  • Riviera Resort – elegant & romantic
  • BoardWalk Inn – nightlife & walkable location
  • Grand Floridian – luxury & fine dining

These resorts offer quieter atmospheres, beautiful lounges and easy access to the best adult‑friendly areas.

Best for Location

  • Contemporary Resort – walk to Magic Kingdom
  • BoardWalk Inn – walk to EPCOT & Hollywood Studios
  • Caribbean Beach – Skyliner hub

Location can make a huge difference to your holiday — especially for UK families staying 14–21 days.
