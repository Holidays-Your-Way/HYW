Holiday Inn Resort Orlando – Lake Buena Vista

A lively, family‑friendly resort with a huge pool and splash zone.

A fun, energetic resort perfect for families

Rooms

Rooms at Holiday Inn Resort LBV are bright, spacious and comfortable, with plenty of room for families to spread out. Expect plush beds, good storage and a clean, modern design that works well for longer stays.

Some rooms offer pool views, which are lovely in the evenings.

Dining

The resort offers several convenient dining options:

And with Lake Buena Vista’s restaurants minutes away, you’ve got endless choice nearby.

Location

Holiday Inn Resort LBV is perfectly located in Lake Buena Vista, close to restaurants, shops and Walt Disney World. It’s ideal for families who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose Holiday Inn Resort LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want a lively, family‑friendly resort with great facilities and brilliant value.
