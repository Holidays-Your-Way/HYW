Holiday Inn & Suites Across from Universal

Spacious, practical and perfectly placed for Universal Orlando.

A reliable, family‑friendly hotel with unbeatable convenience

Holiday Inn & Suites Across from Universal is a brilliant choice if you want space, comfort and a fantastic location without the on‑site price tag. It’s directly across from Universal Orlando, making it one of the most convenient off‑site hotels for families who want easy access to the parks.

The hotel is clean, modern and practical, with a mix of standard rooms and large suites that work perfectly for families. You’ve got a pool, a restaurant, a bar and free shuttles — everything you need for a smooth, easy stay.

Who is Holiday Inn Across from Universal best for?

  • Families who want spacious rooms or suites close to Universal.
  • Guests who prefer a modern, reliable hotel with good facilities.
  • Travellers who want convenience without on‑site pricing.
  • Anyone who values walkability and easy transport options.

Rooms & Suites

Rooms are modern, clean and comfortable, with everything you need for a relaxing stay. The real highlight is the one‑bedroom and two‑bedroom suites, which offer loads of space, separate living areas and kitchenettes — ideal for families or longer stays.

It’s a great step up from the budget hotels on International Drive, especially if you want more room to spread out.

Dining

The on‑site restaurant offers easy breakfasts and dinners, and there’s a bar for evening drinks. You’ve also got a grab‑and‑go market for snacks and essentials. And with International Drive just minutes away, you’re never short of dining options.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with plenty of seating.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site restaurant, bar and convenience market.
  • Free shuttle buses to Universal and other attractions.
  • Spacious suites ideal for families.

Location

The location is one of the biggest reasons families choose this hotel. You’re directly across from Universal Orlando — close enough to walk depending on your route — and just minutes from International Drive, the outlets and loads of dining options. It’s incredibly convenient without being on‑site.

Why choose Holiday Inn Across from Universal?

This hotel is perfect if you want a spacious, comfortable stay right next to Universal without paying on‑site prices. The suites are brilliant for families, the location is unbeatable and the whole place feels modern, friendly and easy. It’s a great all‑rounder for Universal holidays.
