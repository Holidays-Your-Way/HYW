Table‑Service Dining

Relax, unwind and enjoy Disney’s best dining experiences.

What is Table‑Service Dining?

Table‑Service dining includes all sit‑down restaurants at Walt Disney World — from casual family meals to signature fine dining. These restaurants offer full service, themed environments and some of the best food on Disney property.

It’s perfect for families who want a break from the parks, couples looking for a special evening or anyone who enjoys great food in a relaxed setting.

Why Choose Table‑Service Dining?

  • A relaxing break from the parks.
  • High‑quality meals with great variety.
  • Beautiful theming and immersive settings.
  • Perfect for celebrations and special occasions.
  • Many restaurants included in the Disney Dining Plan.

Popular Table‑Service Restaurants

🍽️ Be Our Guest (Magic Kingdom)

Dine inside Beast’s enchanted castle — a stunning, immersive experience.

🍝 Via Napoli (EPCOT)

Authentic Neapolitan pizza baked in wood‑fired ovens.

🥩 Le Cellier Steakhouse (EPCOT)

Canadian‑inspired steakhouse in a cosy, candlelit setting.

🍗 50’s Prime Time Café (Hollywood Studios)

Comfort food with a fun retro twist — and playful “family” servers.

🍛 Yak & Yeti (Animal Kingdom)

Asian‑inspired dishes in a beautifully themed Nepalese setting.

🌅 California Grill (Contemporary Resort)

Signature dining with incredible views of Magic Kingdom fireworks.

🍤 The Boathouse (Disney Springs)

Seafood, steaks and waterfront dining — a Disney Springs favourite.

Table‑Service Dining & the Disney Dining Plan

Table‑Service meals are included in the Disney Dining Plan. Most restaurants require one Table‑Service credit, but some signature restaurants require two credits, such as:

  • California Grill
  • Le Cellier Steakhouse
  • Cinderella’s Royal Table

The Quick‑Service Dining Plan does not include Table‑Service meals.

Top Tips for Table‑Service Dining

  • Book reservations 60 days before arrival — popular restaurants fill quickly.
  • Consider early lunches or late dinners for quieter dining rooms.
  • Use Table‑Service credits for higher‑value meals.
  • Plan around fireworks or nighttime shows for extra magic.

Why I Recommend Table‑Service Dining

Table‑Service restaurants offer some of the best food, theming and atmosphere at Walt Disney World. They’re perfect for relaxing, celebrating and enjoying memorable meals with family and friends. Whether you want a princess meal, a romantic dinner or a fun themed experience, there’s something for everyone.
