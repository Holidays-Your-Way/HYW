Embassy Suites by Hilton – International Drive

Spacious suites, great perks and a fantastic I‑Drive location.

A polished, all‑suite hotel with brilliant extras

Embassy Suites is one of the most comfortable and reliable all‑suite hotels on International Drive. Every room is a full suite with a separate living area, and the hotel’s signature perks — free cooked‑to‑order breakfast and an evening reception with drinks and snacks — make it fantastic value for families and couples.

The huge atrium gives the hotel a bright, open feel, and the location is spot‑on for ICON Park, Universal and the rest of I‑Drive. It’s a brilliant choice if you want space, comfort and a few little luxuries without stepping into premium‑hotel pricing.

Who is Embassy Suites best for?

  • Families who want a separate bedroom and living area.
  • Couples who enjoy a more polished, comfortable hotel.
  • Guests who love the idea of free breakfast and evening drinks.
  • Anyone who wants a spacious, relaxed base near ICON Park.

Suites

Suites are the highlight here — spacious, comfortable and ideal for longer stays. Expect:

  • A separate bedroom and living room.
  • A sofa bed for extra sleeping space.
  • A fridge, microwave and coffee maker.
  • Plenty of storage and room to relax.

Everything feels clean, well‑kept and noticeably more spacious than standard hotel rooms on I‑Drive.

Dining

Embassy Suites offers excellent value with:

  • Free cooked‑to‑order breakfast every morning.
  • Evening reception with complimentary drinks and snacks.
  • A casual on‑site restaurant and bar.
  • Grab‑and‑go options for quick bites.

And with ICON Park and International Drive just minutes away, you’ve got endless restaurants within walking distance.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool and hot tub.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Free breakfast and evening reception.
  • Spacious atrium and lounge areas.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • All‑suite accommodation for every guest.

Location

Embassy Suites is perfectly located near ICON Park, close to restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal is a short drive or shuttle ride away, and you’ve got easy access to SeaWorld and the rest of International Drive.

Why choose Embassy Suites>

This hotel is perfect if you want space, comfort and great perks in a brilliant location. The suites are ideal for families, the free breakfast and evening reception add real value, and the whole place feels polished and welcoming. A superb choice for couples and families who want a little extra comfort on I‑Drive.
