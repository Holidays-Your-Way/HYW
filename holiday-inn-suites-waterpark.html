Holiday Inn Resort Orlando Suites – Waterpark

A fun‑filled family resort with its own waterpark.

A lively, family‑focused resort packed with activities

Holiday Inn Resort Orlando Suites – Waterpark is a huge, entertainment‑packed resort designed especially for families. With its own waterpark, arcade, laser maze, movie nights and spacious family suites, it’s perfect for guests who want plenty to do on‑site as well as easy access to Disney.

It’s colourful, energetic and full of fun — ideal for families who want a resort that keeps the kids happy from morning to night.

Who is Holiday Inn Waterpark best for?

  • Families who want a resort with lots of on‑site activities.
  • Kids who love water slides, splash zones and arcades.
  • Guests who want spacious family suites.
  • Anyone who wants great value close to Disney.

Family Suites

Suites are designed with families in mind, offering separate sleeping areas, plenty of space and fun themed layouts. Expect a fridge, microwave, coffee maker and lots of storage — ideal for longer stays.

The extra space makes a big difference for families who want comfort and flexibility.

Dining

  • Grab‑and‑go market for snacks and essentials.

And with 536 and 192 nearby, you’ve got plenty of restaurants within a short drive.

Facilities

Location

The resort is located on 536, close to Disney and a short drive from 192, restaurants, shops and attractions. It’s ideal for families who want a lively, activity‑packed base near the parks.

Why choose Holiday Inn Waterpark?

This resort is perfect for families who want fun, space and great value. The waterpark is a huge hit with kids, the suites are ideal for longer stays, and the on‑site entertainment makes it easy to enjoy downtime between park days. A brilliant choice for families who want a lively, activity‑packed holiday.
