Rosen Centre Hotel

Elegant, spacious and perfectly placed on International Drive.

A refined, full‑service hotel with a calm, upscale feel

Rosen Centre is one of the most polished hotels on International Drive — elegant, spacious and beautifully maintained. It has a calm, grown‑up atmosphere that works brilliantly for couples, families with older children and anyone who prefers a more refined base for their Orlando holiday.

With a large pool, multiple restaurants, a spa, a fitness centre and direct access to the Orange County Convention Center, it offers more facilities than most hotels in the area. And with Pointe Orlando just a short walk away, you’ve got restaurants, bars and entertainment right on your doorstep.

Who is Rosen Centre best for?

  • Couples who want a stylish, comfortable hotel with great facilities.
  • Families with older children who prefer a quieter, more refined atmosphere.
  • Guests attending events at the Convention Center.
  • Anyone who wants a premium feel without luxury‑hotel prices.

Rooms

Rooms at Rosen Centre are modern, elegant and comfortable, with plush beds, spacious bathrooms and a warm, contemporary design. They feel a step above the typical I‑Drive hotel, offering a more premium experience without the premium price tag.

Suites are available for guests who want extra space or a more luxurious stay.

Dining

Rosen Centre has an impressive selection of dining options, including a steakhouse, a sushi bar, a café, a lobby bar and a 24‑hour deli. Whether you want a relaxed breakfast, a quick bite or a sit‑down dinner, you’ve got plenty of choice on‑site — and even more at Pointe Orlando nearby.

Facilities

  • Large outdoor pool with a spacious sun deck.
  • Spa and fitness centre.
  • Multiple dining options, including sushi and a steakhouse.
  • 24‑hour deli and lobby bar.
  • Direct skybridge access to the Convention Center.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.

Location

Rosen Centre is in a fantastic spot on International Drive — close to Pointe Orlando, ICON Park, restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal is a short drive or shuttle ride away, making it a brilliant base for guests who want convenience with a more refined hotel experience.

Why choose Rosen Centre?

Rosen Centre is perfect if you want a stylish, full‑service hotel with a calm, upscale feel in a brilliant location. It’s elegant, well‑maintained and offers fantastic value for the quality you get. A great choice for couples and families who want something a little more premium on International Drive.
