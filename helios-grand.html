Universal’s Helios Grand Hotel

Elegant, modern and beautifully designed — a premium stay at Universal.

A stylish, sun‑soaked resort with a calm, premium feel

Helios Grand is one of Universal’s newest hotels, and it really shows. The design is modern and elegant, with warm tones, soft lighting and a relaxed Mediterranean feel that makes the whole place feel calm and luxurious.

It’s the kind of hotel where everything feels effortless — from the bright, airy lobby to the beautifully designed pool areas. And because it’s part of the Universal family, you still get Early Park Admission and easy transport to the parks.

If you want a premium stay that feels fresh, modern and relaxing, Helios Grand is a fantastic choice.

Who is Helios Grand best for?

  • Couples who want a stylish, modern hotel with a premium feel.
  • Families who prefer calmer, more elegant resorts.
  • Guests who want a newer Universal hotel with great facilities.
  • Anyone who enjoys bright, airy, Mediterranean‑inspired design.

Rooms

Rooms at Helios Grand are modern, bright and beautifully styled. Think soft colours, natural textures and plenty of space to unwind. The large windows bring in loads of natural light, and the whole design feels calm and refreshing — perfect after long days in the parks.

Suites offer even more space and are ideal for families or anyone wanting a more luxurious stay.

Dining

The hotel has a great mix of dining options, from relaxed Mediterranean‑inspired dishes to quick bites and poolside snacks. Everything feels fresh, modern and well thought out. There’s also a lovely bar area for evening drinks.

Pool & perks

  • Beautifully designed pool areas with a calm, premium feel.
  • Early Park Admission (subject to conditions).
  • Shuttle transport to the parks.
  • Bright, modern design throughout the resort.
  • Lovely outdoor spaces for relaxing in the sunshine.

Why choose Helios Grand?

Helios Grand is perfect if you want a modern, elegant hotel that feels calm and premium without being overly formal. It’s beautifully designed, easy to relax in and offers great value for the quality you get. I recommend it a lot for couples and families who want something a bit more stylish for their Universal stay.
