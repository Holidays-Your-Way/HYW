Rosen Inn Lake Buena Vista

A family‑friendly budget hotel near Disney Springs.

A great value hotel close to Disney

Rosen Inn Lake Buena Vista is one of the best budget hotels in the Disney Springs area. It offers clean, comfortable rooms, a great pool area with a splash zone, and a location that’s ideal for families who want to be close to Disney without paying on‑site prices.

With free parking, on‑site dining and a relaxed, family‑friendly atmosphere, it’s a brilliant choice for guests who want value, convenience and comfort.

Who is Rosen Inn LBV best for?

  • Families who want a budget‑friendly hotel near Disney.
  • Guests who want a clean, reliable base with great facilities.
  • Couples looking for comfort and convenience without the premium price tag.
  • Anyone who wants easy access to Disney Springs and I‑Drive.

Rooms

Rooms at Rosen Inn LBV are simple, clean and well‑maintained. Expect comfortable beds, good storage, a fridge, microwave and coffee maker — ideal for families and longer stays.

They’re not luxury, but they’re reliable, practical and offer excellent value for the location.

Dining

The hotel offers several convenient dining options:

  • Buffet restaurant serving breakfast and dinner.
  • Lite Bite deli for snacks, drinks and essentials.
  • Poolside bar for casual meals and drinks.

And with Disney Springs minutes away, you’ve got endless dining choices nearby.

Facilities

  • Two outdoor pools.
  • Children’s splash zone.
  • On‑site dining and deli.
  • Free parking.
  • Game room.
  • Playground.
  • Shuttle options to Disney.

Location

Rosen Inn LBV is perfectly located near Disney Springs, with shops, restaurants and attractions all close by. It’s ideal for families who want easy access to Disney without staying on‑site.

Why choose Rosen Inn LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want a clean, reliable and affordable base close to Disney. The pools and splash zone are great for families, the rooms are practical and comfortable, and the location is hard to beat for the price. A brilliant choice for budget‑conscious guests who still want comfort and convenience.
