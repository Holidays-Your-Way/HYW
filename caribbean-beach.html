Disney’s Caribbean Beach Resort

Tropical theming, Skyliner access, and a relaxed family atmosphere.

Overview

Disney’s Caribbean Beach Resort is a colourful, laid‑back moderate resort set around a beautiful 45‑acre lake, with villages themed to the islands of Barbados, Jamaica, Aruba, Martinique, and Trinidad. It’s one of the most spacious Disney resorts, giving it a relaxed, holiday‑within‑a‑holiday feel — perfect for families who want room to breathe.

As a Skyliner hub, Caribbean Beach offers quick and easy access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios, making it one of the most convenient moderate resorts for park‑hopping. The tropical theming, sandy beaches, and vibrant colours create a fun, sunny atmosphere that appeals to families, couples, and groups alike.

Resort highlights

Skyliner convenience

Direct gondola access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios — fast, scenic, and easy.

Island‑themed villages

Stay in colourful sections inspired by the Caribbean islands.

Fuentes del Morro Pool

A pirate‑themed pool with slides, splash zones, and family fun.

Who this hotel is perfect for

  • Families who want a fun, colourful resort with plenty of space
  • Guests who want Skyliner access without paying deluxe prices
  • Groups who enjoy a relaxed, beachy atmosphere
  • Anyone who prefers a resort with a “holiday village” feel

Room types

Rooms at Caribbean Beach are bright, colourful, and comfortable, with tropical touches that match the resort’s island theme. Preferred rooms offer shorter walks to dining and transport, while Pirate Rooms add fun theming for younger families.

  • Standard Rooms: Standard, water, or courtyard views.
  • Preferred Rooms: Closer to the main pool, dining, and Skyliner station.
  • Pirate Rooms: Themed rooms in Trinidad with pirate‑ship beds — great for kids.

Location

Caribbean Beach sits between EPCOT and Hollywood Studios, making it one of the best‑located moderate resorts. With the Skyliner right on site, you can reach both parks quickly and easily, while buses connect you to the rest of Walt Disney World.

Things to consider

  • The resort is large — some rooms involve longer walks.
  • Pirate Rooms are fun but located furthest from the main amenities.
  • Skyliner can pause during storms, meaning buses may be used instead.

Why choose Disney’s Caribbean Beach?

  • You want Skyliner access without paying deluxe prices.
  • Your family loves bright, colourful theming.
  • You prefer a resort with space, beaches, and a relaxed vibe.
  • You want a great balance of value, convenience, and atmosphere.
