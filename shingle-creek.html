Rosen Shingle Creek

A peaceful luxury retreat just minutes from the action.

Overview

Rosen Shingle Creek is one of Orlando’s most impressive off‑site resorts — a peaceful, beautifully landscaped property that feels more like a luxury retreat than a theme‑park hotel. Set on a championship golf course, it offers spacious rooms, multiple pools, a relaxing spa, and a calm, grown‑up atmosphere that’s perfect after busy days in the parks.

It’s a fantastic choice for couples, older families, or anyone who wants premium comfort without the price tag of staying on‑site at Disney or Universal. With easy access to International Drive and the major parks, it strikes a great balance between relaxation and convenience.

Room types

Rooms at Rosen Shingle Creek are elegant, spacious, and designed for comfort. Expect warm colours, quality furnishings, and views over the golf course, pools, or gardens.

  • Deluxe Rooms: Large, comfortable rooms with premium amenities.
  • Executive Rooms: Upgraded features and extra space.
  • Suites: Ideal for longer stays or guests wanting more room to relax.

Location

Located just off Universal Boulevard, Rosen Shingle Creek offers quick access to Universal Orlando, SeaWorld, and International Drive. It’s close enough to the action but far enough away to feel peaceful and relaxing.

Things to consider

  • Not themed — better suited to guests who prefer a traditional resort feel.
  • No free theme park transport — taxis, rideshares, or car hire recommended.
  • Large property — some walking required between facilities.
