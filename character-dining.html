Disney Character Dining

Meet your favourite Disney characters while you dine.

What is Character Dining?

Character dining is one of the most magical experiences at Walt Disney World. Enjoy a delicious meal while your favourite Disney characters visit your table for photos, autographs and unforgettable moments — no queues, no stress and no rushing around the parks.

It’s perfect for families, first‑timers and anyone who wants a little extra Disney magic.

Why Families Love Character Dining

  • No waiting in character queues.
  • Meet multiple characters in one meal.
  • Great for young children who need breaks from the parks.
  • Perfect for birthdays and celebrations.
  • Fantastic photo opportunities.

Top Character Dining Experiences

🏰 Cinderella’s Royal Table (Magic Kingdom)

Dine inside Cinderella Castle with Disney Princesses. A truly iconic experience.

👑 Akershus Royal Banquet Hall (EPCOT)

Meet multiple princesses in a beautiful Norwegian setting.

🐻 The Crystal Palace (Magic Kingdom)

Buffet dining with Winnie the Pooh and friends.

🍳 Chef Mickey’s (Disney’s Contemporary Resort)

Mickey, Minnie, Goofy, Donald and Pluto — a classic family favourite.

🌲 Story Book Dining at Artist Point (Wilderness Lodge)

Snow White, Dopey, Grumpy and the Evil Queen in a stunning forest setting.

🌴 Tusker House (Animal Kingdom)

Safari‑themed dining with Donald Duck and friends.

🍽️ Hollywood & Vine (Hollywood Studios)

Seasonal dining with Minnie and friends, plus Disney Junior characters at breakfast.

Character Dining & the Disney Dining Plan

Character dining is included with the Disney Dining Plan. Most experiences require one Table‑Service credit, but a few premium meals require two credits, such as:

  • Cinderella’s Royal Table
  • Story Book Dining at Artist Point

The Quick‑Service Dining Plan does not include character dining.

Top Tips for Character Dining

  • Book early — reservations open 60 days before arrival.
  • Breakfast is usually the best value.
  • Arrive 10–15 minutes early for the best experience.
  • Have your camera ready — characters move quickly.
  • Let me know your must‑do characters and I’ll help you choose the perfect meal.

Why I Recommend Character Dining

Character dining is one of the easiest and most magical ways to meet Disney characters. It saves time, creates wonderful memories and adds something truly special to your holiday. Whether you’re celebrating or simply want a relaxed, magical meal, it’s a must‑do for many families.
