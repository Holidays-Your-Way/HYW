Crowne Plaza Orlando – Universal Blvd

Modern, refined and perfectly placed between Universal and I‑Drive.

A sleek, contemporary hotel with a calm, upscale feel

Crowne Plaza Orlando Universal is one of the most polished hotels in the I‑Drive area — modern, elegant and designed for guests who want a more refined stay without stepping into luxury‑hotel pricing. The atmosphere is calm and grown‑up, the rooms are stylish, and the facilities are excellent for both couples and families with older children.

With a lovely pool area, a great restaurant, a smart lobby bar and a fantastic location between Universal and ICON Park, it’s a brilliant choice for travellers who want comfort, convenience and a touch of sophistication.

Who is Crowne Plaza best for?

  • Couples who want a modern, refined hotel in a great location.
  • Families with older children who prefer a quieter atmosphere.
  • Guests who enjoy contemporary design and polished service.
  • Anyone who wants a premium feel without luxury‑hotel prices.

Rooms

Rooms at Crowne Plaza are sleek, modern and comfortable, with plush beds, stylish bathrooms and a warm, contemporary design. Everything feels clean, fresh and well‑maintained — a noticeable step up from standard I‑Drive hotels.

Suites are available for guests who want extra space or a more premium experience.

Dining

The hotel offers several excellent dining options:

  • A stylish on‑site restaurant serving breakfast and dinner.
  • A smart lobby bar for cocktails and small plates.
  • A grab‑and‑go market for snacks and essentials.

And with ICON Park and International Drive just minutes away, you’ve got endless restaurants within easy reach.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with hot tub and spacious sun deck.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site restaurant and lobby bar.
  • Grab‑and‑go market.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Modern lobby and lounge spaces.

Location

Crowne Plaza sits in a fantastic spot between Universal Orlando and ICON Park, close to restaurants, shops and attractions. It’s quiet enough to feel relaxing, but close enough to everything that getting around is easy.

Why choose Crowne Plaza Universal?

This hotel is perfect if you want a modern, refined base with great facilities and a calm atmosphere. It’s stylish without being formal, comfortable without being expensive, and brilliantly located for Universal and International Drive. A superb choice for couples and families who want something a little more polished.
