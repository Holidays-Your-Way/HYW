Hyatt Place Orlando / International Drive

Modern, spacious and perfectly placed for I‑Drive and Universal.

A modern, comfortable hotel with space to relax

Hyatt Place is one of the most reliable mid‑range hotels on International Drive — modern, clean and designed with families and longer stays in mind. The rooms are larger than average, the layout is clever, and the whole place has a calm, contemporary feel that makes it easy to settle in.

You’re close to ICON Park, restaurants and shops, with Universal just a short drive or shuttle ride away. It’s a brilliant choice if you want comfort, convenience and a little extra space without paying premium prices.

Who is Hyatt Place best for?

  • Families who want larger rooms and a modern feel.
  • Couples who prefer a clean, contemporary hotel.
  • Guests who want a calm, comfortable base near ICON Park.
  • Anyone who values space and convenience without luxury pricing.

Rooms

Rooms at Hyatt Place are a real highlight — spacious, modern and cleverly designed with a separate sleeping and living area. Expect plush beds, a sofa bed, a fridge, a large bathroom and plenty of storage. It’s ideal for families who want room to spread out or couples who enjoy a more comfortable space.

Everything feels clean, fresh and well‑maintained — a noticeable step up from standard I‑Drive hotels.

Dining

Hyatt Place offers a great selection of easy dining options:

  • Free hot breakfast every morning.
  • A 24/7 grab‑and‑go market for snacks and light meals.
  • A casual bar serving drinks and small plates.

And with ICON Park and International Drive just minutes away, you’ve got endless restaurants within walking distance.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with plenty of seating.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Free hot breakfast.
  • 24/7 grab‑and‑go market.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Spacious lobby and lounge areas.

Location

Hyatt Place is perfectly located near ICON Park, close to restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal is a short drive or shuttle ride away, and you’ve got easy access to SeaWorld and the rest of International Drive.

Why choose Hyatt Place?

This hotel is perfect if you want a modern, spacious, comfortable base in a brilliant location. The rooms are bigger than most in this price range, the atmosphere is calm and contemporary, and the free breakfast is a great bonus. A fantastic choice for families and couples who want comfort without the fuss.
