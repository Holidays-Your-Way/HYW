Who I am & how I work

I’m Alison, an independent UK travel agent with a serious soft spot for Orlando and a passport full of stamps from everywhere else. Holidays Your Way is built on one simple idea: your holiday should feel like it’s been planned for you, not copied from a brochure.

Orlando is my specialist subject – Universal, Disney, SeaWorld and everything in between – but I also plan trips to the Maldives, Caribbean, Europe, Asia and beyond. If I haven’t been yet, I’ve probably researched it to death.

You won’t get call‑centre scripts or generic advice here. Just honest guidance, real‑world tips and a proper person you can message when you’re not sure what to do next.
