Coco Key Hotel & Water Park

Colourful, fun and perfect for families who love water play.

A lively, family‑friendly hotel with its own water park

Coco Key is all about fun. It’s bright, colourful and packed with energy — the kind of place younger families absolutely love. The big draw here is the on‑site water park, complete with slides, splash zones and plenty of space for kids to burn off energy on non‑park days.

The hotel itself is simple and comfortable, with a relaxed atmosphere and everything you need for an easy stay. It’s not modern or upscale, but it’s friendly, great value and brilliant for families who want entertainment right on their doorstep.

Who is Coco Key best for?

  • Families with younger children who love water play.
  • Guests who want a fun, colourful hotel with lots to do on‑site.
  • Travellers looking for great value on International Drive.
  • Anyone who wants a lively, activity‑packed base.

Rooms

Rooms at Coco Key are simple, clean and comfortable — exactly what you need for a family‑focused holiday. Expect comfy beds, bright décor and practical amenities like a fridge and coffee maker. They’re not modern, but they’re well‑kept and ideal for families who plan to spend most of their time in the parks or at the water park.

Dining

The hotel has a casual restaurant, a poolside bar and a grab‑and‑go market for snacks and quick meals. It’s easy, family‑friendly dining — and with International Drive right outside, you’ve got loads of restaurants within walking distance.

Facilities

  • Large on‑site water park with slides and splash zones.
  • Outdoor pool separate from the water park.
  • Casual restaurant, pool bar and grab‑and‑go market.
  • Arcade and family activity areas.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Great value for families.

Location

Coco Key is right on International Drive, close to ICON Park, restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal is a short drive or shuttle ride away, making it a brilliant base for families who want convenience and plenty of entertainment nearby.

Why I choose Coco Key resort & waterpark?

Coco Key is perfect if you want a fun, lively, great‑value hotel with a water park on‑site. It’s colourful, energetic and brilliant for younger families who want entertainment built into their stay. It’s not modern or upscale, but it delivers exactly what it promises — and kids absolutely love it.
