Magic Moment Resort & Kids Club

A colourful, family‑focused resort designed with kids in mind.

A fun, family‑friendly resort on Kissimmee’s 192

Magic Moment Resort & Kids Club is a bright, playful and family‑focused hotel located on Kissimmee’s popular 192. It’s designed especially for children, with themed play areas, colourful décor and plenty of activities to keep little ones entertained.

It’s a great choice for families who want a fun, relaxed base close to Disney without the higher price tag of staying on‑site.

Who is Magic Moment Resort best for?

  • Families with younger children who want a playful, kid‑friendly hotel.
  • Guests who want great value close to Disney.
  • Parents who appreciate on‑site activities and themed play areas.
  • Anyone who wants a colourful, relaxed base on Kissimmee’s 192.

Rooms

Rooms are bright, clean and comfortable, with modern décor and practical amenities. Expect plush beds, air‑conditioning, a fridge, microwave and plenty of storage — ideal for families.

Some rooms include fun, kid‑friendly touches that make the stay even more memorable for little ones.

Dining

And with 192 right outside, you’re surrounded by family‑friendly restaurants and cafés.

Facilities

Location

Magic Moment is located on Kissimmee’s lively 192, close to Disney, restaurants, shops and attractions. It’s a brilliant base for families who want convenience and value.

Why choose Magic Moment Resort?

This hotel is perfect for families with younger children. The colourful décor, themed play areas and Kids Club make it a fun and memorable place to stay, while the location and value make it a great choice for parents. A lovely option for families who want a playful, budget‑friendly base near Disney.
