Let the Disney Magic Begin!

Walt Disney World is huge, brilliant and a little bit overwhelming if you’re looking at it for the first time. Four theme parks, water parks, resorts, dining, Lightning Lane, extras and “must‑dos” everywhere you look — it’s a lot. I’ve been visiting for over 40 years, and I still get that little flutter walking down Main Street, U.S.A.

This page is your calm starting point. Instead of trying to learn everything at once, you can dip into the guides that match where you are right now — whether that’s “we’ve never been before”, “we’re taking teens”, “we need help with Lightning Lane” or “we just want to know where to eat”.

You don’t have to become a Disney expert. You just need someone who already is. That’s where these guides — and I — come in.
