La Quinta Inn I‑Drive North

Simple, clean and perfectly located for Universal.

A great value hotel close to Universal Orlando

La Quinta Inn I‑Drive North is a brilliant budget option for families and couples who want to stay close to Universal without spending a fortune. The rooms are clean and comfortable, the pool area is a nice bonus, and the location — right at the top of International Drive — is ideal for park days, dining and shopping.

It’s simple, reliable and great value, making it a popular choice for guests who want comfort and convenience on a budget.

Who is La Quinta I‑Drive North best for?

  • Families who want a budget‑friendly hotel near Universal.
  • Guests who want clean, reliable accommodation without the frills.
  • Couples looking for a simple base close to I‑Drive attractions.
  • Anyone who prioritises location and value over luxury.

Rooms

Rooms are clean, comfortable and practical, with everything you need for a good night’s sleep. Expect air‑conditioning, a fridge, microwave, coffee maker and comfortable beds — ideal for families and longer stays.

They’re basic, but well‑maintained and great value for the location.

Dining

La Quinta offers simple on‑site dining options:

  • Complimentary breakfast.
  • Grab‑and‑go snacks in the lobby.

And with International Drive right outside, you’ve got endless restaurants within walking distance.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool.
  • Free breakfast.
  • Free parking.
  • Shuttle options to Universal.
  • 24‑hour front desk.
  • Pet‑friendly rooms.

Location

The location is one of the biggest selling points — you’re right at the top of International Drive, close to Universal Orlando, restaurants, shops and attractions. It’s ideal for guests who want convenience without the price tag.

Why choose La Quinta I‑Drive North?

This hotel is perfect if you want a clean, simple and affordable base close to Universal. The complimentary breakfast is a great perk, the rooms are comfortable, and the location is hard to beat for the price. A solid choice for budget‑conscious families and couples.
