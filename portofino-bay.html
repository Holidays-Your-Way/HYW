Loews Portofino Bay Hotel

Universal’s most luxurious resort, with Unlimited Express Pass included.

Loews Portofino Bay Hotel

Portofino Bay is Universal’s flagship resort, and it really does feel like something special. Inspired by the Italian seaside village of Portofino, it’s full of charm with cobblestone walkways, pastel coloured buildings, pretty harbour views and that warm, romantic atmosphere you only get from a place designed to feel timeless.

It’s the most luxurious hotel at Universal, but in a very relaxed, welcoming way. Nothing feels stuffy or formal, it’s elegant, calm and beautifully done. Families love it, couples love it, adults love it… it’s just one of those rare resorts that manages to feel both peaceful and full of life at the same time.

And here’s something a lot of people don’t know: Portofino Bay has some of the best family suites at Universal. The Despicable Me kids’ suites are hugely popular with younger families, but there are also spacious, elegant non themed suites for those who want more room without the character theming. These suites offer proper separation between sleeping areas, making them ideal for longer stays or families who want a bit more privacy and comfort.

And of course, the big perk: Unlimited Express Pass is included for your entire stay. This is a huge advantage, especially at busy times of year, and can easily save hours of queueing each day. It’s one of the main reasons Portofino Bay is such incredible value compared to buying Express separately.

The pool areas are gorgeous. There are three in total, including the main “beach pool” with a waterslide, sandy areas and plenty of space to relax. The atmosphere is calm and resort-like, which is perfect for a slower day or an afternoon break.

The rooms are some of the nicest at Universal. They’re spacious, elegant and beautifully designed, with soft colours, big bathrooms and a really luxurious feel. They’re ideal if you want a bit more comfort and space during your stay.

Dining here is excellent. Trattoria del Porto is great for relaxed meals, Bice Ristorante offers a more upscale experience, and the harbour area has lovely spots for drinks or gelato in the evening. There’s also a quick service marketplace and a Starbucks for early starts.

One of the best things about Portofino Bay is the water taxi. It’s peaceful, scenic and a lovely way to get to CityWalk and the parks. You can also walk if you prefer, the walk is beautiful and takes around 15 minutes.

Portofino Bay is perfect if you want a resort that feels luxurious, calm and full of charm. With the massive perk of Unlimited Express Pass included, and the option of family suites that give you extra space, privacy and comfort.

Pros & Considerations

Pros

  • Unlimited Express Pass included
  • Family suites available (including Despicable Me themed suites)
  • Universal’s most luxurious hotel
  • Beautiful Italian‑themed harbour and grounds
  • Three stunning pool areas
  • Excellent dining options
  • Water taxi and walking path to the parks
  • Spacious, elegant rooms
  • Early park entry included

Considerations

  • Most expensive Universal hotel
  • Large resort — some rooms are a long walk from the lobby
  • Quieter atmosphere may not suit those wanting something lively
  • No walking path to Volcano Bay
  • Dining and drinks are pricier than other resorts
