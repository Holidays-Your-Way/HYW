Staybridge Suites Orlando Royale Parc

Spacious suites and home‑from‑home comfort near Disney.

A fantastic all‑suite hotel perfect for families

Staybridge Suites Orlando Royale Parc is one of the best family hotels in the Kissimmee/192 area. With spacious suites, full kitchens, complimentary breakfast and a relaxed New Orleans‑inspired atmosphere, it’s ideal for longer stays and guests who want extra comfort and flexibility.

The location is brilliant too — close to Disney, Old Town and plenty of restaurants and shops.

Who is Staybridge Suites Royale Parc best for?

  • Families who want extra space and full kitchens.
  • Guests staying for a week or more.
  • Couples who want a relaxed, comfortable suite near Disney.
  • Anyone who wants great value without compromising on comfort.

Suites

Suites are spacious, modern and designed for comfort. Expect separate living and sleeping areas, full kitchens with a fridge, microwave, stovetop and dishwasher, plus plenty of storage — ideal for families and longer stays.

The extra space makes a huge difference after long days in the parks.

And with 192 right outside, you’re surrounded by restaurants for every taste and budget.

Location

The hotel is located just off Kissimmee’s lively 192, close to Disney, Old Town, Fun Spot and plenty of restaurants and shops. It’s a brilliant base for families who want convenience and comfort.

Why choose Staybridge Suites Royale Parc?

This hotel is perfect if you want space, comfort and flexibility. The full kitchens are ideal for families, the suites are genuinely spacious, and the complimentary breakfast is a huge perk. A fantastic choice for longer stays and guests who want a relaxed, home‑from‑home base near Disney.
