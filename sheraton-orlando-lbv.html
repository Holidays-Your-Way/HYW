Sheraton Orlando Lake Buena Vista Resort

A lively, family‑friendly resort close to Walt Disney World.

A fun, modern resort with great facilities and brilliant value

Sheraton Orlando Lake Buena Vista Resort is a fantastic choice for families who want a lively, modern hotel with great facilities and a brilliant location close to Disney. The atmosphere is upbeat and friendly, the rooms are bright and comfortable, and the pool area is one of the highlights — perfect for relaxing on non‑park days.

With on‑site dining, a waterslide, Disney shuttles and plenty of space to unwind, it’s a strong all‑round option for families and couples who want comfort and convenience without the premium price tag.

Who is Sheraton Orlando LBV best for?

  • Families who want a lively, resort‑style hotel near Disney.
  • Couples who prefer modern rooms and great facilities.
  • Guests who want a big pool area with slides and activities.
  • Anyone who wants great value in a brilliant location.

Rooms

Rooms at Sheraton Orlando LBV are bright, modern and comfortable, with plush beds, good storage and a clean, contemporary design. They’re ideal for families who want a comfortable base after long park days.

Some rooms offer pool views, which are lovely in the evenings.

Dining

The hotel offers several convenient dining options:

  • A casual on‑site restaurant for breakfast and dinner.
  • A poolside bar and grill for snacks and drinks.
  • A grab‑and‑go market for coffee, pastries and essentials.

And with Lake Buena Vista’s restaurants minutes away, you’re never short of choice.

Facilities

  • Large outdoor pool with waterslides.
  • Children’s splash areas.
  • Poolside bar and grill.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site dining and grab‑and‑go options.
  • Disney park shuttles.
  • Modern lobby and lounge spaces.

Location

Sheraton Orlando LBV is perfectly located in the Lake Buena Vista area, close to restaurants, shops and Walt Disney World. It’s ideal for guests who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose Sheraton Orlando LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want a lively, modern resort with great facilities and brilliant value. The pool area is fantastic, the rooms are comfortable, and the location is ideal for Disney and the wider Orlando area. A superb choice for families and couples who want comfort and fun without the premium price tag.
