Disney’s Grand Floridian Resort & Spa

Timeless elegance and luxury on the Magic Kingdom monorail.

Disney’s flagship Deluxe Resort

Disney’s Grand Floridian Resort & Spa is the crown jewel of Walt Disney World hotels. With its Victorian architecture, stunning lobby, world‑class dining and unbeatable location on the monorail, it offers a luxurious, elegant and truly unforgettable Disney experience.

Whether you’re celebrating something special or simply want the very best, the Grand Floridian delivers comfort, beauty and Disney magic in every detail.

Who is the Grand Floridian best for?

  • Couples celebrating a honeymoon, anniversary or special occasion.
  • Families who want a luxurious, premium Disney stay.
  • Guests who want monorail access to Magic Kingdom and EPCOT.
  • Anyone who loves elegant theming and world‑class dining.

Rooms & Suites

Rooms at the Grand Floridian are beautifully designed with elegant Victorian touches and modern comfort. Expect plush beds, large TVs, coffee makers, mini‑fridges and luxurious bathrooms.

Many rooms offer lagoon or theme park views — perfect for watching the Magic Kingdom fireworks from your balcony.

Dining

The Grand Floridian is home to some of the best dining at Walt Disney World:

  • Victoria & Albert’s — AAA Five Diamond Award‑winning fine dining.
  • Narcoossee’s — waterfront dining with stunning views.
  • Cítricos — upscale Mediterranean‑inspired cuisine.
  • Grand Floridian Café — relaxed table‑service meals.
  • Gasparilla Island Grill — quick‑service dining.
  • Garden View Tea Room — afternoon tea in a beautiful setting.

Whether you want elegant fine dining or a relaxed family meal, this resort has something for every occasion.

Facilities

  • Two pools, including the Beach Pool with waterslide.
  • Senses Spa — full‑service luxury spa.
  • Monorail access to Magic Kingdom and EPCOT.
  • Boat transportation across Seven Seas Lagoon.
  • Beautiful grounds, shops and lounges.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

The Grand Floridian is located in the Magic Kingdom Resort Area. You can take the monorail to Magic Kingdom and EPCOT, or enjoy a relaxing boat ride across the lagoon.

Why choose Disney’s Grand Floridian Resort & Spa?

This resort is perfect if you want the very best Disney has to offer. The atmosphere is elegant and welcoming, the dining is exceptional and the location is unbeatable. A wonderful choice for families and couples who want a luxurious, memorable Disney holiday.
