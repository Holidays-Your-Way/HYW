Disney’s Animal Kingdom

Adventure, nature and storytelling brought to life.

A park full of wonder and exploration

Disney’s Animal Kingdom is a breathtaking blend of nature, adventure and immersive storytelling. It’s home to incredible wildlife encounters, stunning landscapes and some of the most impressive attractions Disney has ever created — including the award‑winning Pandora – The World of Avatar.

With thrilling rides, beautiful trails and unforgettable shows, it’s a park that offers something special for every type of guest.

Who is Animal Kingdom best for?

  • Families who love animals and nature.
  • Guests who enjoy immersive lands and stunning theming.
  • Thrill‑seekers looking for world‑class attractions.
  • Anyone who wants a slower, more scenic Disney day.

The Lands of Animal Kingdom

🌿 Oasis

A peaceful, lush entrance area filled with animal habitats and greenery.

🦁 Discovery Island

The heart of the park, home to the Tree of Life and beautiful trails.

🌍 Africa

Explore Harambe Village and embark on an unforgettable safari adventure.

🐅 Asia

Home to thrilling rides, animal encounters and stunning theming.

🦖 DinoLand U.S.A.

Fun, fossils and prehistoric adventures.

🌌 Pandora – The World of Avatar

A breathtaking land with floating mountains, bioluminescent forests and two incredible attractions.

Dining at Animal Kingdom

Animal Kingdom has some of the most unique and flavourful dining options at Walt Disney World:

  • Tiffins — signature dining inspired by global travel.
  • Yak & Yeti — Asian‑inspired dishes in a beautiful setting.
  • Satu’li Canteen — fresh, customisable bowls in Pandora.
  • Flame Tree Barbecue — popular quick‑service barbecue.
  • Harambe Market — African‑inspired street food.

Entertainment & Wildlife

Animal Kingdom offers incredible live entertainment and wildlife experiences throughout the park:

  • Festival of the Lion King — a must‑see Broadway‑style show.
  • Feathered Friends in Flight — an impressive bird show.
  • Street performers and musicians in Africa and Asia.
  • Animal encounters and educational experiences.

Top Tips for Visiting Animal Kingdom

  • Arrive early for Kilimanjaro Safaris — animals are most active in the morning.
  • Use Genie+ for Na’vi River Journey and Expedition Everest.
  • Stay hydrated — this is the hottest park.
  • Take your time exploring the trails — they’re beautiful and often overlooked.
  • Don’t miss the nighttime atmosphere in Pandora.

Why I Recommend Animal Kingdom

Animal Kingdom is perfect for guests who want adventure, nature and incredible storytelling. From the magic of Pandora to the beauty of the safari, it’s a park full of unforgettable moments. It’s relaxing, exciting and wonderfully immersive — a must‑visit for every Disney holiday.
