Hyatt Place Orlando / Lake Buena Vista

Modern, comfortable and great value close to Disney.

A clean, modern hotel with brilliant value and comfort

Hyatt Place LBV is a fantastic choice for guests who want a modern, comfortable hotel close to Disney without paying resort prices. The rooms are bright and contemporary, the atmosphere is calm and relaxed, and the free breakfast adds great value for families and couples alike.

With a lovely pool, on‑site dining and a brilliant location in Lake Buena Vista, it’s a strong all‑round option for guests who want comfort, convenience and great value.

Who is Hyatt Place LBV best for?

  • Couples who want a clean, modern hotel near Disney.
  • Families who appreciate free breakfast and spacious rooms.
  • Guests who prefer a calm, polished atmosphere.
  • Anyone who wants great value in a brilliant location.

Rooms

Rooms at Hyatt Place are modern, bright and comfortable, with plush beds, a sofa‑sleeper, good storage and a clean, contemporary design. They’re ideal for families who want a bit more space than a standard hotel room.

Everything feels fresh, practical and well‑designed for longer stays.

Dining

Hyatt Place offers simple but genuinely useful dining options:

  • Free hot breakfast every morning.
  • 24/7 grab‑and‑go market.
  • Lobby bar with drinks and small plates.
  • On‑site café for snacks and light meals.

And with Lake Buena Vista’s restaurants minutes away, you’re never short of choice.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with plenty of seating.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Free breakfast.
  • 24/7 market and lobby bar.
  • Disney park shuttles.
  • Modern lobby and lounge spaces.

Location

Hyatt Place LBV is perfectly located in Lake Buena Vista, close to restaurants, shops and Walt Disney World. It’s ideal for guests who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose Hyatt Place LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want a clean, modern base with great value and comfort. The free breakfast is a big bonus, the rooms are spacious and contemporary, and the location is ideal for Disney and the wider Orlando area. A brilliant choice for couples and families who want comfort without the premium price tag.
