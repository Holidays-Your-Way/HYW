Disney’s Port Orleans Resort – French Quarter

A charming, intimate Disney Moderate with New Orleans flair.

Disney’s most intimate and atmospheric Moderate Resort

Disney’s Port Orleans Resort – French Quarter is a beautifully themed, boutique‑style resort inspired by the romance and charm of New Orleans’ French Quarter. With cobblestone streets, wrought‑iron balconies, jazz music and colourful Mardi Gras touches, it’s one of the most atmospheric resorts at Walt Disney World.

It’s also the smallest Moderate Resort, which means everything is close by — a huge bonus for families and couples who want convenience and calm.

Who is French Quarter best for?

  • Couples who want a romantic, peaceful Disney resort.
  • Families who prefer smaller, easy‑to‑navigate resorts.
  • Guests who want boat access to Disney Springs.
  • Anyone who loves New Orleans theming and jazz‑inspired charm.

Rooms

Rooms at French Quarter are elegant, comfortable and subtly themed with New Orleans touches. Expect plush beds, a mini‑fridge, coffee maker, large TV and plenty of storage — perfect for couples and families.

Preferred rooms are available for guests who want to be closer to dining, the pool and transportation.

Dining

French Quarter offers:

  • Sassagoula Floatworks & Food Factory — a lively quick‑service restaurant.
  • Scat Cat’s Lounge — home of live jazz and cocktails.
  • Scat Cat’s Café — famous for its Mickey‑shaped beignets.

You can also walk to Port Orleans Riverside for even more dining options.

Facilities

  • Doubloon Lagoon — a fun Mardi Gras‑themed pool with a waterslide.
  • Hot tub and splash area for kids.
  • Boat transportation to Disney Springs.
  • Walking and jogging trails along the river.
  • Playground and arcade.
  • Free Disney transportation.
  • On‑site laundry.

Location

French Quarter is located in the Disney Springs Resort Area. You can travel to Disney Springs by boat, while buses run to all four theme parks and the water parks.

Why choose Disney’s Port Orleans Resort – French Quarter?

This resort is perfect if you want a peaceful, charming Disney stay with beautiful theming and a boutique feel. The small size makes everything incredibly convenient, the boat to Disney Springs is a lovely bonus, and the atmosphere is warm, romantic and full of character. A brilliant choice for couples and families who want comfort, charm and great value.
