Discovery Cove

Discovery Cove

A tropical, all‑inclusive escape in the heart of Orlando

Your calm, friendly guide to the most relaxing day in Florida

A Tropical Escape That Doesn’t Feel Like a Theme Park

Discovery Cove is one of the most unique experiences in Orlando — and it’s nothing like a theme park. It’s calm, tropical, all‑inclusive, and designed to feel like a luxury resort day. No queues, no rushing, no noise… just warm water, white‑sand beaches and a genuinely peaceful atmosphere.

It’s the perfect “reset day” in the middle of a busy holiday, and one of those places families come home raving about. If you want a day that feels like a holiday within your holiday, Discovery Cove is it.

All‑Inclusive From the Moment You Arrive

One of the biggest surprises for guests is just how much is included. Once you’re inside, you can put your wallet away — everything is taken care of.

  • Freshly prepared breakfast
  • Unlimited lunch and snacks
  • Soft drinks, iced coffees and alcoholic drinks
  • Wetsuits, snorkels, towels and lockers
  • All animal habitats and lagoons
  • All‑day access to beaches, rivers and pools

It’s one of the most relaxing days you can have in Orlando — and the all‑inclusive setup makes it feel premium without being complicated.

The Optional Dolphin Swim

The dolphin swim is the experience Discovery Cove is famous for — but it’s completely optional. You can book a package with or without it, and both versions give you the same access to the rest of the park.

If you choose the swim, you’ll meet a dolphin in a small group, learn about their behaviour, and enjoy a gentle, guided swim. It’s calm, respectful and educational — not a performance.

The Grand Reef – Snorkel With Rays & Tropical Fish

The Grand Reef is one of the highlights of Discovery Cove. You’ll snorkel through crystal‑clear water surrounded by thousands of colourful fish and graceful rays gliding beneath you.

There’s also a glass‑fronted wall where you can safely watch sharks on the other side — close enough to be thrilling, but without getting wet.

Wind‑Away River – The Most Relaxing “Lazy River” in Orlando

This isn’t your typical lazy river. It winds through caves, waterfalls, aviaries and warm lagoons, with gentle currents carrying you along. You can float, swim or just drift — it’s completely up to you.

It’s warm, peaceful and absolutely beautiful. Most families say this is the part they didn’t expect to love as much as they did.

Freshwater Oasis – Otters & Marmosets

A shallow, warm lagoon where you can relax while watching playful otters and curious marmosets in their habitats. It’s a lovely, slow‑paced area that’s perfect for younger children or anyone wanting a quieter moment.

Food & Drink – All Included

Discovery Cove’s food offering is surprisingly good — fresh, varied and available all day. Breakfast and lunch are served in the main restaurant, with snacks and drinks available throughout the park.

  • Hot breakfast (eggs, bacon, pastries, fruit)
  • Lunch with salads, burgers, wraps, chicken, fish and vegetarian options
  • Unlimited snacks (pretzels, crisps, fruit, ice creams)
  • Soft drinks, iced coffees and alcoholic drinks included

It’s one of the few places in Orlando where you genuinely don’t need to plan your meals — everything is taken care of.

Planning Tips – The Good Stuff I Tell My Clients

  • Arrive early: breakfast is lovely and the park feels magical first thing.
  • Book early: Discovery Cove limits capacity, so days often sell out.
  • Don’t rush: this is a slow, relaxing day — enjoy it.
  • Use the wetsuits: they help with buoyancy and warmth.
  • Bring nothing: towels, lockers, sunscreen and snorkels are all included.
  • Perfect mid‑holiday: ideal after a few busy park days.

Who Discovery Cove Is Best For

  • Families wanting a calm, premium day
  • Couples looking for something special
  • Anyone who loves animals or snorkelling
  • Guests who want a break from queues and crowds
  • People celebrating birthdays, anniversaries or honeymoons

Discovery Cove is one of those places people underestimate — and then call the best day of their holiday. It’s peaceful, beautiful and completely different from anything else in Orlando.
