TownePlace Suites – Lake Buena Vista

Modern suites, full kitchens and fantastic value near Disney.

A modern, all‑suite hotel perfect for longer stays

TownePlace Suites LBV is one of the best modern suite hotels in the area — bright rooms, full kitchens and a calm, polished atmosphere that works brilliantly for families and couples. It’s ideal for guests who want more space than a standard hotel room and the flexibility to cook simple meals during a 10–14 night stay.

With free breakfast, a lovely pool and a brilliant location close to Disney, it’s a fantastic value‑for‑space option for anyone wanting comfort and convenience.

Who is TownePlace Suites LBV best for?

  • Families who want full kitchens and modern suites.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who need a home‑from‑home.
  • Couples who prefer a calm, contemporary hotel.
  • Anyone who wants great value close to Disney.

Suites

Suites at TownePlace are modern, bright and designed for real comfort. Expect:

  • Studio and one‑bedroom suites.
  • Full kitchens with fridge, stove, microwave and dishwasher.
  • Separate living and sleeping areas (in one‑bedroom suites).
  • Large workspaces and dining areas.
  • Clean, contemporary décor.

It’s one of the best modern suite options in Lake Buena Vista.

Dining

TownePlace Suites offers simple but genuinely useful dining options:

  • Free hot breakfast every morning.
  • Full kitchens in every suite for easy meals.
  • 24/7 market for snacks and essentials.

And with Lake Buena Vista’s restaurants minutes away, you’re never short of choice.

Facilities

Location

TownePlace Suites is perfectly located in Lake Buena Vista, close to restaurants, shops and Walt Disney World. It’s quiet, convenient and ideal for guests who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose TownePlace Suites LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want space, comfort and flexibility. The suites are modern and well‑designed, the full kitchens make life easier, and the free breakfast adds great value. A superb choice for families and couples who want a relaxed, contemporary base near Disney without the premium price tag.
