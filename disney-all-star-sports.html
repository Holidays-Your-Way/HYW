Disney’s All‑Star Sports Resort

A fun, energetic Disney Value Resort with bold theming.

A lively, sports‑themed Disney Value Resort

Disney’s All‑Star Sports Resort celebrates the world of American sports with bright, oversized icons and a fun, energetic atmosphere. It’s a brilliant choice for families who want great value while staying in the Disney bubble, with colourful theming that kids absolutely love.

With footballs, surfboards, tennis rackets and baseball bats towering over the courtyards, it’s one of the most playful and upbeat Disney hotels.

Who is All‑Star Sports best for?

  • Families who want fun, bold Disney theming.
  • Guests looking for great value while staying on‑site.
  • Kids who love sports‑inspired décor.
  • Anyone who wants simple, comfortable accommodation with Disney perks.

Rooms

Rooms at All‑Star Sports are bright, modern and cleverly designed, with a queen bed and a pull‑down Murphy bed that doubles as a table during the day. They’re compact but comfortable, with everything you need for a great stay.

Expect a mini‑fridge, coffee maker, large TV and plenty of storage — ideal for families and couples.

Dining

The resort offers:

  • End Zone Food Court — a large, varied quick‑service restaurant.
  • Poolside bar for drinks and snacks.
  • Grab‑and‑go options for busy park mornings.

You can also walk to All‑Star Music and All‑Star Movies for even more dining choices.

Facilities

  • Surfboard Bay Pool (main pool).
  • Grand Slam Pool (baseball‑themed).
  • Playground and arcade.
  • Jogging trail.
  • Free Disney transportation.
  • On‑site laundry.

Location

All‑Star Sports is located in the Animal Kingdom Resort Area of Walt Disney World. Buses run to all four theme parks, Disney Springs and the water parks.

Why choose Disney’s All‑Star Sports Resort?

This resort is perfect for families who want fun, bold theming at great value. The atmosphere is lively and playful, the rooms are modern and comfortable, and staying on‑site gives you all the Disney perks. A brilliant choice for families who want to stay in the magic without overspending.
