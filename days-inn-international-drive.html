Days Inn International Drive

A simple, budget‑friendly base close to Universal.

A no‑frills hotel in a great location

Days Inn International Drive is a straightforward, budget hotel ideal for guests who want to stay close to Universal Orlando without spending much. It’s simple, clean and practical — perfect for families and couples who plan to spend most of their time in the parks and just need a comfortable place to sleep.

The location is its biggest strength, with restaurants, shops and attractions all within easy reach.

Who is Days Inn I‑Drive best for?

  • Families on a tight budget who want to be close to Universal.
  • Guests who want simple, clean accommodation without the extras.
  • Couples looking for a low‑cost base near I‑Drive attractions.
  • Anyone who prioritises price and location over facilities.

Rooms

Rooms are basic but comfortable, offering everything you need for a good night’s sleep. Expect air‑conditioning, a fridge, microwave and practical storage — ideal for longer stays.

They’re not modern or luxurious, but they’re clean, functional and great value for the price.

Dining

Days Inn offers simple on‑site dining options:

  • Grab‑and‑go breakfast options.
  • Vending machines for snacks and drinks.

And with International Drive right outside, you’re surrounded by restaurants and cafés.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool.
  • Free parking.
  • Shuttle options to Universal.
  • 24‑hour front desk.
  • Guest laundry.

Location

Days Inn is located at the top of International Drive, close to Universal Orlando, ICON Park, restaurants and shops. It’s ideal for guests who want convenience and value.

Why choose Days Inn I‑Drive?

This hotel is perfect if you want a clean, simple and affordable base close to Universal. It’s not fancy, but it’s reliable, well‑located and great value for families and couples who want to make the most of their time in the parks.
