Hilton Orlando Lake Buena Vista

Polished, modern and steps away from Disney Springs.

A polished Disney Springs resort with unbeatable convenience

Hilton Orlando Lake Buena Vista is an official Disney Springs Resort Area hotel, offering a clean, modern and reliable stay just moments from world‑class dining, shopping and entertainment. It’s the kind of hotel that quietly over‑delivers — great facilities, comfortable rooms and a location that’s hard to beat.

With Disney perks, multiple dining options and two lovely pools, it’s a brilliant choice for families and couples who want comfort and convenience without staying on‑site.

Who is Hilton LBV best for?

  • Couples who want a polished, modern hotel near Disney Springs.
  • Families who prefer clean, contemporary rooms and great facilities.
  • Guests who want Disney perks without Disney pricing.
  • Anyone who values comfort, reliability and location.

Rooms

Rooms at Hilton LBV are bright, modern and comfortable, with plush beds, good storage and a clean, contemporary design. Everything feels fresh and well‑maintained — ideal for unwinding after long park days.

Some rooms offer Disney Springs views, which are lovely in the evenings.

Dining

The hotel offers several excellent dining options:

  • A casual on‑site restaurant for breakfast and dinner.
  • A lobby bar for cocktails and small plates.
  • A grab‑and‑go market for snacks, pastries and coffee.
  • Poolside food and drink service.

And with Disney Springs just steps away, you’ve got endless restaurants within a short walk.

Facilities

  • Two outdoor pools, including a quiet pool.
  • Children’s splash area.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site dining and grab‑and‑go options.
  • Disney park shuttles.
  • Walking distance to Disney Springs.

Location

Hilton LBV is perfectly located in the Disney Springs area — you can walk across the pedestrian bridge and be in the heart of the action within minutes. It’s ideal for guests who want easy access to Disney dining, shopping and entertainment.

Why choose Hilton LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want a polished, comfortable base with unbeatable access to Disney Springs. The rooms are modern, the facilities are excellent, and the whole place feels reliable and well‑run. A fantastic choice for couples and families who want convenience and comfort without the premium price tag.
