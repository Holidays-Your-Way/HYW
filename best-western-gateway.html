Best Western Gateway Hotel

Comfortable, reliable and great value — just minutes from Universal.

A friendly, great‑value hotel close to the Universal action

Best Western Gateway is one of those hotels that keeps things simple — clean rooms, friendly staff and a great location just a few minutes from Universal Orlando. It’s not themed or flashy, but it’s comfortable, reliable and brilliant value for families who want to stay close to the parks without paying on‑site prices.

The hotel has everything you need for an easy stay: a pool, a restaurant, a bar and free shuttles to the parks. It’s a great base if you’re planning full park days and want somewhere comfortable to come back to.

If you want affordability, convenience and a straightforward stay near Universal, this is a solid choice.

Who is Best Western Gateway best for?

  • Families who want great value close to Universal.
  • Guests who prefer simple, comfortable hotels without the extras.
  • Travellers who plan to spend most of their time in the parks.
  • Anyone looking for a budget‑friendly base with free shuttles.

Rooms

Rooms are clean, comfortable and practical — exactly what you need for a park‑focused holiday. Expect comfy beds, good showers and plenty of space for families. It’s not luxury, but it’s reliable and well‑kept.

Dining

The on‑site restaurant offers easy breakfasts and dinners, and there’s a bar for evening drinks. You’re also close to loads of restaurants on International Drive, so you’ll never run out of options.

Pool & perks

  • Outdoor pool with plenty of seating.
  • Free shuttle buses to Universal and other attractions.
  • Great value for the location.
  • Friendly staff and a relaxed atmosphere.
  • Close to restaurants, shops and International Drive.

Why choose Best Western Gateway?

Best Western Gateway is ideal if you want a comfortable, great‑value hotel close to Universal without paying on‑site prices. It’s simple, friendly and reliable — a solid base for families who plan to spend most of their time in the parks. It does exactly what you need it to do, and it does it well.
