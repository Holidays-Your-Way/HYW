Start Your Universal Adventure Here

Universal Orlando is one of my favourite places in the world. It’s full of energy, imagination and those “we’ll talk about this for years” moments. I’ve been visiting Orlando for over 40 years, and I’ve been exploring Universal since it first opened its gates in June 1990.

I’ve watched it grow from a single park into a full resort with four theme parks, brilliant hotels, fantastic dining and so much to explore. This page is your calm, clear starting point — a hub that brings everything together and helps you find the guides that fit your family, your pace and your style.

Whether you’re travelling with little ones, teens, adults, first‑timers or a mix of everything, you don’t need to know it all. You just need someone who does. That’s where I come in.
