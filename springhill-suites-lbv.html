SpringHill Suites – Lake Buena Vista

Modern suites, free breakfast and brilliant value near Disney.

A modern, spacious all‑suite hotel perfect for families

SpringHill Suites LBV is a fantastic choice for guests who want modern, spacious accommodation close to Disney without paying resort prices. Every room is a studio‑style suite, giving families more space to spread out, and the free breakfast adds great value over a longer stay.

With a lovely pool, contemporary design and a calm, polished atmosphere, it’s a brilliant all‑round option for families and couples who want comfort and convenience.

Who is SpringHill Suites LBV best for?

  • Families who want more space than a standard hotel room.
  • Couples who prefer a clean, modern hotel near Disney.
  • Guests who appreciate free breakfast and contemporary design.
  • Anyone who wants great value in a brilliant location.

Suites

Suites at SpringHill are bright, modern and designed for comfort. Expect:

  • Studio‑style suites with open‑plan layouts.
  • Separate living and sleeping areas.
  • Mini‑fridge, microwave and coffee maker.
  • Sofa‑sleeper for families.
  • Clean, contemporary décor.

It’s a great step up from a standard hotel room without jumping into full apartment‑style living.

Dining

SpringHill Suites offers simple but genuinely useful dining options:

  • Free hot breakfast every morning.
  • 24/7 market for snacks and essentials.
  • Lobby lounge for drinks and light bites.

And with Lake Buena Vista’s restaurants minutes away, you’re never short of choice.

Facilities

Location

SpringHill Suites LBV is perfectly located in Lake Buena Vista, close to restaurants, shops and Walt Disney World. It’s ideal for guests who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose SpringHill Suites LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want a clean, modern base with great value and comfort. The suites are spacious, the free breakfast is a big bonus, and the location is ideal for Disney and the wider Orlando area. A brilliant choice for families and couples who want comfort without the premium price tag.
