Caribe Royale Orlando

Elegant, spacious and full of resort‑style luxury.

A premium all‑suite resort with a stunning pool and upscale feel

Caribe Royale is one of Orlando’s most impressive off‑site resorts — elegant, spacious and beautifully maintained, with a premium atmosphere that still feels relaxed and welcoming. The suites are huge, the pool area is gorgeous, and the whole resort has a polished, grown‑up feel that works brilliantly for both families and couples.

With multiple dining options, a waterfall pool, a waterslide, a spa and a fantastic location close to Disney, it’s a superb choice if you want a resort that feels special without stepping into luxury‑hotel pricing.

Who is Caribe Royale best for?

  • Families who want large suites and a premium resort feel.
  • Couples looking for an elegant, comfortable base near Disney.
  • Guests who enjoy beautiful pool areas and great facilities.
  • Anyone who wants a polished, upscale resort without luxury prices.

Suites & Villas

Accommodation at Caribe Royale is a real highlight — spacious, elegant and designed for comfort. Expect:

  • Large one‑bedroom suites with separate living areas.
  • Two‑bedroom villas with full kitchens and extra space.
  • Modern, stylish décor throughout.
  • Luxurious bathrooms and plush bedding.

Everything feels premium, fresh and ideal for longer stays.

Dining

Caribe Royale has an excellent range of dining options:

  • A fine‑dining steakhouse for special evenings.
  • A casual café and grill for relaxed meals.
  • A poolside bar for drinks and snacks.
  • A marketplace for coffee, pastries and essentials.

You’ve also got the Lake Buena Vista area nearby for even more restaurants.

Facilities

  • Stunning waterfall pool with waterslide.
  • Quiet pool for relaxing.
  • Full‑service spa.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Multiple dining options and bars.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Beautifully landscaped grounds.

Location

Caribe Royale is perfectly located in the Lake Buena Vista area, just minutes from Walt Disney World. It’s quiet, convenient and ideal for guests who want easy access to the parks while enjoying a premium resort atmosphere.

Why I choose Caribe Royale?

This resort is perfect if you want a polished, upscale base with beautiful facilities and spacious suites. The pool area is stunning, the atmosphere is calm and elegant, and the whole place feels special without being formal. A superb choice for families and couples who want something a little more premium near Disney.
