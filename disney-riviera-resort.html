Disney’s Riviera Resort

Modern European elegance with Skyliner access.

A stylish, modern Disney Deluxe Villa Resort

Disney’s Riviera Resort blends the charm of the European Riviera with modern Disney luxury. Inspired by Walt Disney’s travels through France and Italy, it offers elegant design, beautiful artwork and a relaxed, upscale atmosphere.

With Skyliner access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios, plus stunning dining and spacious villa options, it’s a fantastic choice for families and couples who want comfort, style and convenience.

Who is Riviera Resort best for?

  • Couples who want a romantic, elegant Disney stay.
  • Families who want spacious villas with kitchen facilities.
  • Guests who want quick Skyliner access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios.
  • Anyone who loves European‑inspired design and modern luxury.

Rooms & Villas

Riviera offers a range of beautifully designed accommodation, from cosy Tower Studios for two to spacious multi‑bedroom villas for larger families. Expect elegant décor, modern amenities and plenty of comfort.

  • Tower Studios — perfect for couples.
  • Deluxe Studios — sleep up to 5 with a kitchenette.
  • 1‑, 2‑ and 3‑Bedroom Villas — full kitchens, living areas and washer/dryer.

Many rooms offer beautiful views of the resort, lake or Skyliner.

Dining

Riviera is home to some of the most popular dining options at Walt Disney World:

  • Topolino’s Terrace — rooftop dining with character breakfast and signature dinner.
  • Primo Piatto — excellent quick‑service meals.
  • Le Petit Café — pastries, coffee and evening cocktails.
  • Bar Riva — relaxed poolside dining.

Whether you want a romantic dinner or a quick bite, the dining here is exceptional.

Facilities

  • Two pools, including a family pool with slide.
  • Skyliner access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios.
  • Beautiful walking paths and gardens.
  • Fitness centre and recreation.
  • Shops, lounges and cultural activities.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

Riviera Resort is located in the EPCOT Resort Area. The Skyliner provides quick access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios, while buses run to Magic Kingdom, Animal Kingdom and Disney Springs.

Why choose Disney’s Riviera Resort?

This resort is perfect if you want a modern, elegant Disney stay with fantastic dining and easy access to the parks. The villas are ideal for families, the atmosphere is relaxed and stylish, and the Skyliner makes getting around incredibly easy. A brilliant choice for guests who want luxury, comfort and European charm.
