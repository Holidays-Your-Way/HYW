Wyndham Lake Buena Vista

The closest hotel to Disney Springs — modern, comfortable and full of Disney perks.

A modern, polished Disney Springs resort with unbeatable convenience

Wyndham Lake Buena Vista is the **official Disney Springs Resort** that sits closest to the action — you can walk across the pedestrian bridge and be in Disney Springs in minutes. The hotel feels modern, bright and comfortable, with great facilities and a relaxed, family‑friendly atmosphere.

With Disney perks, a lovely pool area and a location that’s hard to beat, it’s a brilliant choice for families and couples who want convenience and comfort without staying on‑site.

Who is Wyndham LBV best for?

  • Families who want to walk to Disney Springs.
  • Couples who prefer a modern, polished hotel.
  • Guests who want Disney perks without Disney pricing.
  • Anyone who values convenience and comfort.

Rooms

Rooms at Wyndham LBV are modern, bright and comfortable, with plush beds, good storage and a clean, contemporary design. Some rooms offer Disney Springs views, which are lovely in the evenings.

They’re ideal for guests who want a stylish, comfortable base close to the action.

Dining

The hotel offers several convenient dining options:

  • A casual on‑site restaurant for breakfast and dinner.
  • A poolside bar for drinks and snacks.
  • A grab‑and‑go market for coffee and essentials.

And of course, Disney Springs is right on your doorstep with endless dining choices.

Facilities

  • Large pool with waterfall features.
  • Children’s splash area.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site dining and grab‑and‑go options.
  • Disney park shuttles.
  • Walking distance to Disney Springs.

Location

Wyndham LBV is the closest hotel to Disney Springs — you can walk there in minutes. It’s ideal for guests who want easy access to Disney dining, shopping and entertainment without relying on transport.

Why choose Wyndham LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want a modern, comfortable base with unbeatable access to Disney Springs. The facilities are great, the atmosphere is relaxed, and the location is one of the best in the area. A fantastic choice for families and couples who want convenience and comfort.
