Disney’s Copper Creek Villas & Cabins

Modern luxury and rustic charm at Wilderness Lodge.

A stunning Deluxe Villa Resort inspired by the Pacific Northwest

Disney’s Copper Creek Villas & Cabins blend modern luxury with the warm, rustic charm of Wilderness Lodge. Located within the main lodge building and along the waterfront, these villas offer beautiful design, spacious layouts and a peaceful, nature‑inspired atmosphere.

The resort also includes Boulder Ridge Villas, a separate building with its own cosy, rustic theming — perfect for guests who want a quieter, more traditional lodge feel.

Who are Copper Creek & Boulder Ridge best for?

  • Families who want spacious villas with full kitchens.
  • Couples looking for a romantic, nature‑inspired retreat.
  • Guests who love Wilderness Lodge but want villa amenities.
  • Anyone who wants boat access to Magic Kingdom.

Copper Creek Villas & Cabins

Copper Creek is the newer, more modern villa section of Wilderness Lodge. Located inside the main lodge and along the lakeshore, it offers beautifully designed accommodation with a sleek, contemporary take on rustic theming.

  • Deluxe Studios, 1‑, 2‑ and 3‑Bedroom Villas.
  • Stunning waterfront Cascade Cabins with private hot tubs.
  • Closest to the main lobby, dining and Copper Creek Springs Pool.
  • Ideal for guests who want modern luxury with lodge theming.

Boulder Ridge Villas

Boulder Ridge is a separate building with a warm, cosy and more traditional lodge feel. It’s quieter and more secluded, perfect for guests who want a peaceful retreat.

  • Deluxe Studios and multi‑bedroom villas.
  • Rustic, classic Wilderness Lodge theming.
  • Close to the Boulder Ridge Cove Pool.
  • Ideal for guests who prefer a quieter, more intimate atmosphere.

Studios, Villas & Cabins

Both Copper Creek and Boulder Ridge offer a range of spacious accommodation options:

  • Deluxe Studios — sleep up to 4–5 with a kitchenette.
  • 1‑Bedroom Villas — full kitchen, living area and washer/dryer.
  • 2‑Bedroom Villas — ideal for larger families or groups.
  • 3‑Bedroom Grand Villas — luxurious and incredibly spacious.
  • Waterfront Cascade Cabins — private hot tub, fireplace and stunning views.

The décor varies between modern (Copper Creek) and rustic (Boulder Ridge), giving guests two distinct styles to choose from.

Dining

Guests staying at Copper Creek and Boulder Ridge have access to all Wilderness Lodge dining:

  • Whispering Canyon Café — fun, family‑style dining.
  • Story Book Dining at Artist Point — character dining with Snow White.
  • Geyser Point Bar & Grill — lakeside dining with beautiful views.
  • Roaring Fork — quick‑service meals and snacks.

Whether you want a relaxed lakeside meal or a fun character experience, the dining here is excellent.

Facilities

  • Two pools — Copper Creek Springs and Boulder Ridge Cove.
  • Boat transportation to Magic Kingdom.
  • Walking trails and beautiful outdoor spaces.
  • Fitness centre, hot tubs and recreation.
  • Campfire activities and movies under the stars.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

Copper Creek and Boulder Ridge are located in the Magic Kingdom Resort Area. You can take a relaxing boat ride to Magic Kingdom, while buses run to EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom and Disney Springs.

Why choose Copper Creek Villas & Cabins?

This resort is perfect if you want a luxurious, nature‑inspired Disney stay with beautiful theming and fantastic villa options. Copper Creek offers modern comfort, Boulder Ridge offers cosy charm, and the boat to Magic Kingdom is a lovely bonus. A brilliant choice for families and couples who want space, atmosphere and Disney magic.
