Rosen Inn International

Simple, reliable and great value — right on International Drive.

A budget‑friendly hotel in a brilliant, walkable location

Rosen Inn International is one of the most popular value hotels on International Drive — and for good reason. It’s clean, comfortable and incredibly convenient, with restaurants, shops and attractions all within easy walking distance. It’s not modern or themed, but it’s friendly, reliable and excellent for families who want to keep costs down without sacrificing location.

The hotel has two pools, a restaurant, a small shop and free shuttles to the major theme parks. It’s a great base if you’re planning full park days and want somewhere straightforward and comfortable to come back to.

Who is Rosen Inn International best for?

  • Families who want a budget‑friendly stay close to everything.
  • Guests who like having restaurants and shops within walking distance.
  • Travellers who plan to spend most of their time in the parks.
  • Anyone who wants a simple, comfortable base without the extras.

Rooms

Rooms are basic but clean and comfortable, with everything you need for a practical stay — comfy beds, a fridge, microwave, coffee maker and enough space for families. They’re not modern, but they’re well‑maintained and ideal for park‑focused holidays.

Dining

The on‑site restaurant offers easy buffet breakfasts and simple evening meals. There’s also a small shop for snacks and essentials. And because you’re right on International Drive, you’ve got endless dining options just steps away.

Facilities

  • Two outdoor pools.
  • On‑site restaurant and small convenience store.
  • Free shuttle buses to major theme parks.
  • Children’s play area.
  • Secure gated parking.

Location

The location is one of the biggest reasons people choose this hotel. You’re right on International Drive, close to ICON Park, restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal is a short drive or shuttle ride away, making it a brilliant base for families who want convenience without the higher price tag.

Why choose Rosen Inn International?

This hotel is ideal if you want a clean, comfortable and affordable base in a fantastic location. It’s simple, friendly and reliable — perfect for families who plan to spend most of their time in the parks or exploring International Drive. It offers great value and does exactly what you need it to do.
