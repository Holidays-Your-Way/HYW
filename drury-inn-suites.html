Drury Inn & Suites Orlando

Brilliant value, fantastic freebies and a great I‑Drive location.

A family favourite with unbeatable value and warm hospitality

Drury Inn & Suites is one of the best value hotels in Orlando — not because it’s the cheapest, but because of everything that’s included. You get a hot breakfast every morning, evening snacks and drinks, soft drinks and popcorn throughout the day, and genuinely friendly service that keeps families coming back year after year.

The hotel is clean, modern and comfortable, with a relaxed atmosphere and great facilities. It’s perfectly placed near ICON Park and the Universal end of International Drive, making it a brilliant base for park days and downtime alike.

Who is Drury Inn & Suites best for?

  • Families who want brilliant value with lots of extras included.
  • Guests who appreciate warm, friendly service.
  • Travellers who want a clean, modern hotel in a great location.
  • Anyone who loves the idea of free breakfast and evening snacks.

Rooms & Suites

Rooms at Drury are modern, clean and comfortable, with plush beds, good storage and everything you need for a relaxing stay. Suites offer extra space with separate living areas — ideal for families who want room to spread out.

They’re not luxury, but they’re well‑kept, modern and great for longer stays.

Dining

Drury’s biggest selling point is the value:

  • Free hot breakfast every morning.
  • Free evening snacks (often enough to count as a light dinner).
  • Free soft drinks and popcorn throughout the day.
  • A casual on‑site dining area and nearby restaurants on I‑Drive.

It’s one of the few hotels where you can genuinely save money on food without compromising on quality.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool and indoor/outdoor whirlpool.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Free breakfast, evening snacks and daily refreshments.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Modern lobby and public spaces.
  • Suites available for larger families.

Location

Drury Inn & Suites is perfectly placed near ICON Park and the Universal end of International Drive. You’re close to restaurants, shops and attractions, with easy access to Universal and a short drive to SeaWorld and Disney.

Why I choose Drury Inn & Suites?

This hotel is perfect if you want brilliant value, warm hospitality and lots of extras that genuinely make a difference. The free breakfast and evening snacks are a huge bonus for families, and the whole place feels clean, modern and welcoming. A fantastic all‑rounder for Orlando holidays.
