Sheraton Vistana Resort

Spacious villas, beautiful lakes and a true resort‑neighbourhood feel.

A huge, beautifully landscaped villa resort perfect for families

Sheraton Vistana Resort is one of Orlando’s most established and loved villa resorts — a sprawling, beautifully maintained property with lakes, fountains, palm‑lined walkways and a calm, premium atmosphere. It feels like a peaceful neighbourhood rather than a hotel, making it ideal for families who want space, comfort and a proper “home‑from‑home” base close to Disney.

With seven pools, multiple dining options, sports courts, activities and full villas with kitchens and washer/dryers, it’s a brilliant choice for longer stays or anyone who wants resort facilities without the crowds.

Who is Sheraton Vistana Resort best for?

  • Families who want spacious villas with full kitchens.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who want a premium, homely base.
  • Travellers who enjoy resort facilities and a neighbourhood feel.
  • Anyone who wants a calm, beautifully kept resort near Disney.

Villas

Villas at Vistana Resort are spacious, comfortable and designed for real family living. Expect:

  • 1‑ and 2‑bedroom villas.
  • Full kitchens with fridge, oven, microwave and dishwasher.
  • Separate living and dining areas.
  • Washer/dryer in every villa.
  • Balconies or patios overlooking lakes or gardens.

Everything feels warm, practical and perfect for longer stays.

Dining

The resort offers several easy dining options:

  • On‑site restaurants and bars.
  • Poolside food and drink service in some areas.
  • Marketplace for snacks, coffee and essentials.
  • Full kitchens in every villa for flexible meals.

And with the Lake Buena Vista area nearby, you’ve got endless restaurants within a short drive.

Facilities

  • Seven outdoor pools, each with its own style and atmosphere.
  • Children’s splash areas and water features.
  • Fitness centres.
  • Sports courts and activity areas.
  • Walking paths around lakes and fountains.
  • Marketplace and on‑site dining.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Washer/dryer in every villa.

Location

Sheraton Vistana Resort is perfectly located in the Lake Buena Vista area, just minutes from Walt Disney World. It’s quiet, convenient and ideal for families who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose Sheraton Vistana Resort?

This resort is perfect if you want space, comfort and a premium, peaceful atmosphere in a brilliant location. The villas are fantastic for families, the pools and grounds are beautiful, and the whole place feels safe, relaxed and incredibly well maintained. A superb choice for longer stays or anyone who wants a resort that genuinely feels like home.
