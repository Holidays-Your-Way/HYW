Disney’s Beach Club Resort

A relaxed, coastal‑themed Deluxe Resort steps from EPCOT.

A bright, breezy Disney Deluxe Resort

Disney’s Beach Club Resort blends New England coastal charm with a relaxed, elegant atmosphere. With pastel colours, beautiful landscaping and a light, airy feel, it’s perfect for families and couples who want a premium Disney stay without feeling formal.

Its location is unbeatable — you can walk to EPCOT in minutes, stroll to Disney’s BoardWalk and even reach Hollywood Studios by boat or on foot.

Who is Beach Club best for?

  • Families who want the best pool at Walt Disney World.
  • Guests who want to walk to EPCOT and the BoardWalk.
  • Couples looking for a relaxed, elegant Deluxe Resort.
  • Anyone who loves coastal theming and beautiful surroundings.

Rooms & Villas

Rooms at Beach Club are bright, airy and beautifully themed with soft coastal colours. Expect plush beds, large TVs, coffee makers, mini‑fridges and plenty of storage.

The resort also offers Disney Vacation Club villas for guests who want extra space and kitchen facilities.

Dining

Beach Club has some fantastic dining options, including:

  • Beaches & Cream Soda Shop — home of the famous Kitchen Sink sundae.
  • Cape May Café — character breakfast and seafood dinner buffet.
  • Beach Club Marketplace — quick‑service meals and snacks.
  • Hurricane Hanna’s — poolside drinks and bites.

You’re also just steps away from the dining at Yacht Club and the BoardWalk.

Facilities

  • Stormalong Bay — a 3‑acre water playground with a lazy river and sand‑bottom pool.
  • Quiet leisure pools.
  • Boat transportation to Hollywood Studios.
  • Walking access to EPCOT and the BoardWalk.
  • Fitness centre, shops and recreation.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

Beach Club is located in the EPCOT Resort Area. You can walk to EPCOT in minutes, take a boat to Hollywood Studios or enjoy a short stroll to Disney’s BoardWalk.

Why choose Disney’s Beach Club Resort?

This resort is perfect if you want a relaxed, elegant Deluxe stay with unbeatable access to EPCOT and the BoardWalk. Stormalong Bay is a huge highlight for families, and the coastal theming creates a bright, welcoming atmosphere. A brilliant choice for guests who want comfort, convenience and a touch of seaside charm.
