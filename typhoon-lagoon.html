Disney’s Typhoon Lagoon

A tropical paradise with waves, slides and sandy beaches.

Disney’s tropical water adventure

Typhoon Lagoon is Disney’s tropical‑themed water park, filled with palm trees, sandy beaches and one of the largest wave pools in the world. It’s perfect for families, thrill‑seekers and anyone who wants a relaxing day in the sun with a touch of Disney magic.

With slides, lazy rivers, family attractions and great dining, it’s a brilliant break from the theme parks.

Who is Typhoon Lagoon best for?

  • Families who want a fun, relaxing water park day.
  • Thrill‑seekers who love big slides and waves.
  • Guests who enjoy tropical theming and beach vibes.
  • Anyone looking for a break from the theme parks.

Top Attractions

  • Typhoon Lagoon Surf Pool — massive waves every 90 seconds.
  • Crush ’n’ Gusher — a water coaster with thrilling drops.
  • Miss Adventure Falls — a family raft ride.
  • Castaway Creek — a relaxing lazy river.
  • Humunga Kowabunga — high‑speed body slides.
  • Storm Slides — twisting, turning fun.
  • Ketchakiddee Creek — a dedicated kids’ play area.

Dining at Typhoon Lagoon

Relaxed, beach‑style dining options include:

  • Leaning Palms — burgers, rice bowls and family favourites.
  • Typhoon Tilly’s — seafood baskets and sandwiches.
  • Snack Shack — refreshing drinks and treats.
  • Happy Landings Ice Cream — home of the famous Sand Pail Sundae.

Top Tips for Visiting Typhoon Lagoon

  • Arrive early for the best sunbed spots.
  • Bring water shoes — the ground gets hot.
  • Use a waterproof pouch for phones and valuables.
  • Take breaks — the wave pool is powerful.
  • Consider a cabana or umbrella rental for shade.

Why I Recommend Typhoon Lagoon

Typhoon Lagoon is perfect for families and couples who want a fun, relaxing day in a beautiful tropical setting. With great slides, a huge wave pool and plenty of places to unwind, it’s a brilliant addition to any Disney holiday.
