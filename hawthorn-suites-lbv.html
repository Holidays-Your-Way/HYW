Hawthorn Suites – Lake Buena Vista

Spacious suites, full kitchens and fantastic value near Disney.

A relaxed, all‑suite hotel perfect for families

Hawthorn Suites LBV is a brilliant value‑for‑money option in the Lake Buena Vista area — spacious suites, full kitchens and a friendly, relaxed atmosphere that works perfectly for families. It’s ideal for guests who want more space than a standard hotel room and the flexibility to cook simple meals during a longer stay.

With free breakfast, a lovely pool and a quiet, village‑style layout, it’s a great choice for families and couples who want comfort and convenience close to Disney.

Who is Hawthorn Suites LBV best for?

  • Families who want separate living and sleeping areas.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who need a home‑from‑home.
  • Travellers who prefer full kitchens for easy meals.
  • Anyone who wants great value close to Disney.

Suites

Suites at Hawthorn are spacious, comfortable and designed for longer stays. Expect:

  • One‑bedroom suites with separate living areas.
  • Full kitchens with fridge, oven, microwave and dishwasher.
  • Dining table and workspace.
  • Plenty of storage for families.
  • Bright, homely décor.

It’s one of the best value‑for‑space options in Lake Buena Vista.

Dining

  • BBQ and outdoor dining areas.
  • Grab‑and‑go market for essentials.

And with Lake Buena Vista’s restaurants minutes away, you’re never short of choice.

Location

Hawthorn Suites is perfectly located in Lake Buena Vista, close to restaurants, shops and Walt Disney World. It’s quiet, convenient and ideal for families who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose Hawthorn Suites LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want space, comfort and great value. The suites are ideal for longer stays, the kitchens make life easier, and the whole resort feels calm, friendly and homely. A brilliant choice for families and couples who want a relaxed base near Disney without the premium price tag.
