DoubleTree by Hilton at Universal

Modern, comfortable and perfectly placed for Universal Orlando.

A polished, comfortable hotel just steps from Universal

DoubleTree by Hilton at Universal is one of the best off‑site options if you want comfort, convenience and a more modern feel. It’s directly across from Universal Orlando, making it one of the closest hotels you can stay in without being on‑site — and that alone makes it incredibly popular with families and couples.

The hotel has a bright, contemporary style, great facilities and the warm, friendly service Hilton is known for. And yes — you get the famous warm cookie at check‑in, which always starts the holiday on a good note.

Who is DoubleTree at Universal best for?

  • Families who want a modern, comfortable hotel close to Universal.
  • Couples who prefer a more polished, grown‑up atmosphere.
  • Guests who want great facilities without on‑site prices.
  • Anyone who values location — you can walk to Universal from here.

Rooms

Rooms are modern, spacious and comfortable, with everything you’d expect from a Hilton stay — plush beds, large bathrooms, good storage and plenty of space for families. Many rooms offer lovely views of the Universal area, especially at night.

It’s a great step up from the budget hotels on International Drive, without jumping to on‑site pricing.

Dining

The hotel has a full‑service restaurant, a lobby bar and a grab‑and‑go market for quick snacks and drinks. Breakfast is popular with families, and there are plenty of restaurants within walking distance if you want more choice.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with plenty of seating.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site restaurant, bar and grab‑and‑go market.
  • Walking distance to Universal Orlando.
  • Shuttle transport available.

Location

The location is one of the biggest reasons people choose this hotel. You can walk to Universal Orlando, and you’re just minutes from International Drive, the outlets and loads of dining options. It’s incredibly convenient without being on‑site.

Why choose DoubleTree at Universal?

This hotel is perfect if you want a modern, comfortable stay right next to Universal without paying on‑site prices. It’s polished, friendly and incredibly convenient — a great choice for families and couples who want comfort and location in one place.
