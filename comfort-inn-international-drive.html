Comfort Inn International Drive

Clean, reliable and great value in the heart of I‑Drive.

A dependable budget hotel in a brilliant location

Comfort Inn International Drive is a great choice for guests who want a clean, comfortable and affordable base right in the middle of Orlando’s most popular area. It’s simple, reliable and ideal for families and couples who plan to spend most of their time exploring the parks and attractions.

With free breakfast, a pool and a location surrounded by restaurants and shops, it offers excellent value for money.

Who is Comfort Inn I‑Drive best for?

  • Families who want a budget‑friendly hotel in a central location.
  • Guests who want clean, reliable accommodation without the extras.
  • Couples looking for a simple base close to I‑Drive attractions.
  • Anyone who wants great value and convenience.

Rooms

Rooms are clean, comfortable and practical, with everything you need for a relaxing stay. Expect comfortable beds, air‑conditioning, a fridge, microwave and coffee maker — ideal for families and longer stays.

They’re basic but well‑maintained, offering great value for the location.

Dining

Comfort Inn offers simple on‑site dining options:

  • Complimentary breakfast.
  • Grab‑and‑go snacks in the lobby.

And with International Drive outside your door, you’ve got endless restaurants within walking distance.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool.
  • Free breakfast.
  • Free parking.
  • Shuttle options to the parks.
  • 24‑hour front desk.
  • Guest laundry.

Location

Comfort Inn is perfectly located on International Drive, close to ICON Park, restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal and SeaWorld are both a short drive away, making it ideal for park‑focused holidays.

Why choose Comfort Inn I‑Drive?

This hotel is perfect if you want a clean, simple and affordable base in a brilliant location. The complimentary breakfast is a great perk, the rooms are comfortable, and the location is ideal for exploring Orlando. A solid choice for budget‑conscious families and couples.
