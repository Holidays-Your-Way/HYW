Dockside Inn & Suites

A relaxed, great‑value hotel with big family suites and a friendly, easygoing vibe.

Universal’s Endless Summer Resort – Dockside Inn & Suites

Dockside Inn & Suites is Surfside’s bigger sister. It has the same bright, beachy feel, but on a larger scale and with a bit more going on. It’s one of Universal’s best value hotels, especially if you’re travelling as a family and want plenty of space without stretching the budget.

The atmosphere at Dockside is really laid‑back. It’s the kind of place where you see families heading out early with their coffees, kids splashing in the pool in the afternoon, and everyone just generally enjoying that relaxed “we’re on holiday” feeling. It’s simple, friendly and very easy to settle into.

The two‑bedroom suites are a huge selling point here too. They’re just like Surfside's 2 bedroom suites, spacious, practical and ideal if you’ve got older kids, teens, or just want a bit more room to spread out. You get two separate sleeping areas, a kitchenette with a microwave and fridge, and plenty of storage. Again it’s everything you need for a comfortable stay.

Dockside actually has two large pool areas, which is lovely if you want a bit more space to relax. They’re bright, clean and have that classic Florida sunshine vibe. Nothing overly themed, but perfect for cooling off after a day in the parks. There’s also a pool bar, which is always a nice touch.

The food court is bigger than Surfside’s and has a good mix of quick meals and grab‑and‑go options. It’s ideal for families who want something easy without having to plan ahead. And yes, there’s a Starbucks here too, which is practically essential for early park mornings.

Transport works the same as Surfside: regular shuttle buses that take you straight to the parks. They’re quick, reliable and run all day, so getting around is really simple.

And just like Surfside, Dockside is only a short stroll from International Drive. That means even more dining options, shops and places to wander in the evening. It’s a great bonus if you like having choices beyond the hotel food court. Especially on longer stays when you want a bit of variety.

Dockside is a brilliant choice if you want a relaxed, great‑value hotel with big rooms and a friendly atmosphere. It’s not fancy, but it doesn’t pretend to be. Again it’s clean, comfortable and gives you everything you need for a fun, easy Universal holiday.

Pros & Considerations

Pros

  • Excellent value for money
  • Two‑bedroom suites are ideal for families
  • Two large pool areas
  • Bright, relaxed, friendly atmosphere
  • Starbucks in the lobby
  • Quick, reliable shuttle buses
  • Early park entry included
  • Walking distance to International Drive for extra dining options
  • Great for larger families or longer stays

Considerations

  • Not walking distance to the parks
  • Theming is simple rather than immersive
  • Can feel busy during peak times due to its size
  • Fewer on‑site dining options than the bigger resorts
  • No water taxi or walking path
