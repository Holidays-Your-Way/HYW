Ramada Plaza by Wyndham – International Drive

Affordable, central, and ideal for park‑focused stays.

Overview

Ramada Plaza by Wyndham on International Drive is a popular budget‑friendly hotel offering clean, comfortable rooms in one of the most convenient areas of Orlando. It’s ideal for guests who want to be close to Universal, ICON Park, and the endless dining and entertainment options along I‑Drive — all without paying resort prices.

The hotel offers simple but practical accommodation, with some upgraded rooms providing access to a rooftop pool and lounge. It’s a great choice for families, couples, and groups who plan to spend most of their time in the parks and want a reliable, affordable base in a prime location.

Room types

Rooms at Ramada Plaza are simple, clean, and practical — ideal for guests who want comfort and convenience without unnecessary extras. Club Level rooms offer additional perks and access to the rooftop area.

  • Standard Rooms: Two queen beds or one king bed, mini‑fridge, microwave, and coffee maker.
  • Deluxe Rooms: Slightly upgraded furnishings and views.
  • Club Level Suites: Extra space plus access to the rooftop pool and lounge.

Location

Located on International Drive, the hotel is within walking distance of ICON Park, restaurants, bars, shops, and attractions. Universal Orlando is just a short drive away, making it a convenient and affordable base for park‑focused holidays.

Things to consider

  • Not a luxury hotel — expect simple, practical accommodation.
  • Some rooms and areas may feel dated.
  • Shuttle schedule is limited — rideshares or car hire may be easier.

Why choose Ramada Plaza?

  • You want a clean, comfortable hotel at a great price.
  • You want to stay in the heart of International Drive.
  • You prefer to spend your budget on parks, food, and experiences.
  • You want optional access to a rooftop pool and lounge.

Travel agent insight

It’s perfect for park‑heavy holidays where you’re out most of the day and just need somewhere clean and comfortable to sleep. The Club Level upgrade is worth considering for the rooftop pool alone.
