Disney’s Caribbean Beach Resort

A colourful, tropical escape with Skyliner access.

A relaxed, island‑inspired Disney Moderate Resort

Disney’s Caribbean Beach Resort brings the laid‑back charm of the Caribbean to Walt Disney World. With pastel villages, sandy beaches, palm trees and a beautiful lakeside setting, it’s a fantastic choice for families and couples who want a more relaxed Disney stay.

As the central hub of the Disney Skyliner, it also offers unbeatable access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios.

Who is Caribbean Beach best for?

  • Families who want a relaxed, tropical Disney resort.
  • Guests who want Skyliner access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios.
  • Couples looking for a peaceful, scenic setting.
  • Anyone who wants more space and comfort than a Value Resort.

Rooms

Rooms are bright, colourful and inspired by the Caribbean islands. Expect comfortable beds, a mini‑fridge, coffee maker, large TV and plenty of storage. Some rooms include subtle pirate‑themed touches for younger guests.

Preferred rooms are available for guests who want to be closer to dining and the Skyliner station.

Dining

Caribbean Beach offers several great dining options:

  • Centertown Market — a large, varied quick‑service restaurant.
  • Banana Cabana — a relaxed poolside bar and grill.
  • Grab‑and‑go options for busy park mornings.

You can also walk to Disney’s Riviera Resort for additional dining choices.

Facilities

  • Fuentes del Morro Pool — a pirate‑themed main pool with slides.
  • Five leisure pools across the villages.
  • Disney Skyliner station.
  • Beaches, hammocks and walking trails.
  • Playgrounds and activities for kids.
  • Free Disney transportation.
  • On‑site laundry.

Location

Caribbean Beach is located in the EPCOT Resort Area of Walt Disney World. The Skyliner provides quick access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios, while buses run to Magic Kingdom, Animal Kingdom and Disney Springs.

Why choose Disney’s Caribbean Beach Resort?

This resort is perfect if you want a relaxed, tropical atmosphere with the convenience of the Skyliner. The theming is beautiful, the pool is fantastic for families, and the location makes getting around incredibly easy. A brilliant choice for guests who want comfort, scenery and great Disney value.
