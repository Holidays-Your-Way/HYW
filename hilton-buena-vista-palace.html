Hilton Orlando Buena Vista Palace

Premium, modern and steps from Disney Springs — with a stunning pool complex.

A premium Disney Springs resort with a modern, upscale feel

Hilton Buena Vista Palace is one of the most impressive hotels in the Disney Springs area — a tall, modern tower with bright rooms, a polished atmosphere and one of the best pool complexes outside of Disney’s own resorts. It’s stylish, comfortable and brilliantly located, making it a fantastic choice for families and couples who want a more premium experience without the luxury‑hotel price tag.

With Disney perks, multiple dining options and a lazy‑river‑style pool, it’s a resort that feels special from the moment you arrive.

Who is Hilton Buena Vista Palace best for?

  • Families who want a premium, modern hotel near Disney Springs.
  • Couples who enjoy stylish rooms and a resort‑style pool.
  • Guests who want Disney perks without staying on‑site.
  • Anyone who wants a polished, comfortable base close to Disney.

Rooms

Rooms at Buena Vista Palace are bright, modern and spacious, with large windows, plush beds and a clean, contemporary design. Many rooms offer Disney Springs or pool views, which look fantastic at night.

Everything feels fresh, polished and ideal for guests who want a more premium stay.

Dining

The hotel offers a great selection of dining options:

  • A casual on‑site restaurant for breakfast and dinner.
  • A lobby bar for cocktails and small plates.
  • A grab‑and‑go market for snacks, pastries and coffee.
  • Poolside food and drink service.

And with Disney Springs just steps away, you’ve got endless restaurants within a short walk.

Facilities

  • Stunning pool complex with lazy‑river‑style layout.
  • Quiet pool for relaxing.
  • Children’s splash areas.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site dining and grab‑and‑go options.
  • Disney park shuttles.
  • Walking distance to Disney Springs.

Location

Hilton Buena Vista Palace is perfectly located in the Disney Springs area — you can walk across the pedestrian bridge and be in the heart of the action within minutes. It’s ideal for guests who want easy access to Disney dining, shopping and entertainment.

Why choose Hilton Buena Vista Palace?

This hotel is perfect if you want a premium, modern base with a resort‑style pool and unbeatable access to Disney Springs. The rooms are stylish, the facilities are excellent, and the whole place feels polished and well‑run. A superb choice for families and couples who want something a little more special without the on‑site price tag.
