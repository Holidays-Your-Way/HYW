Sheraton Vistana Villages

Spacious villas, beautiful grounds and a premium resort feel.

A beautifully maintained villa resort perfect for families

Sheraton Vistana Villages is one of Orlando’s most polished family resorts — spacious villas, stunning pool areas and a calm, premium atmosphere that still feels relaxed and welcoming. It’s ideal for families who want more space than a hotel room, but still want resort facilities and a brilliant location close to Disney.

With multiple themed pools, lush landscaping and a layout that feels like a peaceful neighbourhood, it’s a fantastic choice for longer stays or anyone who wants comfort, flexibility and a touch of luxury without the luxury price tag.

Who is Sheraton Vistana Villages best for?

  • Families who want spacious villas with full kitchens.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who want a premium, homely base.
  • Travellers who enjoy resort facilities without hotel crowds.
  • Anyone who wants a calm, beautifully kept resort near Disney.

Villas

Villas at Vistana Villages are spacious, elegant and designed for real family living. Expect:

  • 1‑ and 2‑bedroom villas.
  • Full kitchens with fridge, oven, microwave and dishwasher.
  • Separate living and dining areas.
  • Washer/dryer in every villa.
  • Balconies overlooking the resort grounds.

Everything feels modern, comfortable and perfect for longer stays.

Dining

The resort offers flexible dining options ideal for families:

  • A casual on‑site restaurant and bar.
  • Poolside food and drink service in some areas.
  • A marketplace for snacks, coffee and essentials.
  • Full kitchens in every villa for easy meals.

And with the Lake Buena Vista area nearby, you’ve got endless restaurants within a short drive.

Facilities

  • Multiple themed pools, including the famous St. Augustine pool.
  • Children’s splash areas and water features.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Walking paths and beautifully landscaped grounds.
  • Marketplace and on‑site dining.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Washer/dryer in every villa.

Location

Sheraton Vistana Villages is perfectly located on International Drive South, close to Disney, SeaWorld and the Lake Buena Vista area. It’s quiet, convenient and ideal for families who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose Sheraton Vistana Villages?

This resort is perfect if you want space, comfort and a premium feel in a brilliant location. The villas are fantastic for families, the pools are beautiful, and the whole place feels calm, safe and incredibly well maintained. A superb choice for longer stays or anyone who wants a resort that feels like a home‑from‑home.
