Avanti Palms Resort & Conference Center

Spacious, modern and perfectly placed near Universal Orlando.

A large, modern resort with great facilities and a brilliant location

Avanti Palms is a big, bright, modern resort at the Universal end of International Drive. It’s stylish without being expensive, and it offers more facilities than many hotels in the same price range — including a large pool, a splash area, a café, a bar and a fitness centre.

The lobby and public areas feel fresh and contemporary, and the location is excellent for Universal fans. You’re close to restaurants, shops and attractions, with easy transport links to the parks.

Who is Avanti Palms best for?

  • Families who want a modern, great‑value resort near Universal.
  • Couples who like a bright, contemporary atmosphere.
  • Guests who want more facilities than a standard I‑Drive hotel.
  • Anyone who wants a lively, resort‑style feel without premium prices.

Rooms

Rooms at Avanti Palms are modern and comfortable, with bright décor and plenty of space. Expect comfy beds, a fridge, coffee maker and everything you need for a relaxing stay. Tower rooms offer great views of the Universal area, especially at night.

They’re not luxury, but they’re clean, fresh and ideal for park‑focused holidays.

Dining

The Marketplace Café offers quick meals, snacks and drinks throughout the day, and the lobby bar is great for evening cocktails. There’s also a Starbucks‑style coffee bar for grab‑and‑go breakfasts. And with International Drive right outside, you’ve got endless dining options within minutes.

Facilities

  • Large resort‑style pool with plenty of seating.
  • Children’s splash area.
  • Marketplace Café and lobby bar.
  • Fitness centre and games room.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Modern lobby and public spaces.

Location

Avanti Palms is perfectly placed for Universal Orlando — close enough to see the parks from the tower rooms. You’re right on International Drive, near ICON Park, restaurants, shops and attractions. It’s a brilliant base for families who want convenience without the higher cost of staying on‑site.

Why choose Avanti Palms?

Avanti Palms is ideal if you want a modern, lively, great‑value resort with more facilities than the typical I‑Drive hotel. The pool area is lovely, the location is excellent and the whole place feels fresh and welcoming. It’s a brilliant choice for families and couples who want style without the price tag.
