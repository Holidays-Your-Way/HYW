Drury Plaza Hotel Orlando

Modern, family‑friendly and packed with value — just minutes from Disney Springs.

A modern Disney Springs resort with unbeatable value

Drury Plaza is one of the newest and best‑reviewed hotels in the Disney Springs area — modern, bright and incredibly family‑friendly. What sets it apart is the value: **free hot breakfast every morning** and a **free evening Kickback** with hot food and drinks. For families, that’s a huge saving over a 10–14 night stay.

Add in a lovely pool, spacious rooms and Disney perks, and you’ve got one of the strongest all‑round options in Lake Buena Vista.

Who is Drury Plaza best for?

  • Families who want exceptional value without compromising on quality.
  • Guests who appreciate modern rooms and great facilities.
  • Travellers who love free breakfast and evening food/drinks.
  • Anyone who wants Disney perks without the premium price tag.

Rooms

Rooms at Drury Plaza are modern, bright and spacious, with a clean, contemporary design. Expect plush beds, good storage, a fridge, microwave and plenty of space to unwind after long park days.

Everything feels fresh, comfortable and ideal for families.

And with Disney Springs nearby, you’ve got endless dining options within minutes.

Location

Drury Plaza is perfectly located in the Disney Springs area, close to restaurants, shops and entertainment. It’s ideal for guests who want easy access to Disney without staying on‑site.

Why choose Drury Plaza?

This hotel is perfect if you want modern rooms, great facilities and exceptional value. The free breakfast and evening food/drinks make a huge difference for families, and the location is brilliant for Disney Springs and the parks. A superb all‑round choice for comfort, convenience and savings.
