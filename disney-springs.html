Disney Springs Guide

Dining, shopping and entertainment for the whole family.

Welcome to Disney Springs

Disney Springs is Walt Disney World’s dining, shopping and entertainment district — and it’s completely free to enter. With world‑class restaurants, unique shops, live music and family‑friendly fun, it’s the perfect place to spend an evening or enjoy a relaxing non‑park day.

This guide combines everything you need to know — the best restaurants, snacks, shops and things to do — all in one place.

Best Restaurants at Disney Springs

🍗 Chef Art Smith’s Homecomin’

Southern comfort food, incredible fried chicken and some of the best cocktails on Disney property.

🍝 The BOATHOUSE

Waterfront dining, amazing seafood and the iconic Amphicar rides.

🍕 Blaze Pizza

Quick, custom pizzas — perfect for families and picky eaters.

🍔 D-Luxe Burger

Great burgers, brilliant fries and a relaxed atmosphere.

🍣 Morimoto Asia

Upscale Asian cuisine with stunning theming — ideal for date nights.

🍷 Wine Bar George

A favourite for adults — small plates, incredible wine list and a relaxed vibe.

Sweet Treats You Can’t Miss

  • Gideon’s Bakehouse – giant cookies & incredible cakes
  • Everglazed Donuts – over‑the‑top doughnuts
  • Salt & Straw – artisan ice cream with unique flavours
  • Amorette’s Patisserie – beautiful pastries & mini cakes
  • The Ganachery – handcrafted chocolates

Disney Springs is a paradise for anyone with a sweet tooth — queues form early for Gideon’s!

Best Shopping at Disney Springs

  • World of Disney – the largest Disney store in the world
  • LEGO Store – huge models & build‑your‑own fun
  • DisneyStyle – trendy Disney fashion
  • Uniqlo – brilliant value clothing
  • Coca‑Cola Store – rooftop bar & global drinks
  • Marketplace Co‑Op – unique Disney homeware & gifts

Whether you want souvenirs, clothing or unique gifts, Disney Springs has something for everyone.

Entertainment & Things to Do

  • Live music throughout the day and evening
  • Aerophile Balloon – stunning views over Disney
  • Splitsville Luxury Lanes – bowling & dining
  • Cirque du Soleil: Drawn to Life – breathtaking show
  • Amphicar rides at The BOATHOUSE
  • Photo spots – perfect for Instagram‑friendly moments

Disney Springs is perfect for relaxed evenings, date nights or family fun.

Top Tips for Visiting Disney Springs

  • Arrive early for popular restaurants — they get busy
  • Use mobile ordering where available
  • Visit Gideon’s Bakehouse earlier in the day
  • Plan a Disney Springs evening on a rest day
  • Parking is free — a rare treat in Orlando
  • Weekends are busiest — weekdays are calmer
