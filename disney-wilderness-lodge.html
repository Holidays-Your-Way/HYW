Disney’s Wilderness Lodge

Rustic luxury inspired by the American Pacific Northwest.

A warm, atmospheric Disney Deluxe Resort

Disney’s Wilderness Lodge is a stunning, nature‑inspired resort that feels cosy, peaceful and beautifully themed. With its grand timber lobby, crackling fireplaces, bubbling springs and forested surroundings, it’s perfect for guests who want a relaxing retreat just minutes from Magic Kingdom.

It’s one of the most immersive and charming Deluxe Resorts — ideal for families and couples who love rustic luxury and beautiful theming.

Who is Wilderness Lodge best for?

  • Couples who want a romantic, cosy Disney resort.
  • Families who love themed resorts with beautiful details.
  • Guests who want boat access to Magic Kingdom.
  • Anyone who loves nature, rustic design and peaceful surroundings.

Rooms & Suites

Rooms at Wilderness Lodge are warm, comfortable and beautifully themed with natural wood, earthy tones and subtle nods to the great outdoors. Expect plush beds, large TVs, coffee makers, mini‑fridges and plenty of storage.

Many rooms offer views of the courtyard, forest or Bay Lake — all adding to the peaceful atmosphere.

Dining

Wilderness Lodge has some fantastic dining options:

  • Whispering Canyon Café — fun, family‑style dining with a lively twist.
  • Story Book Dining at Artist Point — character dining with Snow White.
  • Geyser Point Bar & Grill — lakeside dining with beautiful views.
  • Roaring Fork — quick‑service meals and snacks.

Whether you want a relaxed lakeside meal or a fun character experience, this resort has great variety.

Facilities

  • Two pools, including the Copper Creek Springs Pool with a waterslide.
  • Boat transportation to Magic Kingdom.
  • Walking trails and beautiful outdoor spaces.
  • Fitness centre and hot tubs.
  • Campfire activities and movies under the stars.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

Wilderness Lodge is located in the Magic Kingdom Resort Area. You can take a relaxing boat ride to Magic Kingdom, while buses run to EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom and Disney Springs.

Why choose Disney’s Wilderness Lodge?

This resort is perfect if you want a cosy, beautifully themed Disney stay with a peaceful atmosphere. The dining is excellent, the theming is stunning and the boat to Magic Kingdom is a lovely bonus. A brilliant choice for families and couples who want rustic luxury and Disney magic.
