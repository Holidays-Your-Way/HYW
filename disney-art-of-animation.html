Disney’s Art of Animation Resort

Step into the worlds of your favourite Disney and Pixar films.

Disney’s most immersive Value Resort

Disney’s Art of Animation Resort brings beloved Disney and Pixar stories to life with incredible theming, colourful landscapes and some of the best family suites on Disney property. From The Lion King to Finding Nemo, Cars and The Little Mermaid, every area feels like stepping straight into the films.

With Skyliner access, huge pools and brilliant family‑friendly facilities, it’s one of the most popular Disney hotels for families.

Who is Art of Animation best for?

  • Families who want amazing theming and larger‑than‑life Disney magic.
  • Guests who need extra space — especially in the family suites.
  • Anyone who wants Skyliner access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios.
  • Kids who love Cars, Nemo, Lion King or The Little Mermaid.

Rooms & Family Suites

Art of Animation offers two types of accommodation:

  • Family Suites (Lion King, Cars, Finding Nemo) — sleep up to 6, with a bedroom, two bathrooms, kitchenette and living area.
  • Standard Rooms (Little Mermaid) — bright, fun and perfect for smaller families or couples.

The suites are some of the best value on Disney property for larger families who want space and comfort.

Dining

The resort offers:

  • Landscape of Flavors — a large, varied quick‑service restaurant.
  • Poolside bar for drinks and snacks.
  • Grab‑and‑go options for busy park mornings.

You can also walk to Pop Century for even more dining choices.

Facilities

  • The Big Blue Pool — the largest pool at any Disney hotel.
  • Two additional themed pools.
  • Disney Skyliner station.
  • Playgrounds and splash areas.
  • Arcade and jogging trails.
  • Free Disney transportation.
  • On‑site laundry.

Location

Art of Animation is located in the Wide World of Sports area of Walt Disney World, with Skyliner access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios. Buses run to Magic Kingdom, Animal Kingdom and Disney Springs.

Why choose Disney’s Art of Animation Resort?

This resort is perfect for families who want full Disney magic from the moment they arrive. The theming is incredible, the family suites are spacious and practical, and the Skyliner access makes getting around so easy. A brilliant choice for families who want fun, colour and convenience.
