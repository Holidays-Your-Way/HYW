Wyndham Orlando Resort International Drive

Tropical, relaxed and right in the heart of International Drive.

A lush, resort‑style escape with a brilliant I‑Drive location

Wyndham Orlando Resort International Drive is one of the most tropical‑feeling hotels on I‑Drive — palm trees, winding pathways, bright colours and a relaxed, resort‑style atmosphere. It’s a great choice for families and couples who want something a little more scenic and spacious without jumping into premium pricing.

The hotel has two lovely pools, a fitness centre, a restaurant, a bar and easy access to ICON Park, which is just a short walk away. It’s a brilliant all‑rounder for guests who want comfort, convenience and a bit of that “holiday resort” feel.

Who is Wyndham I‑Drive best for?

  • Families who want a tropical, resort‑style hotel without premium prices.
  • Couples who prefer a relaxed, scenic atmosphere.
  • Guests who want to be within walking distance of ICON Park.
  • Anyone who wants a comfortable base with great facilities.

Rooms

Rooms are bright, clean and comfortable, with a warm, modern design. Expect plush beds, good storage, a fridge and everything you need for a relaxing stay. Many rooms open directly onto the tropical gardens or pool areas, adding to the resort feel.

They’re not luxury, but they’re spacious, well‑kept and ideal for families.

Dining

The on‑site restaurant offers easy breakfasts and dinners, and the lobby bar is great for evening drinks. There’s also a grab‑and‑go market for snacks and essentials. And with ICON Park and International Drive right outside, you’ve got endless dining options within minutes.

Facilities

  • Two tropical outdoor pools.
  • Hot tub and spacious sun decks.
  • On‑site restaurant, bar and grab‑and‑go market.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Beautiful landscaped gardens and walkways.

Location

The location is one of the biggest reasons people choose Wyndham. You’re right on International Drive, within walking distance of ICON Park, restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal is a short drive or shuttle ride away, making it a brilliant base for exploring Orlando.

Why choose Wyndham I‑Drive?

This hotel is perfect if you want a relaxed, tropical, resort‑style stay in a fantastic location. The gardens and pool areas are lovely, the atmosphere is calm and welcoming, and the whole place offers great value for the facilities you get. A brilliant choice for families and couples alike.
