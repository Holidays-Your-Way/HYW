Sonesta ES Suites – International Drive

Bright, modern suites with space to relax and a fantastic I‑Drive location.

A stylish, all‑suite hotel designed for comfort and flexibility

Sonesta ES Suites is one of the most popular family hotels on International Drive — bright, modern and designed with longer stays in mind. Every room is a full suite with a kitchen, living area and plenty of space, making it ideal for families who want comfort, flexibility and great value.

The atmosphere is relaxed and friendly, the pool area is lovely, and the location is spot‑on for ICON Park, Universal and the rest of I‑Drive. It’s a brilliant choice if you want a home‑from‑home feel with modern style.

Who is Sonesta ES Suites best for?

  • Families who want space, comfort and a separate living area.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who want a homely base.
  • Travellers who like having a kitchen for easy meals.
  • Anyone who wants a modern, relaxed hotel near ICON Park.

Suites

Suites at Sonesta are bright, modern and spacious — perfect for families who want room to spread out. Expect:

  • A separate bedroom and living room.
  • A full kitchen with fridge, microwave, hob and dishwasher.
  • A dining area.
  • A sofa bed for extra sleeping space.

Everything feels clean, fresh and well‑maintained — a noticeable step up from standard hotel rooms on I‑Drive.

Dining

Sonesta offers great flexibility with:

  • A full kitchen in every suite.
  • A small convenience shop for snacks and essentials.
  • Easy access to restaurants at ICON Park and along I‑Drive.

It’s ideal for families who like the option of eating in or mixing things up during their stay.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool and hot tub.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Full kitchens in every suite.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Modern lobby and lounge areas.
  • Pet‑friendly rooms available.

Location

Sonesta ES Suites is perfectly located opposite ICON Park, close to restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal is a short drive or shuttle ride away, and you’ve got easy access to SeaWorld and the rest of International Drive.

Why choose Sonesta ES Suites?

This hotel is perfect if you want modern, spacious suites in a brilliant location. The kitchens make life easier, the layout is ideal for families, and the whole place feels bright, relaxed and great value. A superb choice for longer stays or anyone who wants a stylish home‑from‑home near Universal.
