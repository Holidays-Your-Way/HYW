CityWalk Dining Guide

The best restaurants, snacks and treats at Universal CityWalk.

Welcome to Universal’s Dining & Entertainment Hub

CityWalk is the vibrant heart of Universal Orlando — packed with brilliant restaurants, fun bars, sweet treats and entertainment. Whether you’re visiting after a day in the parks or planning a special evening out, there’s something here for every taste and every age.

This guide highlights the best places to eat, family‑friendly favourites and insider tips to help you make the most of your visit.

Top Restaurants at CityWalk

🍔 The Cowfish Sushi Burger Bar

A quirky mix of sushi and burgers — perfect for families with different tastes. Great kids’ menu and fun vibe.

🍕 VIVO Italian Kitchen

Fresh pasta, wood‑fired pizzas and a warm, modern atmosphere. A brilliant choice for families and date nights.

🍗 Bigfire

A stylish lakeside restaurant with open‑fire cooking. Think steaks, burgers and comfort food with a twist.

🌮 Antojitos Authentic Mexican Food

Colourful, lively and full of flavour — with live music on select nights.

🍩 Voodoo Doughnut

A must‑visit for fun, creative doughnuts. Perfect for a late‑night treat or a snack on the go.

Best Family‑Friendly Options

  • Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. – themed, fun and great for kids
  • Bread Box – simple, tasty sandwiches and quick meals
  • Red Oven Pizza Bakery – fast, fresh and perfect for picky eaters
  • Toothsome Chocolate Emporium – incredible milkshakes and desserts

CityWalk is very family‑friendly, with plenty of outdoor seating and quick‑service options for tired little ones.

Sweet Treats You Can’t Miss

  • Toothsome Chocolate Emporium: outrageous milkshakes
  • Voodoo Doughnut: iconic, colourful doughnuts
  • Cold Stone Creamery: mix‑in ice cream creations

Perfect for an evening stroll or a reward after a long park day.

Top Tips for Dining at CityWalk

  • Arrive early — restaurants get busy after park closing
  • Mobile order where available to save time
  • Plan a CityWalk evening on a rest day for a relaxed night out
  • Toothsome and Voodoo Doughnut often have queues — go off‑peak if you can

Why CityWalk Is Perfect for UK Families

CityWalk is the ideal place to unwind after a busy day in the parks. With brilliant food, fun entertainment and a lively atmosphere, it’s a highlight of many UK families’ holidays. I’m here to help you choose the best restaurants for your family’s tastes and budget.
