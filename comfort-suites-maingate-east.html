Comfort Suites Maingate East

Spacious suites and fantastic value near Disney.

A spacious, family‑friendly hotel in a brilliant Kissimmee location

Comfort Suites Maingate East is one of the most popular family hotels on Kissimmee’s 192 — and for good reason. The suites are huge, the complimentary breakfast is excellent, and the location right next to Old Town gives you entertainment, dining and shops on your doorstep.

It’s a fantastic choice for families who want comfort, space and great value close to Disney.

Who is Comfort Suites Maingate East best for?

  • Families who want large suites with plenty of space.
  • Guests who want great value close to Disney.
  • Anyone who appreciates complimentary breakfast and reliable facilities.
  • Travellers who want to be next to Old Town and Fun Spot.

Suites

Suites here are genuinely spacious, with separate sleeping and living areas — ideal for families who want room to spread out. Expect comfortable beds, a sofa‑sleeper, fridge, microwave, coffee maker and plenty of storage.

The layout makes longer stays much more comfortable than a standard hotel room.

Dining

Comfort Suites offers:

  • Complimentary hot breakfast.
  • Poolside bar (seasonal).
  • Grab‑and‑go snacks available on‑site.

And with Old Town and 192 right outside, you’re surrounded by restaurants for every taste and budget.

Facilities

  • Large outdoor pool and hot tub.
  • Complimentary breakfast.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Game room.
  • Free parking.
  • Shuttle options to Disney.
  • 24‑hour front desk.

Location

The hotel is located right next to Old Town on Kissimmee’s lively 192, close to Disney, Fun Spot, restaurants, shops and attractions. It’s a brilliant base for families who want convenience and value.

Why choose Comfort Suites Maingate East?

This hotel is perfect if you want space, comfort and great value near Disney. The suites are huge, the breakfast is excellent, and being next to Old Town gives you entertainment right on your doorstep. A fantastic choice for families who want a relaxed, comfortable base.
