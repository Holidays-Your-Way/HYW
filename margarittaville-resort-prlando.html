Margaritaville Resort Orlando

A tropical, laid‑back escape just minutes from Disney.

A premium, island‑inspired resort perfect for families and couples

Margaritaville Resort Orlando is one of the most stylish and relaxing resorts in the Kissimmee/192 area. With its tropical theme, lagoon‑style pools, spacious rooms and access to the amazing Island H2O Water Park, it’s a fantastic choice for guests who want a more luxurious, resort‑style holiday close to Disney.

It’s bright, modern and beautifully designed — ideal for families, couples and groups who want something a little bit special.

Who is Margaritaville Resort best for?

  • Families who want a premium resort with great facilities.
  • Couples looking for a relaxed, tropical escape.
  • Guests who want access to Island H2O Water Park.
  • Anyone who wants a stylish, modern base near Disney.

Rooms & Cottages

Margaritaville offers two types of accommodation: beautifully designed hotel rooms and spacious standalone cottages. Both options feature bright, coastal décor, plush beds, modern bathrooms and plenty of space to relax.

Cottages are ideal for families and groups who want extra privacy and home‑style comfort.

Dining

The resort offers several fantastic dining options:

  • On‑site restaurants with island‑inspired menus.
  • Poolside bars for drinks and light bites.
  • Grab‑and‑go options for snacks and essentials.

Plus, the Promenade at Sunset Walk is right next door, offering even more restaurants, cafés and entertainment.

Facilities

  • Lagoon‑style pools with cabanas.
  • Access to Island H2O Water Park.
  • Full‑service spa.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site dining and bars.
  • Free Wi‑Fi.
  • Shuttle options to Disney.
  • 24‑hour front desk.

Location

Margaritaville is located on 192, close to Disney and steps from Sunset Walk’s restaurants, shops and entertainment. It’s a brilliant base for guests who want a premium resort with everything close by.

Why choose Margaritaville Resort Orlando?

This resort is perfect if you want a stylish, relaxing and premium base near Disney. The tropical theme, lagoon pools and access to Island H2O Water Park make it a fantastic choice for families and couples who want something a little more special. A brilliant blend of comfort, fun and luxury.
