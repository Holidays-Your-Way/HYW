Great value, Disney perks and the closest walk to Disney Springs.

A brilliant value option with the same unbeatable location

Wyndham Garden shares the same incredible location and facilities as Wyndham Lake Buena Vista — the only difference is the room style. Garden rooms are simpler and more budget‑friendly, making this a fantastic choice for families who want Disney convenience without the higher price tag.

You still get Disney perks, the same pool complex, the same dining options and the same walk‑to‑Disney‑Springs location — just at a lower cost.

Who is Wyndham Garden LBV best for?

  • Families who want Disney Springs access at a lower price.
  • Guests who don’t mind simpler rooms.
  • Travellers who want Disney perks without the premium.
  • Anyone who values location above all else.

Rooms

Rooms at Wyndham Garden are clean, comfortable and practical. They’re simpler than the tower rooms at Wyndham LBV, but still offer everything you need for a great stay — plush beds, good storage and a bright, modern feel.

They’re ideal for guests who want value and location over luxury.

Dining

Guests at Wyndham Garden share all the same dining options as Wyndham LBV:

  • On‑site restaurant.
  • Poolside bar.
  • Grab‑and‑go market.

And of course, Disney Springs is just a short walk away.

Facilities

  • Children’s splash area.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Disney park shuttles.
  • Shared facilities with Wyndham LBV.
  • Walking distance to Disney Springs.

Location

Wyndham Garden shares the same unbeatable location — the closest hotels to Disney Springs. It’s perfect for guests who want dining, shopping and entertainment right on their doorstep.

Why choose Wyndham Garden LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want Disney Springs access and Disney perks at a lower price point. The rooms are simpler, but the facilities and location are outstanding. A brilliant value choice for families who prioritise convenience and budget.
