Understanding the Categories

Value Hotels

Best for families who want space and great prices. Early entry, free shuttles and simple, bright theming. Most Value hotels also offer large two bedroom family suites, making them ideal for bigger families.

Prime Value Hotels

A step up in theming and features. Cabana Bay has family suites and a lively resort feel, while Aventura is modern, calm and stylish.

Preferred Hotels

A lovely middle ground, more premium without Premier prices. Sapphire Falls offers family suites and a tropical atmosphere, while Helios Grand is closest to Epic Universe.

Premier Hotels (Express Pass Included)

The top tier, and the only hotels that include Unlimited Express Pass. All three Premier hotels offer family suites and beautiful theming.
