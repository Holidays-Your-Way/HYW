Disney’s Contemporary Resort

Iconic, modern and just steps from Magic Kingdom.

A sleek, modern Disney Deluxe Resort with unbeatable location

Disney’s Contemporary Resort is one of the most iconic hotels at Walt Disney World. With its striking A‑frame design, monorail running straight through the lobby and incredible views of Magic Kingdom, it’s a favourite for families and couples who want luxury and convenience.

It’s also the only Disney hotel where you can walk to Magic Kingdom — a huge bonus for families with little ones.

Who is the Contemporary best for?

  • Families who want to walk to Magic Kingdom.
  • Guests who love modern, sleek design.
  • Couples looking for a premium Disney stay with great dining.
  • Anyone who wants monorail access and incredible views.

Rooms & The Incredibles Theme

Rooms at the Contemporary are bright, modern and newly refurbished with subtle Incredibles‑inspired touches. Expect plush beds, large TVs, coffee makers, mini‑fridges and plenty of storage.

Theme park view rooms offer stunning views of Magic Kingdom — perfect for watching the fireworks from your room.

Dining

The Contemporary has some of the best dining options on Disney property:

  • California Grill — signature dining with incredible views.
  • Chef Mickey’s — a hugely popular character meal.
  • Steakhouse 71 — modern American dining.
  • Contempo Café — quick‑service meals and snacks.

Whether you want character dining or a romantic evening, this resort has it all.

Facilities

  • Two pools, including a waterslide.
  • Monorail access to Magic Kingdom and EPCOT.
  • Walking path to Magic Kingdom.
  • Fitness centre and lakeside activities.
  • Shops, lounges and entertainment.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

The Contemporary is located in the Magic Kingdom Resort Area. You can walk to Magic Kingdom, take the monorail to EPCOT and use buses or boats for the other parks.

Why choose Disney’s Contemporary Resort?

This resort is perfect if you want luxury, convenience and iconic Disney design. The ability to walk to Magic Kingdom is a huge advantage, the dining is fantastic and the modern rooms are ideal for families and couples. A brilliant choice for guests who want a premium Disney experience.
