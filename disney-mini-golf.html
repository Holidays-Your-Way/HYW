Disney Mini Golf

Fun, themed courses perfect for families and rest days.

A fun break from the theme parks

Disney offers two brilliantly themed mini golf locations, each with two 18‑hole courses. They’re perfect for families, couples and anyone looking for a relaxed activity outside the parks. Whether you want whimsical fairytale fun or festive chaos, Disney mini golf is a great way to add something different to your holiday.

Fantasia Gardens & Fairways Miniature Golf

Located near the Walt Disney World Swan & Dolphin Hotels, Fantasia Gardens offers two very different courses — one whimsical and one surprisingly challenging.

✨ Fantasia Gardens Course

A fun, family‑friendly course inspired by Disney’s *Fantasia*. Expect dancing hippos, magical fountains and musical surprises.

⛳ Fairways Course

A more advanced, “miniature golf meets real golf” course with bunkers, slopes and water hazards. Great for older kids and adults.

  • Location: Near EPCOT Resort Area
  • Two 18‑hole courses
  • Great for families and golfers alike

Winter Summerland Miniature Golf

Located next to Blizzard Beach, Winter Summerland is a festive, Santa‑themed mini golf course with two playful sides — one snowy, one summery.

❄️ Winter Course

Snowmen, Christmas trees and icy obstacles — all in the Florida sunshine.

🌴 Summer Course

A beach‑themed holiday adventure with sandcastles, surfboards and Santa on vacation.

  • Location: Next to Blizzard Beach
  • Two 18‑hole courses
  • Perfect for younger children

Top Tips for Disney Mini Golf

  • Arrive early or late afternoon to avoid the midday heat.
  • Mini golf is included with some Disney holiday packages.
  • Both locations offer free parking.
  • Great for arrival or departure day when you don’t want a full park visit.

Why I Recommend Disney Mini Golf

Disney’s mini golf courses are fun, affordable and perfect for a relaxed break from the parks. With creative theming, two locations and four unique courses, it’s a brilliant activity for families, couples and groups.
