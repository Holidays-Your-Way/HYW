Disney’s Yacht Club Resort

Elegant, nautical luxury just steps from EPCOT.

A sophisticated, nautical Disney Deluxe Resort

Disney’s Yacht Club Resort offers a refined, New England yacht‑club atmosphere with elegant décor, beautiful grounds and a calm, upscale feel. It’s the more sophisticated sister resort to Disney’s Beach Club, and together they share one of the best pool complexes in all of Walt Disney World — Stormalong Bay.

With walking access to EPCOT, boat service to Hollywood Studios and the BoardWalk on your doorstep, it’s a fantastic choice for families and couples who want luxury and convenience.

Who is Yacht Club best for?

  • Couples who want a more refined, elegant Deluxe Resort.
  • Families who want access to Stormalong Bay.
  • Guests who want to walk to EPCOT and the BoardWalk.
  • Anyone who prefers a quieter, more polished atmosphere than Beach Club.

Rooms

Rooms at Yacht Club are stylish, modern and nautically themed with deep blues, crisp whites and elegant detailing. Expect plush beds, large TVs, coffee makers, mini‑fridges and plenty of storage.

Many rooms offer water or garden views, and the overall feel is slightly more luxurious and grown‑up than its Beach Club neighbour.

Dining

Yacht Club offers excellent dining options, including:

  • Yachtsman Steakhouse — signature dining with exceptional steaks.
  • Ale & Compass Restaurant — modern American comfort food.
  • Ale & Compass Lounge — cocktails and small plates.
  • Market at Ale & Compass — quick‑service meals and snacks.

You’re also steps away from Beach Club dining and the BoardWalk restaurants.

Facilities

  • Stormalong Bay — a 3‑acre water playground with lazy river and sand‑bottom pool.
  • Quiet leisure pools.
  • Boat transportation to Hollywood Studios.
  • Walking access to EPCOT and the BoardWalk.
  • Fitness centre, tennis courts and recreation.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

Yacht Club is located in the EPCOT Resort Area. You can walk to EPCOT in minutes, take a boat to Hollywood Studios or enjoy a short stroll to Disney’s BoardWalk.

Why choose Disney’s Yacht Club Resort?

This resort is perfect if you want a refined, elegant Deluxe stay with unbeatable access to EPCOT and the BoardWalk. The nautical theming is beautiful, the atmosphere is calm and sophisticated, and Stormalong Bay is a huge highlight for families. A brilliant choice for guests who want luxury, convenience and a polished Disney experience.
