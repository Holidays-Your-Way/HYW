Clarion Inn International Drive

A simple, budget‑friendly base in the heart of I‑Drive.

Affordable, central and ideal for park‑focused stays

Clarion Inn International Drive is a straightforward, budget‑friendly hotel in a brilliant location. It’s ideal for guests who want to be right in the middle of International Drive without spending a fortune. The rooms are simple but comfortable, the pool area is a nice bonus, and you’re surrounded by restaurants, shops and attractions.

It’s a great choice for families and couples who want value, convenience and a clean place to sleep after long days in the parks.

Who is Clarion Inn I‑Drive best for?

  • Families on a tight budget who want a central location.
  • Guests who plan to spend most of their time in the parks.
  • Couples looking for simple, affordable accommodation.
  • Anyone who wants to be right on International Drive.

Rooms

Rooms are basic but clean and comfortable, with everything you need for a good night’s sleep. Expect air‑conditioning, a fridge, microwave, coffee maker and practical storage — ideal for longer stays.

They’re not modern or luxurious, but they’re perfectly adequate for park‑focused holidays.

Dining

Clarion Inn offers simple on‑site dining options:

  • Breakfast restaurant.
  • Poolside bar for drinks and snacks.
  • Grab‑and‑go options in the lobby.

And with International Drive outside your door, you’ve got endless restaurants within walking distance.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool.
  • On‑site dining.
  • Free parking.
  • Shuttle options to the parks.
  • 24‑hour front desk.
  • Gift shop and convenience store.

Location

The location is one of the biggest selling points — you’re right on International Drive, close to ICON Park, restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal and SeaWorld are both a short drive away.

Why choose Clarion Inn I‑Drive?

This hotel is perfect if you want a clean, simple and affordable base in a fantastic location. It’s ideal for families and couples who plan to spend most of their time exploring Orlando and just need a comfortable place to unwind at the end of the day. A great value option in the heart of International Drive.
