Homewood Suites – Lake Buena Vista

All‑suite comfort, full kitchens and fantastic value near Disney.

A homely, all‑suite hotel perfect for longer stays

Homewood Suites LBV is one of the best family hotels in the area — spacious suites, full kitchens and a relaxed, friendly atmosphere that makes longer stays feel easy. It’s ideal for guests who want more space than a standard hotel room and the flexibility to cook simple meals during a 10–14 night stay.

With free breakfast, evening socials, a lovely pool and a brilliant location close to Disney, it’s a fantastic value‑for‑space option for families and couples alike.

Who is Homewood Suites LBV best for?

  • Families who want separate living and sleeping areas.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who need a proper home‑from‑home.
  • Travellers who prefer full kitchens for easy meals.
  • Anyone who wants great value close to Disney.

Suites

Suites at Homewood are spacious, comfortable and designed for real family living. Expect:

  • Studio and one‑bedroom suites.
  • Full kitchens with fridge, stove, microwave and dishwasher.
  • Separate living and sleeping areas.
  • Dining table and workspace.
  • Plenty of storage for longer stays.

It’s one of the best value‑for‑space options in Lake Buena Vista.

Dining

Homewood Suites offers excellent included dining:

  • Free hot breakfast every morning.
  • Evening socials on select nights with drinks and snacks.
  • Full kitchens in every suite for easy meals.
  • 24/7 market for snacks and essentials.

And with Lake Buena Vista’s restaurants minutes away, you’re never short of choice.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with plenty of seating.
  • Whirlpool spa.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Free breakfast and evening socials.
  • BBQ and picnic areas.
  • Disney park shuttles.
  • Quiet, relaxed grounds.

Location

Homewood Suites is perfectly located in Lake Buena Vista, close to restaurants, shops and Walt Disney World. It’s quiet, convenient and ideal for families who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose Homewood Suites LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want space, comfort and flexibility. The suites are fantastic for longer stays, the full kitchens make life easier, and the free breakfast and evening socials add brilliant value. A superb choice for families and couples who want a relaxed base near Disney without the premium price tag.
