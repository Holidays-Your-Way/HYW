Magic Kingdom Dining

From princess feasts to classic Disney treats.

Where Magic Meets Mealtime

Magic Kingdom is home to some of the most iconic dining experiences at Walt Disney World. Whether you’re looking for a quick bite between rides, a character meal your children will never forget or a beautiful sit‑down dinner inside a castle, this park has something for everyone.

This guide highlights the best Quick‑Service, Table‑Service and snack options to help you plan the perfect day.

Character Dining in Magic Kingdom

🏰 Cinderella’s Royal Table

Dine inside Cinderella Castle with Disney Princesses. A magical, unforgettable experience.

🐻 The Crystal Palace

Buffet dining with Winnie the Pooh and friends — great for families with little ones.

Both restaurants are included in the Disney Dining Plan (Cinderella’s Royal Table requires two Table‑Service credits).

Top Table‑Service Restaurants

🍽️ Be Our Guest Restaurant

Dine inside Beast’s enchanted castle. Stunning theming and a beautiful atmosphere.

🍗 Liberty Tree Tavern

Family‑style comfort food in a cosy colonial setting.

🍝 Tony’s Town Square Restaurant

Italian classics inspired by *Lady and the Tramp*.

🍳 The Plaza Restaurant

Casual American dining with great views of Main Street, U.S.A.

Best Quick‑Service Restaurants

🍔 Cosmic Ray’s Starlight Café

Classic American favourites with entertainment from Sonny Eclipse.

🍲 Columbia Harbour House

Seafood, chicken and lighter options — a great alternative to burgers.

🍕 Pinocchio Village Haus

Flatbreads and Italian‑inspired dishes with views of “it’s a small world.”

🍟 Casey’s Corner

Famous hot dogs and classic American snacks on Main Street, U.S.A.

Must‑Try Snacks

  • Dole Whip (Aloha Isle)
  • Mickey Pretzel
  • Cheshire Cat Tail (Cheshire Café)
  • Corn Dog Nuggets (Casey’s Corner)
  • Fresh Fruit Waffle Sandwich (Sleepy Hollow)

Many snacks are included in both Disney Dining Plans — look for the Dining Plan symbol on menus.

Top Dining Tips for Magic Kingdom

  • Book Table‑Service restaurants 60 days before arrival.
  • Use mobile ordering for Quick‑Service meals to skip queues.
  • Eat at off‑peak times for quieter dining rooms.
  • Plan a sit‑down meal during the hottest part of the day.
  • Let me know your family’s favourites — I’ll help you choose the perfect restaurants.
