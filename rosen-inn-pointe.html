Rosen Inn at Pointe Orlando

Reliable, great value and right in the heart of International Drive.

A comfortable, great‑value base with everything on your doorstep

Rosen Inn at Pointe Orlando is one of those hotels that just works. It’s clean, comfortable and brilliantly located — right on International Drive, opposite Pointe Orlando and surrounded by restaurants, shops and entertainment. It’s not fancy, but it’s reliable, friendly and excellent value for money.

The hotel has everything you need for an easy stay: two pools, a restaurant, a sports bar, a small shop and free shuttles to the major theme parks. It’s a great choice if you want to be close to the action without paying on‑site prices.

Who is Rosen Inn at Pointe Orlando best for?

  • Families who want great value in a central location.
  • Guests who like having restaurants and entertainment within walking distance.
  • Travellers who plan full park days and want a simple, comfortable base.
  • Anyone who prefers a quieter, more traditional hotel feel.

Rooms

Rooms are clean, comfortable and practical — exactly what you need for a park‑focused holiday. Expect comfy beds, a microwave, fridge, coffee maker and plenty of space for families. They’re not modern or themed, but they’re well‑kept and reliable.

Dining

The on‑site restaurant offers easy breakfasts and dinners, and the sports bar is great for relaxed evenings. There’s also a small convenience store for snacks and essentials. And of course, you’re right on International Drive — so you’ve got endless dining options within a few minutes’ walk.

Facilities

  • Two outdoor pools.
  • Sports bar and on‑site restaurant.
  • Small convenience store.
  • Free shuttle buses to major theme parks.
  • Secure gated parking.

Location

The location is one of the biggest selling points. You’re right on International Drive, opposite Pointe Orlando and close to ICON Park. Universal is just a short drive or shuttle ride away, and you’ve got restaurants, shops and entertainment in every direction.

Why choose Rosen Inn at Pointe Orlando?

This hotel is perfect if you want a clean, comfortable, great‑value base in a brilliant location. It’s simple, friendly and reliable — ideal for families who plan to spend most of their time in the parks or exploring International Drive. It does exactly what you need it to do, and it does it well.
