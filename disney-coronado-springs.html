Disney’s Coronado Springs Resort

A stylish, modern Disney Moderate with a stunning lakeside setting.

A beautifully themed resort with a deluxe feel

Disney’s Coronado Springs Resort blends Spanish, Mexican and Southwestern influences with modern Disney design. With its gorgeous lakeside setting, beautifully renovated rooms and the impressive Gran Destino Tower, it feels more like a Deluxe Resort at a Moderate price.

It’s a fantastic choice for couples, families and guests who want a more refined Disney stay without the higher price tag.

Who is Coronado Springs best for?

  • Couples who want a stylish, modern Disney resort.
  • Families who want more comfort and space than a Value Resort.
  • Guests who love great dining and beautiful theming.
  • Anyone who wants a Deluxe‑style experience at a Moderate price.

Rooms & Gran Destino Tower

Rooms at Coronado Springs are modern, spacious and beautifully designed, with comfortable beds, large TVs, coffee makers and plenty of storage. The resort also features the stunning Gran Destino Tower — offering upgraded rooms, incredible views and a more luxurious feel.

Whether you choose the tower or the village buildings, you’ll enjoy a stylish, comfortable stay.

Dining

Coronado Springs has some of the best dining options of any Moderate Resort:

  • Three Bridges Bar & Grill — a beautiful lakeside restaurant.
  • Toledo — a rooftop restaurant with stunning views.
  • El Mercado de Coronado — a large quick‑service dining hall.
  • Dahlia Lounge — stylish rooftop cocktails.

Whether you want casual or upscale dining, this resort has it covered.

Facilities

  • The Lost City of Cibola Pool — one of the best Moderate pools.
  • Hot tubs and leisure pools.
  • Beautiful lakeside walkways.
  • Fitness centre and spa services.
  • Jogging trails.
  • Free Disney transportation.
  • On‑site laundry.

Location

Coronado Springs is located in the Animal Kingdom Resort Area of Walt Disney World. Buses run to all four theme parks, Disney Springs and the water parks.

Why choose Disney’s Coronado Springs Resort?

This resort is perfect if you want a more refined Disney stay without the Deluxe price tag. The rooms are modern, the dining is excellent and the Gran Destino Tower adds a touch of luxury. A brilliant choice for couples and families who want comfort, style and great value.
