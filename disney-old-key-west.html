Disney’s Old Key West Resort

Spacious villas and relaxed island charm.

A peaceful, spacious Disney Deluxe Villa Resort

Disney’s Old Key West Resort is the original Disney Vacation Club resort, offering a relaxed island atmosphere inspired by the charm of the Florida Keys. With pastel buildings, palm trees and beautiful waterways, it’s one of the most peaceful places to stay at Walt Disney World.

The villas here are some of the largest on property, making it a fantastic choice for families, groups and guests who want extra space and comfort.

Who is Old Key West best for?

  • Families who want large villas with full kitchens.
  • Groups who need multi‑bedroom accommodation.
  • Guests who prefer a quiet, laid‑back resort.
  • Anyone who wants easy access to Disney Springs.

Resort Layout & Atmosphere

Old Key West is a large, beautifully spread‑out resort with a relaxed neighbourhood feel. Villas are arranged in pastel‑coloured buildings along waterways and golf course views, creating a peaceful, scenic environment.

The resort has several “neighbourhoods,” each close to pools, bus stops or the main hospitality area.

Studios & Villas

Old Key West offers some of the most spacious villas at Walt Disney World, perfect for longer stays or families who want room to spread out:

  • Deluxe Studios — sleep up to 4 with a kitchenette.
  • 1‑Bedroom Villas — huge living space, full kitchen and washer/dryer.
  • 2‑Bedroom Villas — ideal for larger families or groups.
  • 3‑Bedroom Grand Villas — incredibly spacious and luxurious.

The décor is bright, airy and inspired by the laid‑back charm of the Florida Keys.

Dining

Old Key West offers relaxed, family‑friendly dining options:

  • Olivia’s Café — homestyle comfort food and a hidden gem.
  • Good’s Food to Go — quick‑service meals and snacks.
  • Gurgling Suitcase — a cosy Key West‑style lounge.

You’re also just a short boat ride from the incredible dining at Disney Springs.

Facilities

  • Multiple pools, including the Sandcastle Pool with a waterslide.
  • Boat transportation to Disney Springs.
  • Fitness centre, tennis courts and golf course access.
  • Walking paths and beautiful grounds.
  • Playgrounds and recreation activities.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

Old Key West is located in the Disney Springs Resort Area. You can take a relaxing boat ride to Disney Springs, while buses run to all four theme parks and the water parks.

Why choose Disney’s Old Key West Resort?

This resort is perfect if you want space, comfort and a peaceful atmosphere. The villas are some of the largest at Walt Disney World, the theming is relaxed and welcoming, and the boat to Disney Springs is a lovely bonus. A brilliant choice for families and groups who want a home‑from‑home Disney stay.
