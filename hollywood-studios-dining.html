Hollywood Studios Dining

Big flavours, bold themes and movie‑magic meals.

Dining with a Touch of Hollywood Magic

Disney’s Hollywood Studios is packed with immersive dining — from retro comfort food to Star Wars‑themed dishes straight from Batuu. Whether you want a fun family meal, a quick bite between shows or a themed dining experience you’ll talk about for years, this park has fantastic options for every taste.

Character Dining at Hollywood Studios

⭐ Hollywood & Vine

A lively buffet with two different character experiences:

  • Breakfast: Disney Junior Play n’ Dine — perfect for little ones.
  • Lunch & Dinner: Minnie’s Seasonal Dining — Minnie and friends in rotating seasonal outfits.

Included in the Disney Dining Plan (1 Table‑Service credit).

Top Table‑Service Restaurants

🍗 50’s Prime Time Café

Comfort food served in a retro 1950s kitchen — complete with playful “family rules” from your server.

🍽️ The Hollywood Brown Derby

Signature dining inspired by the Golden Age of Hollywood. Famous for the Cobb salad and grapefruit cake.

🍔 Sci‑Fi Dine‑In Theater Restaurant

Dine in a classic car under the stars while watching vintage sci‑fi clips. One of the most unique restaurants at Disney.

🍝 Mama Melrose’s Ristorante Italiano

Cosy Italian dining with hearty pasta dishes and warm atmosphere.

Best Quick‑Service Restaurants

🍜 Docking Bay 7 Food & Cargo (Star Wars: Galaxy’s Edge)

Creative, flavourful dishes inspired by the Star Wars universe — a must‑try.

🍟 Woody’s Lunch Box (Toy Story Land)

Grilled sandwiches, tots and nostalgic comfort food. Very popular at lunchtime.

🍔 ABC Commissary

A great mix of American favourites with plenty of indoor seating.

🍗 Backlot Express

Burgers, chicken and quick bites — ideal for families on the go.

Must‑Try Snacks

  • Blue Milk & Green Milk (Galaxy’s Edge)
  • Num Num Cookie (Pixar Place)
  • Carrot Cake Cookie (Trolley Car Café)
  • Jack‑Jack’s Shortbread Cookie
  • Popcorn from Kat Saka’s Kettle

Many snacks are included in both Disney Dining Plans — look for the Dining Plan symbol on menus.

Top Dining Tips for Hollywood Studios

  • Book Table‑Service restaurants 60 days before arrival.
  • Woody’s Lunch Box gets busy — mobile order early.
  • Plan a sit‑down meal during the afternoon heat.
  • Try Sci‑Fi Dine‑In for a unique, immersive experience.
  • Ask me for personalised recommendations based on your family’s tastes.

Why I Recommend Hollywood Studios Dining

Hollywood Studios offers some of the most fun and immersive dining at Walt Disney World. From retro kitchens to sci‑fi drive‑ins and Star Wars‑themed meals, there’s something here for every family. It’s the perfect park for memorable meals and exciting flavours.
