Disney’s Animal Kingdom Lodge

A breathtaking African‑inspired resort with real savanna views.

A truly unique Disney Deluxe Resort

Disney’s Animal Kingdom Lodge is one of the most immersive and unforgettable resorts at Walt Disney World. Inspired by the beauty and culture of Africa, it offers stunning architecture, warm hospitality and the chance to see over 200 animals and birds right from the resort’s savannas.

The resort is split into two distinct areas — Jambo House and Kidani Village — each offering its own atmosphere, facilities and room types.

Who is Animal Kingdom Lodge best for?

  • Families who want a unique, immersive Disney experience.
  • Animal lovers who want to see wildlife from their balcony.
  • Couples looking for a peaceful, beautifully themed resort.
  • Guests who enjoy cultural activities, great dining and stunning views.

Jambo House

Jambo House is the main lodge — home to the iconic grand lobby, incredible dining and a mix of standard rooms and Disney Vacation Club villas. It’s lively, atmospheric and full of beautiful African art and design.

  • Standard rooms and villas available.
  • Closest to the main lobby and most dining options.
  • Home to the Uzima Springs Pool.
  • Ideal for guests who want to be in the heart of the resort.

Kidani Village

Kidani Village is the resort’s villa‑only building — quieter, more relaxed and perfect for families who want extra space. It has its own lobby, pool and dining, but guests can easily travel between both areas.

  • Studios and multi‑bedroom villas only.
  • More spacious layouts with kitchenettes or full kitchens.
  • Home to the Samawati Springs Pool.
  • Ideal for families who want more room and a calmer atmosphere.

Rooms & Savanna Views

Rooms and villas at Animal Kingdom Lodge are warm, beautifully themed and inspired by African design. Many offer savanna views — giving you the chance to see giraffes, zebras, antelope and more right from your balcony.

Whether you choose Jambo House or Kidani Village, you’ll enjoy a comfortable, immersive stay with plenty of space and stunning surroundings.

Dining

Animal Kingdom Lodge is home to some of the most celebrated restaurants at Walt Disney World:

  • Boma — African‑inspired buffet with incredible flavours.
  • Jiko — signature dining with a South African wine list.
  • Sanaa — famous for its bread service and savanna views.
  • The Mara — quick‑service meals and snacks.

Whether you want adventurous flavours or comforting favourites, the dining here is exceptional.

Facilities

  • Two beautiful pools — Uzima Springs and Samawati Springs.
  • Animal viewing areas with guides.
  • Cultural activities and storytelling.
  • Fitness centre and jogging trails.
  • Shops, lounges and recreation.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

Animal Kingdom Lodge is located in the Animal Kingdom Resort Area. Buses run to all four theme parks, Disney Springs and the water parks.

Why I recommend Disney’s Animal Kingdom Lodge

This resort is perfect if you want a truly unique Disney experience. The savanna views are unforgettable, the theming is stunning and the atmosphere is peaceful and welcoming. Whether you stay in Jambo House or Kidani Village, it’s a brilliant choice for families and couples who want something special.
