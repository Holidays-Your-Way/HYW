Lightning Lane Premier Pass

Unlimited Lightning Lane access. No planning. No stress.

What is the Lightning Lane Premier Pass?

The Lightning Lane Premier Pass is Disney’s most premium queue‑skipping option. It gives you unlimited, walk‑up Lightning Lane access to participating attractions across the parks — no return times, no advance bookings and no need to plan ahead.

It’s the closest thing Disney has ever offered to an all‑day “skip the queue” pass, making it perfect for guests who want maximum flexibility and minimal waiting.

How Premier Pass Works

  • Scan your pass at any participating Lightning Lane entrance.
  • Enjoy near‑immediate access — no return times required.
  • Use it as many times as you like throughout the day.
  • Valid for one park or multiple parks (depending on your ticket).

It’s simple, fast and incredibly convenient — especially on busy days.

What’s Included?

Premier Pass includes Lightning Lane access to most attractions that normally use Multi Pass. It does not include the top‑tier Single Pass attractions.

Who is Premier Pass Best For?

  • Families who want a stress‑free, flexible day.
  • Guests visiting during peak seasons.
  • Anyone who dislikes planning or scheduling return times.
  • Visitors who want to ride as much as possible.
  • Guests doing a short trip and want to maximise time.

Top Tips for Premier Pass

  • Arrive early to combine short standby waits with unlimited LL access.
  • Use Single Pass for the top‑tier rides not included.
  • Park Hop to maximise value — Premier Pass works everywhere.
  • Perfect for families who want to repeat favourite rides.

Why I Recommend Lightning Lane Premier Pass

Premier Pass is the ultimate way to enjoy Walt Disney World with complete freedom. No planning, no return times and no stress — just walk up and ride. It’s perfect for families, couples and anyone who wants a premium, time‑saving experience.
