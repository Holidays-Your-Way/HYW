Disney Dining Plan

Simple, flexible and great value for families.

What is the Disney Dining Plan?

The Disney Dining Plan is a pre‑paid meal package available exclusively to guests staying at Disney Resort hotels. It allows you to enjoy meals, snacks and drinks across Walt Disney World without worrying about budgeting during your holiday.

There are two dining plans to choose from — both flexible, great value and perfect for families.

How the Dining Plan Works

  • You receive a set number of meals and snacks per person, per night of your stay.
  • Use your credits whenever you like — breakfast, lunch or dinner.
  • Tap your MagicBand or phone to redeem meals.
  • Choose from over 200 dining locations across Walt Disney World.
  • Non‑alcoholic drinks included with all meals; alcoholic drinks included for guests 21+.

It’s simple, convenient and perfect for families who want predictable costs.

The Two Disney Dining Plans

🍽️ Disney Quick‑Service Dining Plan

Perfect for families who prefer fast, flexible meals.

  • 2 Quick‑Service meals per night
  • 1 Snack per night
  • Refillable resort mug
🍴 Disney Dining Plan

Ideal for families who enjoy sit‑down meals and character dining.

  • 1 Table‑Service meal per night
  • 1 Quick‑Service meal per night
  • 1 Snack per night
  • Refillable resort mug

Where You Can Use the Dining Plan

  • All four Disney theme parks
  • Disney Springs restaurants
  • Disney Resort hotel dining
  • Character dining experiences (Disney Dining Plan only)
  • Most snack carts and quick‑service locations

Look for the Dining Plan symbol on menus — it’s everywhere.

Character Dining & Special Meals

Character dining is included with the Disney Dining Plan. Some premium experiences require two Table‑Service credits, such as:

  • Cinderella’s Royal Table
  • Story Book Dining at Artist Point
  • Hoop‑Dee‑Doo Musical Revue

These meals are incredibly popular — booking early is essential.

Top Tips for the Dining Plan

  • Use Table‑Service credits for higher‑value meals.
  • Character dining is a great way to maximise value.
  • Save snacks for festival booths at EPCOT.
  • Use your refillable mug at your resort for coffee, tea and soft drinks.
  • Track your credits in the My Disney Experience app.

Why I Recommend the Disney Dining Plan

The Dining Plan is perfect for families who want convenience, flexibility and predictable costs. It takes the stress out of budgeting and lets you enjoy amazing meals across Walt Disney World without worrying about prices during your holiday. It’s a brilliant way to make your trip feel all‑inclusive.
