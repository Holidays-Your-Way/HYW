Delta Hotels Orlando Lake Buena Vista

Modern, clean and perfectly placed for Walt Disney World.

A sleek, modern hotel with great value and a brilliant Disney location

Delta Hotels Lake Buena Vista is a clean, contemporary hotel designed for guests who want comfort, convenience and great value just minutes from Walt Disney World. The atmosphere is calm and modern, the rooms are fresh and well‑designed, and the whole place feels polished without being premium‑priced.

With a lovely pool area, on‑site dining and a fantastic location close to Disney Springs, it’s a great choice for families and couples who want a stylish, reliable base for their Orlando holiday.

Who is Delta Hotels LBV best for?

  • Couples who want a modern, comfortable hotel near Disney.
  • Families who prefer clean, contemporary rooms.
  • Guests who want great value in a brilliant location.
  • Anyone who likes a sleek, uncluttered hotel style.

Rooms

Rooms at Delta Hotels are modern, bright and thoughtfully designed, with plush beds, good storage and a clean, contemporary look. Everything feels fresh and well‑maintained — ideal for guests who want a comfortable, stylish space to unwind after long park days.

They’re not huge, but they’re modern, practical and great value for the location.

Dining

The hotel offers several convenient dining options:

  • A casual on‑site restaurant for breakfast and dinner.
  • A lobby bar for drinks and small plates.
  • A grab‑and‑go market for snacks, coffee and essentials.

And with Disney Springs just minutes away, you’ve got endless restaurants and entertainment options nearby.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with plenty of seating.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site dining and grab‑and‑go options.
  • Shuttle transport to Walt Disney World.
  • Modern lobby and lounge areas.

Location

Delta Hotels LBV is perfectly located just minutes from Walt Disney World and close to Disney Springs. It’s convenient, quiet and ideal for guests who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose Delta Hotels LBV?

This hotel is perfect if you want a clean, modern, great‑value base close to Disney. The rooms feel fresh and contemporary, the facilities are excellent for the price, and the location is unbeatable for Disney Springs and the parks. A brilliant choice for couples and families who want style without the premium price tag.
