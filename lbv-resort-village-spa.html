Lake Buena Vista Resort Village & Spa

Spacious suites, a pirate‑themed pool and fantastic value for families.

A family‑friendly all‑suite resort with room to relax

Lake Buena Vista Resort Village & Spa is one of the best value family resorts near Disney — spacious suites, great facilities and a relaxed, resort‑style atmosphere. The famous pirate ship pool is a huge hit with children, and the suites give families the space and flexibility they need for longer stays.

The resort feels safe, welcoming and self‑contained, with a spa, a large pool area, a convenience store and easy access to the nearby outlet mall. It’s a brilliant choice if you want comfort, convenience and great value just minutes from Walt Disney World.

Who is LBV Resort Village & Spa best for?

  • Families who want large suites with full kitchens.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who want space to spread out.
  • Travellers who love a resort feel without premium prices.
  • Anyone who wants to be close to Disney without staying on‑site.

Suites

Suites are the highlight here — spacious, comfortable and ideal for families. Expect:

  • 1‑, 2‑, 3‑ and 4‑bedroom suites.
  • Full kitchens with fridge, oven, microwave and dishwasher.
  • Separate living and dining areas.
  • Washer/dryer in most suites.
  • Balconies with resort or pool views.

Everything feels homely, practical and perfect for longer stays.

Dining

The resort offers several easy dining options:

  • A casual on‑site pub and grill.
  • A poolside bar for snacks and drinks.
  • A convenience store for essentials.
  • Full kitchens in every suite for flexible meals.

And with the outlet mall next door, you’ve got even more dining choices within a short walk.

Facilities

  • Pirate‑themed pool with slides and splash areas.
  • Quiet pool for relaxing.
  • Full‑service spa.
  • Fitness centre.
  • On‑site dining and convenience store.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Washer/dryer in most suites.

Location

The resort is just minutes from Walt Disney World, close to the Lake Buena Vista area and right next to a large outlet mall. It’s quiet, convenient and perfectly placed for families who want easy access to the parks without staying on‑site.

Why choose LBV Resort Village & Spa?

This resort is perfect if you want space, comfort and great value near Disney. The suites are fantastic for families, the pirate ship pool is a huge hit with children, and the whole place feels relaxed and welcoming. A brilliant choice for longer stays or anyone who wants a homely base with resort‑style facilities.
