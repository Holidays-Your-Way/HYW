Floridays Resort Orlando

Spacious suites, a relaxed resort feel and fantastic family value.

A warm, welcoming all‑suite resort perfect for families

Floridays Resort is one of the most loved family resorts in Orlando — spacious suites, a relaxed atmosphere and consistently excellent service. It’s the kind of place families return to year after year because it feels homely, comfortable and incredibly easy to settle into.

With two lovely pools, a full kitchen in every suite and a brilliant location on International Drive, it’s ideal for longer stays or anyone who wants space, comfort and great value close to the parks.

Who is Floridays best for?

  • Families who want large, comfortable suites with full kitchens.
  • Guests staying 10–14 nights who want a homely base.
  • Travellers who prefer a relaxed, resort‑style atmosphere.
  • Anyone who wants great value close to Disney and I‑Drive.

Suites

Suites at Floridays are bright, spacious and designed for real family living. Expect:

  • 2‑ and 3‑bedroom suites.
  • Full kitchens with fridge, oven, microwave and dishwasher.
  • Separate living and dining areas.
  • Washer/dryer in every suite.
  • Balconies overlooking the resort or pool.

Everything feels warm, comfortable and perfect for longer stays.

Dining

Floridays offers flexible dining options ideal for families:

  • A poolside bar and grill for casual meals.
  • A marketplace café for snacks, coffee and essentials.
  • Full kitchens in every suite for easy breakfasts and dinners.

And with International Drive just minutes away, you’ve got endless restaurants nearby.

Facilities

  • Large main pool with fountains and family areas.
  • Quiet pool for relaxing.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Poolside bar and grill.
  • Marketplace café.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Washer/dryer in every suite.

Location

Floridays is perfectly located on International Drive, close to restaurants, shops and attractions. Disney is a short drive away, and you’ve got easy access to SeaWorld, Universal and the Lake Buena Vista area.

Why choose Floridays Resort?

Floridays is perfect if you want space, comfort and a warm, welcoming atmosphere in a brilliant location. The suites are fantastic for families, the pools are lovely, and the whole resort feels relaxed and homely. A superb choice for longer stays or anyone who wants a comfortable base close to Disney.
