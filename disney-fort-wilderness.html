Disney’s Fort Wilderness Resort & Campground

A peaceful, rustic Disney escape with cabins and campsites.

A unique Disney resort surrounded by nature

Disney’s Fort Wilderness Resort & Campground is unlike any other Disney hotel. Set among 750 acres of pine and cypress forest, it offers a peaceful, rustic escape with charming cabins, spacious campsites and plenty of outdoor activities. It’s perfect for families who want a slower pace while still enjoying all the perks of staying on‑site.

With horseback riding, archery, campfires and boat access to Magic Kingdom, it’s a brilliant blend of nature and Disney magic.

Who is Fort Wilderness best for?

  • Families who love the outdoors and want a peaceful Disney stay.
  • Guests who want the space and comfort of a cabin.
  • Travellers with RVs or who enjoy camping.
  • Anyone who wants a unique Disney experience with nature and wildlife.

Cabins

The Fort Wilderness Cabins are perfect for families who want extra space and privacy. Each cabin includes:

  • A separate bedroom.
  • Living area with a pull‑down bed.
  • Full kitchen.
  • Private deck with a picnic table.
  • Parking right outside your cabin.

They’re a fantastic option for families who want home‑style comfort while staying close to the magic.

Campsites

Fort Wilderness offers a range of campsites for tents, pop‑ups and full RVs. Each site includes:

  • Water, electricity and picnic table.
  • Charcoal grill.
  • Access to comfort stations with showers and laundry.

It’s one of the most popular RV destinations in the USA — and for good reason.

Dining

Fort Wilderness has some of the most beloved dining options at Walt Disney World:

  • Trail’s End — hearty Southern‑style comfort food.
  • P&J’s Southern Takeout — great for quick meals.
  • Crockett’s Tavern — relaxed drinks and snacks.
  • Chip ‘n’ Dale’s Campfire Sing‑Along — a family favourite.

You can also take a boat to Magic Kingdom for even more dining choices.

Facilities

  • Two pools, including the Meadow Swimmin’ Pool with a waterslide.
  • Horseback riding and pony rides.
  • Archery and canoeing.
  • Fishing and nature trails.
  • Campfire sing‑along with Chip ‘n’ Dale.
  • Boat transportation to Magic Kingdom.
  • Golf cart rentals (very popular here).
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

Fort Wilderness is located in the Magic Kingdom Resort Area. You can travel to Magic Kingdom by boat, while buses run to EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom and Disney Springs.

Why choose Disney’s Fort Wilderness Resort & Campground?

This resort is perfect if you want a peaceful, nature‑filled Disney stay with plenty of space and charm. The cabins are fantastic for families, the activities are brilliant for kids, and the boat to Magic Kingdom is a lovely bonus. A wonderful choice for families who want something different from the typical hotel experience.
