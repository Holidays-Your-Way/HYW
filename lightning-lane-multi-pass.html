Lightning Lane Multi Pass

Plan ahead. Skip the queues. Enjoy more magic.

What is the Lightning Lane Multi Pass?

The Lightning Lane Multi Pass is Disney’s new, simplified way to skip the queues at many attractions across all four theme parks. It replaces the old Genie+ system and makes planning much easier.

With Multi Pass, you can choose your attractions before your holiday, select your return times in advance, and enjoy a smoother, more relaxed day in the parks.

How Multi Pass Works

  • You choose 3 attractions per park in advance.
  • You select your return times before you arrive.
  • After using your 3 selections, you can book more throughout the day.
  • Works with Park Hopper tickets.
  • Pricing varies by date and park.

It’s flexible, easy to use and far more predictable than the old Genie+ system.

Attraction Tiers

Each park has two tiers: Tier 1 and Tier 2. You may choose one Tier 1 attraction and two Tier 2 attractions when making your initial selections.

Magic Kingdom
  • Tier 1: Seven Dwarfs Mine Train, Peter Pan’s Flight, Jungle Cruise
  • Tier 2: Space Mountain, Pirates of the Caribbean, Haunted Mansion, Buzz Lightyear and more
EPCOT
  • Tier 1: Remy’s Ratatouille Adventure, Frozen Ever After, Soarin’
  • Tier 2: Spaceship Earth, Living with the Land, Nemo, Mission: SPACE and more
Hollywood Studios
  • Tier 1: Slinky Dog Dash, Mickey & Minnie’s Runaway Railway
  • Tier 2: Toy Story Mania, Tower of Terror, Star Tours and more
Animal Kingdom
  • Tier 1: Kilimanjaro Safaris, Na’vi River Journey
  • Tier 2: Expedition Everest, Kali River Rapids and more

When Can You Book?

  • Disney Resort Guests: 7 days before arrival (for up to 14 days)
  • Off‑site Guests: 3 days before each park day

This gives Disney Resort guests a huge advantage — especially for popular attractions.

Best Uses for Multi Pass

  • Families who want a structured, stress‑free day.
  • Guests who like planning ahead.
  • Anyone visiting during busy seasons.
  • Guests who want to avoid long queues without buying Premier Pass.

Top Tips for Multi Pass

  • Book your Tier 1 attraction first — they fill up quickly.
  • Choose morning return times so you can book more later.
  • Use Park Hopper to maximise value.
  • Combine Multi Pass with Single Pass for the biggest rides.

Why I Recommend Lightning Lane Multi Pass

Multi Pass is the easiest, most predictable way to skip queues at Walt Disney World. It lets you plan ahead, choose your return times and enjoy more attractions with less waiting. It’s ideal for families and anyone who wants a smoother, more relaxed park day.
