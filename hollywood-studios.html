Disney’s Hollywood Studios

Step into the movies with thrills, stories and cinematic magic.

A park full of action, adventure and storytelling

Hollywood Studios is where Disney brings blockbuster films to life. From Star Wars to Toy Story to classic Hollywood glamour, this park is packed with immersive lands, thrilling attractions and incredible shows.

It’s a must‑visit for families, thrill‑seekers and anyone who loves stepping straight into their favourite films.

Who is Hollywood Studios best for?

  • Thrill‑seekers who love big rides.
  • Star Wars fans — Galaxy’s Edge is unmissable.
  • Families with older children and teens.
  • Guests who love immersive lands and cinematic storytelling.

The Lands of Hollywood Studios

🌌 Star Wars: Galaxy’s Edge

Explore Batuu, fly the Millennium Falcon and face the First Order.

🤠 Toy Story Land

Shrink to the size of a toy and enjoy colourful, family‑friendly fun.

🎬 Hollywood Boulevard

Classic Hollywood glamour and the gateway to the park.

🎭 Echo Lake

Home to Frozen fun, Indiana Jones and classic attractions.

🎸 Sunset Boulevard

Thrills, shows and iconic Disney entertainment.

🎥 Animation Courtyard

Disney Junior favourites and behind‑the‑scenes magic.

Dining at Hollywood Studios

Hollywood Studios has a great mix of themed dining, quick bites and immersive restaurants:

  • Oga’s Cantina — Star Wars‑themed drinks and atmosphere.
  • Woody’s Lunch Box — comfort food with a Toy Story twist.
  • Hollywood Brown Derby — signature dining.
  • 50’s Prime Time Café — homestyle meals with a fun retro theme.
  • ABC Commissary — modern quick‑service meals.
  • Docking Bay 7 Food & Cargo — creative Star Wars‑inspired dishes.

Entertainment & Shows

Hollywood Studios is home to some of the best live entertainment at Walt Disney World:

  • Fantasmic! — the iconic nighttime spectacular.
  • Beauty and the Beast Live on Stage.
  • Frozen Sing‑Along Celebration.
  • Indiana Jones Epic Stunt Spectacular.
  • Disney Junior Dance Party.

Top Tips for Visiting Hollywood Studios

  • Arrive early — Rise of the Resistance queues build quickly.
  • Use Genie+ for Slinky Dog Dash and Runaway Railway.
  • Mobile order meals to save time.
  • Catch Fantasmic! — it’s a must‑see.
  • Plan breaks — this park gets hot and busy.

Why I Recommend Hollywood Studios

Hollywood Studios is perfect for guests who love thrills, immersive lands and cinematic storytelling. From Star Wars to Toy Story to classic Disney shows, it offers unforgettable experiences for families, teens and adults. It’s exciting, atmospheric and full of must‑do attractions.
