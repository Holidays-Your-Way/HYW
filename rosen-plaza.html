Rosen Plaza Hotel

Stylish, comfortable and perfectly placed on International Drive.

A polished, modern hotel with a calm, grown‑up atmosphere

Rosen Plaza is one of the more refined hotels on International Drive — modern, comfortable and beautifully maintained. It has a calm, professional feel that works brilliantly for couples, families with older children and anyone who prefers a quieter, more elegant base for their Orlando holiday.

The hotel has a lovely pool area, several dining options, a fitness centre and direct access to the Orange County Convention Center via the skybridge. And with Pointe Orlando right next door, you’ve got restaurants, bars and entertainment within a few minutes’ walk.

Who is Rosen Plaza best for?

  • Couples who want a stylish, comfortable hotel in a great location.
  • Families with older children who prefer a quieter atmosphere.
  • Guests attending events at the Convention Center.
  • Anyone who wants a polished hotel without luxury prices.

Rooms

Rooms at Rosen Plaza are modern, clean and comfortable, with plush beds, spacious bathrooms and a warm, contemporary design. They’re a noticeable step up from the budget hotels on I‑Drive, offering a more refined feel without jumping into premium pricing.

Suites are available for guests who want extra space or a more luxurious stay.

Dining

Rosen Plaza has several dining options, including a steakhouse, a casual café, a lobby bar and a 24‑hour deli. Whether you want a relaxed breakfast, a quick bite or a sit‑down dinner, you’ve got plenty of choice on‑site — and even more at Pointe Orlando next door.

Facilities

  • Outdoor pool with a spacious sun deck.
  • Fitness centre.
  • Multiple dining options, including a steakhouse and 24‑hour deli.
  • Direct skybridge access to the Convention Center.
  • Shuttle transport to major theme parks.
  • Modern lobby and public spaces.

Location

Rosen Plaza is in a fantastic spot on International Drive — right next to Pointe Orlando and close to ICON Park, restaurants, shops and attractions. Universal is a short drive or shuttle ride away, making it a brilliant base for guests who want convenience with a more polished hotel experience.

Why choose Rosen Plaza?

Rosen Plaza is perfect if you want a stylish, comfortable hotel with a calm, grown‑up feel in a brilliant location. It’s modern, well‑maintained and offers great value for the quality you get. A fantastic choice for couples and families who want something a little more refined on International Drive.
