Lightning Lane Single Pass

Skip the queue for Disney’s biggest, most popular attractions.

What is the Lightning Lane Single Pass?

The Lightning Lane Single Pass lets you purchase one‑off access to Disney’s most in‑demand attractions. These rides are not included in the Multi Pass, so if you want to skip the queue, this is the way to do it.

You choose your attraction, select a return time and enjoy a much shorter wait — perfect for the rides that regularly reach 90–180 minute queues.

How Single Pass Works

  • Choose a participating attraction.
  • Select your preferred return time.
  • Purchase Lightning Lane access for that specific ride.
  • Use the Lightning Lane entrance during your time window.

You can buy more than one Single Pass per day, and you can combine them with Multi Pass for maximum flexibility.

Which Attractions Use Single Pass?

These are the top‑tier attractions that require a Single Pass or standby queue:

Magic Kingdom
  • TRON Lightcycle / Run
EPCOT
  • Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind
Hollywood Studios
  • Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance
Animal Kingdom
  • Avatar Flight of Passage

These attractions are extremely popular, so Single Pass is often the easiest way to experience them without a long wait.

When Can You Book?

  • Disney Resort Guests: 7 days before arrival (for up to 14 days)
  • Off‑site Guests: 3 days before each park day

Return times for the most popular attractions can sell out quickly, so booking early is a big advantage.

Who is Single Pass Best For?

  • Guests who want to guarantee access to the biggest rides.
  • Families who don’t want to wait in long queues.
  • Visitors with limited time in the parks.
  • Anyone who wants flexibility without buying Premier Pass.

Top Tips for Single Pass

  • Book early — especially for Rise of the Resistance and Flight of Passage.
  • Choose morning return times to free up your day.
  • Combine with Multi Pass for the most efficient park day.
  • Prices vary — expect higher costs on weekends and holidays.

Why I Recommend Lightning Lane Single Pass

Single Pass is the easiest way to experience Disney’s headline attractions without long waits. It’s flexible, simple and perfect for families who want to make the most of their time in the parks. Whether you’re riding TRON, soaring through Pandora or joining the Resistance, Single Pass makes it stress‑free.
