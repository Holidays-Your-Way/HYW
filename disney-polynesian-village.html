Disney’s Polynesian Village Resort

A tropical, luxurious escape on the Magic Kingdom monorail.

A stunning, island‑inspired Disney Deluxe Resort

Disney’s Polynesian Village Resort is one of the most iconic and beloved hotels at Walt Disney World. With its lush tropical landscaping, white‑sand beaches, tiki torches and warm island theming, it feels like a relaxing getaway — all while being just minutes from Magic Kingdom.

With monorail access, incredible dining and beautiful views across Seven Seas Lagoon, it’s a dream resort for families and couples alike.

Who is the Polynesian best for?

  • Families who want a luxurious, tropical Disney stay.
  • Couples looking for a romantic, premium resort.
  • Guests who want monorail access to Magic Kingdom and EPCOT.
  • Anyone who loves island theming and beautiful views.

Rooms & Themed Suites

Rooms at the Polynesian are spacious, beautifully themed and inspired by the islands of the South Pacific. Expect plush beds, large TVs, coffee makers, mini‑fridges and plenty of storage.

Many rooms offer lagoon views, and some even look directly across to Magic Kingdom — perfect for watching the fireworks from your balcony.

Dining

The Polynesian is home to some of the most popular dining options at Walt Disney World:

  • ‘Ohana — famous for its character breakfast and family‑style dinner.
  • Kona Café — known for Tonga Toast and fresh flavours.
  • Trader Sam’s Grog Grotto — an unforgettable themed tiki bar.
  • Capt. Cook’s — quick‑service meals and snacks.
  • Pineapple Lanai — home of the iconic Dole Whip.

Whether you want a relaxed breakfast or a fun evening cocktail, the Polynesian has it all.

Facilities

  • Lava Pool with waterslide and volcano theming.
  • Oasis Pool for a quieter experience.
  • White‑sand beaches with views of Magic Kingdom.
  • Monorail access to Magic Kingdom and EPCOT.
  • Boat transportation across Seven Seas Lagoon.
  • Jogging trails, shops and lounges.
  • Free Disney transportation.

Location

The Polynesian is located in the Magic Kingdom Resort Area. You can take the monorail to Magic Kingdom and EPCOT, or enjoy a relaxing boat ride across the lagoon.

Why choose Disney’s Polynesian Village Resort?

This resort is perfect if you want a luxurious, tropical Disney stay with unbeatable convenience. The theming is beautiful, the dining is fantastic and the monorail access makes getting around incredibly easy. A brilliant choice for families and couples who want something truly special.
